### [1] [Modeling the mutational dynamics of very short tandem repeats](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25628)
*Amos Onn,Tzipy Marx,Liming Tao,Tamir Biezuner,Ehud Shapiro,Christoph A. Klein,Peter F. Stadler*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 短串联重复序列（STRs）可作为快速分子钟用于单细胞谱系重建，其突变动力学在不同细胞系间存在差异


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 短串联重复序列（STRs）具有极高的突变不稳定性，特别是在1-2bp重复单元的情况下，几个细胞分裂周期内就可能发生突变。这种快速积累的变异使其成为回顾性单细胞谱系重建的有趣系统发育标记。

Method: 首先在单个重复单元类型水平上建模STRs的突变动力学，然后聚合多个STR位点的长度变异，旨在获得一个非常快速的"分子钟"。基于已知谱系结构的培养细胞数据集对模型进行校准。

Result: STRs的突变动力学在给定细胞系内具有合理的稳定性，但在不同细胞系之间存在差异。这表明动力学不完全由看护基因突变解释，其他因素如组织来源和分化状态可能也起作用。

Conclusion: STRs可作为快速分子钟用于单细胞谱系重建，但需要进一步的数据和研究来评估各种因素（包括组织来源和分化状态）对突变动力学的相对影响。

Abstract: Short tandem repeats (STRs) are low-entropy regions in the genome, consisting of a short (1-6 bp) unit that is consecutively repeated multiple times. They are known for high mutational instability, due to so-called stutter-mutations, in which the number of units in the run increases or descreases. In particular, STRs with repeat unit length of 1-2 bp are prone to mutate even within several cell divisions. The extremely rapid accumulation of variation makes them interesting phylogenetic markers for retrospective single-cell lineage reconstruction. Here we model their mutational dynamics at the level of individual repeat unit type and then aggregate length variations over many STR loci with the aim of obtaining a very fast ``molecular clock''. We calibrate our model based on several datasets with known lineage structure prepared from cultured cells. We find that the mutational dynamics of STRs are reasonably consistent for a given cell line, but vary among different ones. This suggests that the dynamics are not entirely explained by mutations in caretaker genes, rather, various other factors play a role -- possibly tissue origin and differentiation state. Further data and research is necessary to asses their relative effects.

### [2] [The Self-Replication Phase Diagram: Mapping Where Life Becomes Possible in Cellular Automata Rule Space](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25239)
*Don Yin*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 该研究系统分析了262,144个Moore邻域外总细胞自动机规则，发现7.69%支持模式增殖，主要分布在低规则密度和中等背景稳定性区域，背景稳定性和近似质量守恒是自复制相边界的主要决定因素。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究旨在识别支持生命自复制的基本基质特征，通过系统分析细胞自动机规则空间，探索自复制现象出现的条件参数。

Method: 采用外总二进制细胞自动机规则，在Moore邻域下对262,144个规则进行穷举分类，在(λ, F)参数平面生成相图，使用三层检测层次（模式增殖、扩展长度确认、因果扰动）识别自复制规则。

Result: 发现20,152个规则（7.69%）支持模式增殖，集中在低规则密度（λ≈0.15-0.25）和中等背景稳定性（F≈0.2-0.3）区域；自复制规则具有更好的近似质量守恒性（质量平衡0.21 vs 0.34）；邻域大小增加时自复制率单调上升。

Conclusion: 背景稳定性和近似质量守恒是自复制相边界的主要决定因素，这些特征为理解生命自复制的基本基质条件提供了定量框架。

Abstract: What substrate features allow life? We exhaustively classify all 262,144 outer-totalistic binary cellular automata rules with Moore neighbourhood for self-replication and produce phase diagrams in the $(λ, F)$ plane, where $λ$ is Langton's rule density and $F$ is a background-stability parameter. Of these rules, 20,152 (7.69%) support pattern proliferation, concentrated at low rule density ($λ\approx 0.15$--$0.25$) and low-to-moderate background stability ($F \approx 0.2$--$0.3$), in the weakly supercritical regime (Derrida coefficient $μ= 1.81$ for replicators vs. $1.39$ for non-replicators). Self-replicating rules are more approximately mass-conserving (mass-balance 0.21 vs. 0.34), and this generalises to $k{=}3$ Moore rules. A three-tier detection hierarchy (pattern proliferation, extended-length confirmation, and causal perturbation) yields an estimated 1.56% causal self-replication rate. Self-replication rate increases monotonically with neighbourhood size under equalised detection: von Neumann 4.79%, Moore 7.69%, extended Moore 16.69%. These results identify background stability and approximate mass conservation as the primary axes of the self-replication phase boundary.

### [3] [Fast Iteration of Spaced k-mers](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25417)
*Lucas Czech*

Main category: q-bio.GN

TL;DR: 提出基于CPU位操作指令的高效提取间隔k-mer方法，比现有方法快一个数量级，吞吐量达每秒每核750MB序列数据


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有方法在提取间隔k-mer时效率低下，限制了其在高性能生物信息学应用中的使用，需要更高效的实现方法

Method: 基于CPU级别的位操作指令开发高效算法，识别并避免k-mer处理中的常见陷阱，实现C++20的优化实现

Result: 方法比现有方法快一个数量级，实现更简单，吞吐量达到每秒每核750MB序列数据，可在高性能应用中无显著性能损失地使用间隔k-mer

Conclusion: 提出的高效间隔k-mer提取方法使间隔k-mer能够应用于高性能生物信息学应用，代码以MIT许可证开源提供

Abstract: We present efficient approaches for extracting spaced k-mers from nucleotide sequences. They are based on bit manipulation instructions at CPU level, making them both simpler to implement and up to an order of magnitude faster than existing methods. We further evaluate common pitfalls in k-mer processing, which can cause major inefficiencies. Combined, our approaches allow the utilization of spaced k-mers in high-performance bioinformatics applications without major performance degradation, offering a throughput of up to 750MB of sequence data per second per core.
  Availability: The implementation in C++20 is published under the MIT license, and freely available at https://github.com/lczech/fisk

### [4] [Lingshu-Cell: A generative cellular world model for transcriptome modeling toward virtual cells](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25240)
*Han Zhang,Guo-Hua Yuan,Chaohao Yuan,Tingyang Xu,Tian Bian,Hong Cheng,Wenbing Huang,Deli Zhao,Yu Rong*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: Lingshu-Cell是一个基于掩码离散扩散模型的单细胞转录组学基础模型，能够学习细胞状态分布并进行扰动条件下的生成模拟，在虚拟细胞挑战中表现优异。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的单细胞转录组学基础模型主要提供静态表征，无法显式建模细胞状态分布以进行生成模拟。需要开发能够学习转录组状态分布并支持扰动条件下条件模拟的模型。

Method: 提出Lingshu-Cell，一个掩码离散扩散模型，直接在离散标记空间中操作，兼容单细胞转录组数据的稀疏、非序列特性。该模型能够捕获约18,000个基因的复杂转录组表达依赖关系，无需依赖先验基因选择。通过联合嵌入细胞类型或供体身份与扰动信息，预测新组合下的全转录组表达变化。

Result: Lingshu-Cell在不同组织和物种中准确再现转录组分布、标记基因表达模式和细胞亚型比例，展示了捕获复杂细胞异质性的能力。在Virtual Cell Challenge H1遗传扰动基准测试中取得领先性能，并在预测人类PBMCs中细胞因子诱导反应方面表现优异。

Conclusion: Lingshu-Cell建立了一个灵活的细胞世界模型，用于细胞状态和扰动响应的计算机模拟，为生物发现和扰动筛选的新范式奠定了基础。

Abstract: Modeling cellular states and predicting their responses to perturbations are central challenges in computational biology and the development of virtual cells. Existing foundation models for single-cell transcriptomics provide powerful static representations, but they do not explicitly model the distribution of cellular states for generative simulation. Here, we introduce Lingshu-Cell, a masked discrete diffusion model that learns transcriptomic state distributions and supports conditional simulation under perturbation. By operating directly in a discrete token space that is compatible with the sparse, non-sequential nature of single-cell transcriptomic data, Lingshu-Cell captures complex transcriptome-wide expression dependencies across approximately 18,000 genes without relying on prior gene selection, such as filtering by high variability or ranking by expression level. Across diverse tissues and species, Lingshu-Cell accurately reproduces transcriptomic distributions, marker-gene expression patterns and cell-subtype proportions, demonstrating its ability to capture complex cellular heterogeneity. Moreover, by jointly embedding cell type or donor identity with perturbation, Lingshu-Cell can predict whole-transcriptome expression changes for novel combinations of identity and perturbation. It achieves leading performance on the Virtual Cell Challenge H1 genetic perturbation benchmark and in predicting cytokine-induced responses in human PBMCs. Together, these results establish Lingshu-Cell as a flexible cellular world model for in silico simulation of cell states and perturbation responses, laying the foundation for a new paradigm in biological discovery and perturbation screening.
