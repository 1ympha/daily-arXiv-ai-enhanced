### [1] [Emergent spatial organization of competing species under environmental stress and cooperation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.22177)
*Ton Viet Ta*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 该研究开发了一个空间显式的反应-扩散框架，研究在气候变异、污染、资源异质性和合作等多重压力下竞争物种的共存机制，并引入Swin Transformer进行参数反演和预测。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解物种如何在相互作用的压力因子下持续存在是生态学的核心挑战。随着全球变化的加速，需要建立能够连接稀疏观测与机制动态的统一框架，以推进生物多样性预测。

Method: 1. 建立空间显式反应-扩散框架，模拟竞争物种在气候变异（低频温度振荡）、污染扩散、资源异质性和合作影响下的动态；2. 使用边界几何、分形维数和空间熵进行定量分析；3. 开发基于Swin Transformer的混合逆建模框架，从仅两个时间快照推断高维参数。

Result: 1. 数值模拟揭示了持续空间组织的自发涌现，包括优势分离和稳定竞争边界；2. 从混合初始状态向低复杂度准稳态配置的转变；3. 共存通过不同策略实现：一个物种占据更大面积，另一个维持更高局部密度；4. 合作增强恢复力但在污染区域崩溃，形成异质性"社会缓冲"；5. Swin Transformer模型能准确恢复人口统计、扩散和环境敏感性参数，短期空间预测可靠但长期预测因非线性系统敏感性而发散。

Conclusion: 该统一框架将稀疏观测与机制动态联系起来，推进了在加速全球变化下的生物多样性预测，为理解多重压力下的物种共存提供了新的理论工具和预测方法。

Abstract: Understanding how species persist under interacting stressors is a central challenge in ecology. We develop a spatially explicit reaction-diffusion framework to investigate competing species in landscapes shaped by climate variability, pollution, resource heterogeneity, and cooperation. Here, temperature follows low-frequency oscillations, while pollution and resources diffuse from localized sources. Growth is governed by a dynamic carrying capacity integrating abiotic stress with an endogenous, pollution-sensitive cooperation field.
  Numerical simulations reveal the spontaneous emergence of persistent spatial organization, including dominance segregation and stable competitive boundaries. Quantitative analyses-using boundary geometry, fractal dimension, and spatial entropy-demonstrate a transition from intermixed initial states to low-complexity, quasi-stationary configurations. Coexistence occurs through distinct strategies: one species occupies more area, while the other maintains higher local densities. Cooperation enhances resilience but collapses in polluted zones, creating heterogeneous "social buffering."
  We further introduce a hybrid inverse modeling framework using a Swin Transformer to infer high-dimensional parameters from only two temporal snapshots. Trained on synthetic data, the model accurately recovers demographic, diffusive, and environmental-sensitivity parameters. While it achieves reliable short-term spatial predictions, long-term forecasts diverge due to the intrinsic sensitivity of nonlinear systems. This unified framework links sparse observations to mechanistic dynamics, advancing biodiversity forecasting under accelerating global change.

### [2] [Structural properties of distance-bounded phylogenetic reconciliation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.22193)
*Cyriac Antony,Alessio Martino,Blerina Sinaimeri*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文研究宿主-共生体共进化中的系统发育协调问题，特别关注宿主切换距离限制为d=3和d=4时的计算复杂性。虽然d=2时已知为多项式时间可解，但更大d值的复杂性仍未解决。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究宿主-共生体共进化中的系统发育协调问题，当宿主切换受到固定最大距离d限制时，寻找时间可行的最优协调在计算上很困难。虽然d=2的情况已知为多项式时间可解，但更大d值（如d=3和d=4）的复杂性仍然开放，需要进一步研究。

Method: 研究宿主切换距离限制为d=3和d=4的情况。虽然可能出现任意大的循环，但研究表明只要协调满足更强的无环性（时间可行性），只需检查有限大小的循环（作者提供了完整的列表）。

Result: 研究显示，对于d=3和d=4的情况，虽然可能产生任意大的循环，但在满足更强无环性条件的情况下，只需检查有限大小的循环即可确定时间可行性。作者提供了需要检查的循环的完整列表。

Conclusion: 这些结果虽然未能解决一般情况下的计算复杂性，但揭示了距离限制协调的结构特性，推进了对距离限制系统发育协调问题的理解，为进一步研究提供了理论基础。

Abstract: Phylogenetic reconciliation seeks to explain host-symbiont co-evolution by mapping parasite trees onto host trees through events such as cospeciation, duplication, host switching, and loss. Finding an optimal reconciliation that ensures time feasibility is computationally hard when timing information is incomplete, and the complexity remains open when host switches are restricted by a fixed maximum distance $d$. While the case $d=2$ is known to be polynomial, larger values are unresolved. In this paper, we study the cases $d=3$ and $d=4$. We show that although arbitrarily large cycles may occur, it suffices to check only bounded-size cycles (we provide a complete list), provided the reconciliation satisfies acyclicity (i.e., time-feasibility) in a stronger sense. These results do not resolve the general complexity, but highlight structural properties that advance the understanding of distance-bounded reconciliations.

### [3] [How adaptation to food resources and death rates shape oscillatory dynamics in a microbial population](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.22967)
*Benedetta Ciarmoli,Sophie Marbach*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 该研究提出了一个最小化的消费者-资源模型，揭示了微生物种群振荡动力学出现的物理机制：当微生物适应食物供应变化的时间尺度超过死亡时间尺度时，系统会在稳态附近出现衰减振荡。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统Lotka-Volterra模型忽略了食物源动力学，而消费者-资源模型对此考虑较少。特别是，在单种群水平上控制振荡动力学的物理机制尚不清楚，这种现象只能通过消费者-资源模型来捕捉。

Method: 提出了一个最小化的消费者-资源模型，描述单个微生物种群在连续补充底物条件下的生长和死亡动力学。通过分析时间尺度的相互作用来理解振荡动力学的出现机制。

Result: 发现衰减振荡仅当微生物适应食物供应变化的时间尺度超过死亡时间尺度时才会出现。微生物残骸回收或多食物源互补使用会减少振荡的参数范围并增加振荡衰减率；而需要多个同时存在的食物源则有相反效果。

Conclusion: 促进生长会减少固定点附近振荡的可能性。这种阻尼振荡行为在噪声环境中与持续振荡行为相关。该工作有望推动消费者-资源模型在食物源时空变化环境中的进一步研究。

Abstract: Microbes constantly interact with their environment by depleting and transforming food sources. Theoretical studies have mainly focused on Lotka-Volterra models, which do not account for food source dynamics. In contrast, consumer-resource models, which consider food source dynamics, are less explored. In particular, it is still unclear what physical mechanisms control oscillatory dynamics at a single population level, a phenomenon which can only be captured by a consumer-resource model. Here, we present a minimalistic consumer-resource model of a single microbial population with growth and death dynamics, consuming a continuously replenishing substrate. Our model reveals that decaying oscillations can occur around steady state if and only if the timescale of microbial adaptation to food supply changes exceeds the death timescale. This interplay of timescales allows us to rationalize the emergence of oscillatory dynamics when adding various biophysical ingredients to the model. We find that microbial necromass recycling or complementary use of multiple food sources reduces the parameter range for oscillations and increases the decay rate of oscillations. Requiring multiple simultaneous food sources has the opposite effect. Essentially, facilitating growth reduces the likelihood of oscillations around a fixed point. We further demonstrate that such damped oscillatory behavior is correlated with persistent oscillatory behavior in a noisy environment. We hope our work will motivate further investigations of consumer-resource models to improve descriptions of environments where food source distributions vary in space and time.
