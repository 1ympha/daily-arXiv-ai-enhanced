### [1] [The Black Death Anomaly: A Non-Abelian Field Theory of Epidemiological Safe Zones](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.08874)
*Jose de Jesus Bernal-Alvarado,David Delepine*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 该论文将黑死病传播的传统反应-扩散模型扩展为非阿贝尔规范理论，通过引入SU(N)环境规范场将地理位移与表型突变耦合，解释了历史安全区的形成并非统计异常而是拓扑空洞的必然结果。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统反应-扩散模型无法解释14世纪黑死病期间鼠疫耶尔森菌的快速遗传辐射以及中欧等广阔未受影响地理安全区的异常出现，需要新的理论框架来解析这些历史异常。

Method: 使用Doi-Peliti形式主义将多菌株流行病的随机主方程映射为协变经典场论，引入SU(N)环境规范场A_μ，将地理位移与表型突变主动耦合，将进化漂移视为空间输运现象。通过线性稳定性分析和大N('t Hooft)连续极限扩展病原体多重态。

Conclusion: 通过将宏观病原体动力学嵌入非阿贝尔规范理论，成功解释了黑死病传播的历史异常，表明安全区不是统计异常或完美隔离的结果，而是拓扑空洞的必然产物，为流行病传播与进化动力学提供了新的理论框架。

Abstract: Classical reaction-diffusion models of the 14th-century Black Death fail to explain the rapid genetic radiation of \textit{Yersinia pestis} and the anomalous emergence of vast, untouched geographic safe zones, such as Central Europe. In this work, we resolve these historical anomalies by embedding macroscopic pathogen dynamics within a non-Abelian gauge theory. Utilizing the Doi-Peliti formalism, we map the stochastic master equation of a multi-strain epidemic into a covariant classical field theory. We introduce an $SU(N)$ environmental gauge field, $\mathbf{A}_μ$, which actively couples geographic displacement to phenotypic mutation, treating evolutionary drift as a spatial transport phenomenon. We demonstrate via linear stability analysis that this covariant advection drives a Differential Flow (Turing-Hopf) instability, spontaneously breaking spatial symmetry to generate traveling waves of mutation. Furthermore, by extending the pathogen multiplet to the large-$N$ ('t Hooft) continuum limit, we prove that historical safe zones are not statistical outliers nor the result of perfect quarantine, but are mathematically necessary topological voids. In this continuous limit, the destructive interference of the mutating wavefronts analytically resolves into a stable, isotropic macroscopic node governed by a zeroth-order Bessel function ($J_0$), precisely mapping onto the historical survival of Poland and Bohemia.

### [2] [Misspecification of the generation time distribution and its impact on Rt estimates in structured populations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09451)
*Ioana Bouros,Robin Thompson,David Gavaghan,Ben Lamber*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 研究比较了基于更新方程的两种Rt推断框架：单群体模型和多群体模型，分析了群体间世代时间分布差异对Rt估计的影响，并提出了在结构化群体下选择合适世代时间分布的方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 时间依赖的再生数Rt是追踪传染病传播和量化干预效果的重要指标，但传统更新方程模型假设所有群体共享相同的世代时间分布，而现实中不同群体间的世代时间分布可能存在系统性差异，这可能导致Rt估计偏差。

Method: 1) 比较基于更新方程的两种Rt推断框架：单群体同质模型和考虑结构化群体的多群体模型；2) 通过解析分析和模拟研究，探究两种模型结论差异的条件；3) 提出为单群体模型选择合适世代时间分布的方法，使其能正确封装不同群体间的变异；4) 使用真实流行病数据验证两种模型的实践差异。

Result: 研究发现：1) 单群体模型和多群体模型的Rt估计在实践中确实存在差异；2) 当群体间世代时间分布存在系统性差异时，使用单群体模型可能导致Rt估计偏差；3) 通过选择适当的世代时间分布，单群体模型可以在一定程度上适应结构化群体；4) 真实数据分析证实了两种模型估计结果的差异。

Conclusion: 研究强调了收集详细流行病数据和考虑群体间差异的重要性，以提高Rt估计的准确性，从而更好地指导公共卫生政策响应。需要更严格的数据收集和模型选择来确保Rt推断的可靠性。

Abstract: Due to its ability to summarise 'real-time' epidemic behaviour, the time-dependent reproduction number, Rt, is a useful metric for tracking pathogen transmission and quantifying the effects of interventions during infectious disease outbreaks. The predominant models underlying inferred Rt trajectories are renewal equations, their success owing in part to the relatively few assumptions they require. One necessary assumption is the generation time distribution, which summarises the time periods between infections in infector-infectee transmission pairs. This distribution is typically assumed to be the same across all members of a population. In reality, however, it may vary systematically between population groups. In this study, we consider two Rt inference frameworks based on renewal equation models: one for a single, homogeneous group and another accounting for a structured population. We compare the estimates of Rt generated by the two models and investigate, both analytically and through simulations, under which conditions the conclusions drawn from these modelling paradigms differ. We also demonstrate a methodology for selecting the generation time for the one-group model that correctly encapsulates variations between different population groups; this allows us to use a renewal framework for a one-group model to infer Rt when, in fact, the population is structured. Finally, we use real epidemic data to demonstrate that practical Rt estimates can differ depending on whether the underlying model is the one-group model or the multi-group model. Our results motivate the need for rigorous collection of detailed epidemic data and consideration of differences between population groups to improve the accuracy of Rt estimates that are used to guide public health policy responses.

The framework can also help us make cross-study comparison of abscopal effects and standardizes the reporting of abscopal magnitude beyond simple statistical significance.

Main category: q-bio.QM

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究在系统动力学推断中，添加分类单元（taxa）何时以及为何能改善推断效果，理解序列数据本身在揭示潜在谱系方面的根本限制。

Method: 构建观测末端的自然排序，对结果过滤应用序贯贝叶斯分析，将添加分类单元的期望方差减少分解为学习、失配和协方差分量，根据失配的路径行为将估计量分类为学习类别。

Conclusion: 该框架揭示了系统动力学推断中数据添加效应的基本机制，并确定了序列数据在揭示潜在谱系方面的根本限制，为理解何时以及为何添加分类单元能改善推断提供了理论基础。

The gap between oracle and analyst is irreducible assumptions that are likely to hold for many real phylodynamic estimands, establishing a fundamental limit on what sequence data alone can reveal about the latent genealogy.

### [5] [Association of Radiologic PPFE Change with Mortality in Lung Cancer Screening Cohorts](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09531)

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 通过低剂量CT自动量化胸膜肺实质弹力纤维增生症（PPFE）的纵向变化，发现PPFE进展与肺癌筛查人群的死亡率和呼吸系统不良结局独立相关。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: PPFE是一种与间质性肺病死亡率增加相关的上叶为主的纤维化异常，但其在肺癌筛查人群中的影像学进展的临床意义尚不清楚。研究旨在探讨PPFE的纵向变化是否独立与死亡率和呼吸系统发病率相关。
