### [1] [An analytical framework to unify ecological and engineering resilience near critical transitions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13921)
*Tristan Gamot,Tom J. M. Van Dooren*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文建立了分岔诱发临界转换（ tipping points ）附近生态韧性与工程韧性的尺度律理论框架，证明了这两类韧性在分岔点附近存在内在关联，并推导了不同分岔类型下的韧性衰减速率。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 复杂系统常表现出临界转换（ tipping points ），理解分岔点附近生态韧性与工程韧性的联合变化对表征和预测这类突变至关重要，但此前缺乏统一的理论框架。

Method: 利用规范型理论（ normal form theory ）推导韧性指标随分岔参数距离变化的尺度律，并在典型模型上验证。

Result: 不同分岔类型和韧性指标的衰减速率不同；在分岔点足够近时，生态韧性与工程韧性存在内在联系；推导的尺度律替代了之前基于启发式论证的标度关系。

Conclusion: 该理论框架为理解和预测分岔诱发临界转换时的韧性变化提供了严格的数学基础，并在三个代表性一维系统中得到验证。

Abstract: The capacity of dynamical systems to resist and recover from perturbations, broadly referred to as resilience, is commonly expressed by two complementary quantities: ecological and engineering resilience. As many complex systems exhibit critical transitions, or tipping points, understanding how these resiliences jointly change nearby them is central to characterising and anticipating such shifts. Here, we develop a theoretical framework that clarifies this for bifurcation-induced tipping, i.e., critical transitions triggered by the crossing of a local bifurcation. Using normal form theory, we derive explicit scaling laws for commonly used resilience metrics as functions of the distance to the bifurcation point in parameter space, and show that these extend to general models up to a scaling factor. They are particularly relevant for detecting tipping, where the relative behaviour of metrics matters more than their absolute values. The rates at which metrics decrease as the bifurcation is approached depend on both the type of bifurcation and the metric considered. Furthermore, our results show that, sufficiently close to a local bifurcation, resiliences are intrinsically linked. Our predictions, which replace previously proposed scalings based on heuristic arguments, are validated for three representative models covering all commonly encountered local bifurcations in one-dimensional systems.

### [2] [CisTransCell: Single-Cell Perturbation Prediction via Gene Function, Regulatory Control, and Cellular Context](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13713)
*Wei Zhang,Xun Jiang,Yuesi Xi,Ming Tang*

Main category: q-bio.GN

TL;DR: CisTransCell是一个多模态框架，通过整合基因的调控序列先验和编码序列先验，实现了零样本下对单细胞遗传扰动转录响应的精准预测。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 单细胞生物学中预测遗传扰动后的转录响应是一个核心难题，尤其是在零样本场景下（扰动基因或基因组合在训练中未出现）。扰动效应不仅取决于表达状态，还依赖于基因产物如何影响下游因子、顺式调控元件以及当前细胞状态的调控程序，现有方法难以捕捉这种生物复杂性。

Method: 提出CisTransCell，一种细胞条件化的多模态框架。为每个基因引入两种互补先验：调控序列先验（捕获基因如何被调控）和编码序列先验（捕获基因产物的功能）。通过将这些先验与细胞表达状态整合，将扰动响应建模为从基因功能→调控控制→下游转录变化的级联过程。

Result: 在基准单细胞扰动数据集上，CisTransCell在零样本扰动预测任务中取得了强劲性能。

Conclusion: CisTransCell通过融合调控序列和编码序列先验，有效建模了遗传扰动响应的生物级联过程，为零样本单细胞扰动预测提供了有力工具。

Abstract: Predicting cellular transcriptional responses to genetic perturbations is a central problem in single-cell biology, especially in the zero-shot setting where the perturbed gene or gene combination is unseen during training. A major difficulty is that perturbation effects are not determined by expression state alone: they depend on how the perturbed gene product influences other genes and proteins, how those downstream factors act on cis-regulatory elements, and which regulatory programs are active in the current cell state. To better capture this biological complexity, we propose CisTransCell, a cell-conditioned multi-modal framework for single-cell perturbation prediction that augments each gene with two complementary priors: a regulatory-sequence prior that captures how the gene is controlled, and a coding-sequence prior that captures what the gene product does. By integrating these priors with cellular expression state, CisTransCell models perturbation response as a cascade from gene function to regulatory control to downstream transcriptional change. Experiments on benchmark single-cell perturbation datasets show that CisTransCell achieves strong performance in zero-shot perturbation prediction.

</details>
