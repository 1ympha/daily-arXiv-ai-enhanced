### [1] [Global remote sensing reveals vegetation clustering as a physical footprint of shifting aridity trends in drylands](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22122)
*David Pinto-Ramos,Marcel Gabriel Clerc,Abdelkader Makhoute,Mustapha Tlidi*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 该研究通过全球八种不同生态系统的遥感数据验证，发现植被斑块的空间形态可作为干旱区生态系统退化或恢复路径的直接物理足迹：退化时呈现周期性阵列，恢复时表现为无标度聚类。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有理论预测空间异质但各向同性的环境会导致平滑滞后回路，产生周期性（退化）或无序聚类（恢复）模式，但缺乏全球尺度的实证验证。

Method: 利用遥感数据对全球八个不同生态系统进行实证验证，结合历史生物气候数据库，分析植被斑块的空间形态特征。

Result: 干旱加剧的生态系统呈现具有明确波长的周期性阵列，而干旱减缓的恢复生态系统则表现为无标度聚类。

Conclusion: 植被斑块空间形态可作为非破坏性、基于卫星的稳健指标，用于诊断干旱生态系统处于退化还是恢复路径。

Abstract: Due to climatic changes, excessive grazing, and deforestation, semi-arid and arid ecosystems are vulnerable to desertification and land degradation. As aridity increases, vegetation cover often self-organizes into spatial patterns before collapsing to bare soil. While recent theoretical work has established that spatially heterogeneous yet isotropic environments induce a smooth hysteresis loop -- yielding either periodic (hexagonal) patterns during degradation or disordered (clustered) patterns during recovery -- empirical validation of this physical footprint at a global scale has been lacking. Here, we present an extensive empirical validation using remote sensing across eight distinct global ecosystems, coupled with historical bio-climatic databases. We demonstrate that the spatial morphology of vegetation patches acts as a direct physical footprint of the ecosystem's historical aridity trend. Our results show that ecosystems experiencing increasing aridity display periodic arrays with a defined wavelength, whereas those recovering under decreasing aridity exhibit scale-free clustering. This framework provides a non-destructive, robust satellite-based indicator for diagnosing whether a dryland ecosystem is on a degradation or recovery pathway.

### [2] [Simple sign epistasis and evolutionary detours in fitness landscapes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22611)
*Paolo Ribeca,Alejandro Castro,Alejandro Lage-Castellanos,Alisa Sergeeva,Sebastian Matuszewski,Ruben Gustavo Paccosi,Vitaly Belik,Mahan Ghafari,Joachim Krug,Guillaume Achaz,Luca Ferretti*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文研究了简单符号上位性在弱上位性适应性地形中的普遍性及其与进化绕路的关系，发现简单符号上位性远比互惠符号上位性更常见，且与间接、更长的适应度增加路径相关。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 尽管互惠符号上位性与多峰地形和粗糙度已被充分研究，但简单符号上位性在适应性地形中的作用和相对频率尚未被系统探讨。

Method: 通过理论证明简单符号上位性与进化绕路（包含回复突变的间接适应度增加路径）的关联，并结合实验解析的弱上位性地形数据进行统计比较，同时推导了多数地形模型的理论预测。

Result: 在实验解析的弱上位性地形中，简单符号上位性频率显著高于互惠符号上位性；该结果与大多数地形模型的理论预测一致，但块模型和RNA稳定性地形（由成对等位基因不相容性主导）除外。

Conclusion: 进化绕路是弱上位性地形中进化轨迹的普遍特征，简单符号上位性是其主要驱动因素。

Abstract: In epistatic fitness landscapes, the fitness effect of a mutation depends on the genetic background and may even switch between deleterious and beneficial depending on the presence of another mutation. Epistatic interactions may cause both mutations to change the sign of each other's fitness effects (reciprocal sign epistasis) or only one mutation to do so (simple sign epistasis). Both these forms of epistasis influence evolutionary trajectories. While reciprocal sign epistasis has been associated with multi-peaked landscapes and their ruggedness, the role and relative frequency of simple sign epistasis in fitness landscapes have not been systematically investigated. Here, we prove that the presence of simple sign epistasis is associated with evolutionary detours, i.e., indirect, longer fitness-increasing paths to fitness peaks that include back-mutations. We also show that in experimentally resolved, weakly epistatic landscapes, simple sign epistasis occurs much more frequently than reciprocal sign epistasis. This result is consistent with the theoretical predictions we derive for most landscape models, with the exception of the block model and of landscapes dominated by pairwise allelic incompatibilities, such as RNA stability landscapes. Our results suggest that detours represent a general feature of evolutionary trajectories in weakly epistatic landscapes.

### [3] [Local growth laws determine global shape of molluscan shells](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21988)
*Huan Liu,Kaushik Bhattacharya*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于李群作用的数学模型，用三个参数（标量缩放、向量方向、曲线边缘）统一描述了几乎所有软体动物壳的形状生成规律，并关联到系统发育树。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解软体动物壳在环境变化下仍能保持物种特征形状的鲁棒复杂性，遵循D'Arcy Thompson的思想，探索无需复杂生物机制控制的局部几何生长法则。

Method: 基于两个原则：1）生长由固定的生长法则重复连续应用驱动；2）生长法则仅依赖于生长边缘的局部几何。由此导出李群作用生成壳形，并得到特定的李群表示，最终将壳形归结为三个参数。

Result: 几乎所有已知软体动物壳的形状可以用三个参数描述：标量（缩放）、向量（方向）和曲线（原壳边缘），并与系统发育树建立了关联。

Conclusion: 该工作不仅提供了形态发生学的新见解，还可能为工程复杂结构的构建提供新思路。

Abstract: Molluscan shells come in various shapes and sizes. Despite this diversity, each species produces a shell with a characteristic shape that is independent of environmental conditions. We seek to understand this robust complexity. We are guided by two principles in the spirit of D'Arcy Thompson. First, the growth is governed by the repeated and continuous application of a fixed growth law, even as the shell evolves in overall shape, without any complex biological machinery to monitor and control the growth. Second, the growth law depends solely on local geometry at the shell's growing edge. The first principle naturally leads to the mathematical statement that the shape of the shell is generated by the action of a Lie group on a protoconch. The second naturally leads to a particular representation of the Lie group. We use this representation to show that the shapes of nearly all known molluscan shells can be described by essentially three parameters: a scalar (scaling), a vector (orientation), and a curve (edge of the protoconch). We relate these parameters to the phylogenetic tree. In addition to the morphogenetic insight, our results potentially point to a new approach to engineering complex structures.

</details>

### [4] [Supregraph: Enabling Information-Optimal Assembly Graph Representation of a Read Set](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21951)
*Anton Bankevich*

Main category: q-bio.GN

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种新的基因组组装图模型——supregraphs，它通过迭代转换de Bruijn图构建，在无错误读取假设下提供理论最优的基因组组装表示。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的de Bruijn图和重叠图在基因组组装中各有局限性：de Bruijn图无法完整表示读取信息，而重叠图因需要丢弃包含读取而导致contig断裂。本文旨在建立一种正式的数学模型，确定如何正确地将读取集转换为组装图。

Method: 提出了supregraphs这一新类别的组装图，通过迭代使用复用（multiplexing）过程转换de Bruijn图来构建，该过程此前已在LJA和Verkko基因组组装器中得到应用。

Result: 证明了在无错误读取假设下，存在一种正确的读取集表示形式——supregraphs；并证明了在一组自然假设条件下，supregraphs可为构建理论最优的基因组组装提供基础。

Conclusion: Supregraphs为基因组组装提供了一种形式化的数学框架，克服了传统方法的局限性，有望实现理论最优的组装结果。

Abstract: The first step in any genome assembly algorithm entails the conversion from the domain of strings and overlaps to the language of graphs and paths, typically using one of the two conventional methods: de Bruijn graphs or overlap graphs. However, both standard approaches are known to have limitations. De Bruijn graphs fail to represent complete information from reads, while the overlap graphs often produce artificial breaks in contigs due to the necessity to discard contained reads as a preliminary step. In this work we present a mathematical model for genome assembly that provides a formal framework to determine what constitutes a correct conversion of a read set into an assembly graph under the assumption of error-free reads. We prove that a correct representation of a read set exists in the form of a new class of assembly graphs, which we call supregraphs. We show that supregraphs can be constructed by iteratively transforming de Bruijn graphs using the multiplexing procedure, previously employed in the genome assemblers LJA and Verkko. Finally, we demonstrate that, under a set of natural assumptions, supregraphs provide a foundation for constructing theoretically optimal genome assemblies.

</details>
