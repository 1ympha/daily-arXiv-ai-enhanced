### [1] [A general classification of the replication dynamics with a unique fixed point in the interior of simplex $S_N$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13883)
*Hongju,Chen,Bin Yi,Zhanshan,Ma*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文研究了进化博弈论中复制动态方程的固定点分类问题，给出了当 n≥2 时，单纯形内部存在唯一固定点的充要条件，并讨论了不同类型。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 当 n>3 时，复制动态方程的分类问题尚未解决，本文旨在填补这一空白。

Method: 通过数学分析，推导出复制动态方程在单纯形内部存在唯一固定点的充要条件，并进行类型讨论。

Result: 给出了 n≥2 时复制动态方程在 IntS_n 中存在唯一固定点的充要条件。

Conclusion: 为更高维复制动态方程的分类提供了理论基础。

Abstract: The replication dynamics (differential equation system) is the foundation of evolutionary game theory. When n=2, there are four possible types of replication dynamics. When n=3, there are 49 possible types of replication dynamics. However, when n>3, the classification of replication dynamics has not been solved. In this article, the sufficient and necessary conditions of the replication dynamics equation with a unique fixed point in the interior of simplex $S_n$(Int$S_n$) for $n\geq 2$ are presented. Furthermore, the different types of replication dynamics equations with a unique fixed point in IntSn is discussed.
