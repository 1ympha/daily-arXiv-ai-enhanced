### [1] [Time-Varying Environmental and Polygenic Predictors of Substance Use Initiation in Youth: A Survival and Causal Modeling Study in the ABCD Cohort](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07368)
*Mengman Wei,Qian Peng*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 研究整合了动态环境因素和多基因风险评分，识别青少年物质使用起始的关键风险因素，发现冲动性、父母监控和咖啡因使用是重要预测因子。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 早期开始使用酒精、尼古丁、大麻等物质会预测后期的物质使用障碍和相关精神病理问题。研究旨在整合时间变化的环境因素与多基因风险评分，识别青少年物质使用起始的决定因素。

Method: 使用ABCD研究数据，定义首次使用酒精、尼古丁、大麻和任何物质的时间-事件结局。构建高维时间变化环境协变量面板（涵盖家庭、学校、社区、行为和健康领域），结合时间不变协变量和物质使用障碍的多基因风险评分。应用具有聚类标准误的时间变化Cox模型。

Result: 单变量分析显示早期起始与多个环境领域广泛相关，包括冲动性、睡眠障碍、父母监控、咖啡因使用和学校功能。多变量模型中，冲动性特质、父母监控和特定健康生活方式因素保持稳健预测作用。多基因风险评分与早期起始正相关，尼古丁相关遗传风险效应最强最一致。边际结构模型分析表明，更高的父母监控具有保护作用，而更高的冲动性和咖啡因暴露与风险增加相关。

Conclusion: 整合动态环境暴露与遗传易感性可以识别青少年物质使用起始的关键风险因素，并突出可操作的预防目标，为预防干预提供科学依据。

Abstract: Early initiation of alcohol, nicotine, cannabis, and other substances predicts later substance use disorders and related psychopathology. We integrate time-varying environmental factors with polygenic risk scores (PRS) in a longitudinal framework to identify determinants of substance initiation in adolescence. Using data from the Adolescent Brain Cognitive Development (ABCD) Study with repeated assessments over approximately four years, we defined time-to-event outcomes for first use of alcohol, nicotine, cannabis, and any substance. We constructed high-dimensional panels of time-varying environmental covariates across family, school, neighborhood, behavioral, and health domains, alongside time-invariant covariates and PRS for alcohol, cannabis, nicotine, and general substance use disorders. Time-varying Cox models with clustered standard errors were applied.
  Univariate analyses showed broad associations between earlier initiation and multiple environmental domains, including impulsivity, sleep disturbance, parental monitoring, caffeine use, and school functioning. In multivariable models, a smaller set of predictors remained robust, particularly impulsivity traits, parental monitoring, and selected health and lifestyle factors. PRS were positively associated with earlier initiation, with the strongest and most consistent effects for nicotine-related genetic risk. Secondary analyses using marginal structural models suggested that higher parental monitoring is protective, whereas higher impulsivity and caffeine exposure are associated with increased risk.
  These results demonstrate that integrating dynamic environmental exposures with genetic liability can identify key risk factors for adolescent substance initiation and highlight actionable targets for prevention.

### [2] [Predicting Activity Cliffs for Autonomous Medicinal Chemistry](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07560)
*Michael Cuccarese*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 该研究开发了一个预测活性悬崖的系统，能识别哪些位置的小结构修改最可能导致活性显著变化，将化学家需要探索的位置从3.1个减少到2.1个（减少31%），并发布了开源代码和交互式网页应用。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 活性悬崖预测（识别小结构变化导致大活性变化的位置）一直是计算药物化学中的持久挑战。本研究关注一个简洁的定义：哪些位置的小修改最可能导致活性结果变化。

Method: 使用来自50个ChEMBL靶点、跨越6个蛋白质家族的2500万匹配分子对计算位置级敏感性。对于"哪些位置是真正的活性悬崖"问题，开发了一个包含11个特征和3D药效团背景的模型，使用SALI归一化来捕捉小修改导致不成比例大效应的情况。

Result: 模型在预测活性悬崖位置方面表现优异（NDCG@3 = 0.910 vs. 0.839随机），在六个蛋白质家族、新支架（0.913）和时间分割（0.878）上都具有良好泛化能力。模型能首先识别出悬崖易发位置的概率为53%（vs. 27%随机 - 2倍提升），将化学家需要探索的位置从3.1个减少到2.1个（减少31%）。仅从结构预测具体修改方案不可行（Spearman 0.268，在新支架上降至-0.31）。

Conclusion: 研究成功开发了一个能有效预测活性悬崖位置的系统，显著减少了化学家需要实验探索的位置数量。系统已作为开源代码和交互式网页应用发布，为药物化学中的活性悬崖预测提供了实用工具。

Abstract: Activity cliff prediction - identifying positions where small structural changes cause large potency shifts - has been a persistent challenge in computational medicinal chemistry. This work focuses on a parsimonious definition: which small modifications, at which positions, confer the highest probability of an outcome change. Position-level sensitivity is calculated using 25 million matched molecular pairs from 50 ChEMBL targets across six protein families, revealing that two questions have fundamentally different answers. "Which positions vary most?" is answered by scaffold size alone (NDCG@3 = 0.966), requiring no machine learning. "Which are true activity cliffs?" - where small modifications cause disproportionately large effects, as captured by SALI normalization - requires an 11-feature model with 3D pharmacophore context (NDCG@3 = 0.910 vs. 0.839 random), generalizing across all six protein families, novel scaffolds (0.913), and temporal splits (0.878). The model identifies the cliff-prone position first 53% of the time (vs. 27% random - 2x lift), reducing positions a chemist must explore from 3.1 to 2.1 - a 31% reduction in first-round experiments. Predicting which modification to make is not tractable from structure alone (Spearman 0.268, collapsing to -0.31 on novel scaffolds). The system is released as open-source code and an interactive webapp.

### [3] [Quantifying the Spatiotemporal Dynamics of Engineered Cardiac Microbundles](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07576)
*Hiba Kobeissi,Samuel J. DePalma,Javiera Jilberto,David Nordsletten,Brendon M. Baker,Emma Lejeune*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 开发了一个开源计算流程，用于量化人类诱导多能干细胞来源的心脏微束的时空收缩动态，定义了16个可解释的指标，应用于670个样本，揭示了收缩表型的连续变异特征。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 明场延时成像在心脏组织工程中广泛应用，但缺乏标准化、可解释的分析框架限制了可重复性和跨平台比较。

Method: 基于开源工具"MicroBundleCompute"和"MicroBundlePillarTrack"，开发了一个可扩展的计算流程，包括全场位移跟踪、应变重建、空间配准、降维和基于拓扑的矢量场分析，定义了16个结构、功能和时空指标。

Result: 应用于20个实验条件下的670个心脏微束，发现收缩表型呈现连续变异而非离散的特定条件聚类，条件内变异常超过条件间差异。冗余分析识别出10个核心指标，去噪位移场分析显示收缩主要由全局各向同性模式主导。

Conclusion: 开发了一个开源、可扩展的计算框架，用于可重复、可扩展的动态组织力学分析，所有软件和工作流程均已公开发布。

Abstract: Brightfield time-lapse imaging is widely used in cardiac tissue engineering, yet the absence of standardized, interpretable analytical frameworks limits reproducibility and cross-platform comparison. We present an open, scalable computational pipeline for quantifying spatiotemporal contractile dynamics in microscopy videos of human induced pluripotent stem cell-derived cardiac microbundles. Building on our open-source tools "MicroBundleCompute" and "MicroBundlePillarTrack," we define a suite of 16 interpretable structural, functional, and spatiotemporal metrics that capture tissue deformation, synchrony, and heterogeneity. The framework integrates full-field displacement tracking, strain reconstruction, spatial registration, dimensionality reduction, and topology-based vector-field analysis within a unified workflow. Applied to a dataset of 670 cardiac microbundles spanning 20 experimental conditions, the pipeline reveals continuous variation in contractile phenotypes rather than discrete condition-specific clustering, with intra-condition variability often exceeding inter-condition differences. Redundancy analysis identifies a reduced core set of 10 metrics that retain most informational content while minimizing multicollinearity. Analysis of denoised displacement fields shows that contraction is dominated by a global isotropic mode, with localized saddle-type deformation patterns present in approximately half of the samples. All software and workflows are released openly to enable reproducible, scalable analysis of dynamic tissue mechanics.
