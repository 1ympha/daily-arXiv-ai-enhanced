### [1] [Disentangling the interactive effects of anthropogenic disturbances on biodiversity](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29116)
*Isaac Planas-Sitjà,Ryosuke Iritani,Adam L. Cronin*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 该研究通过统一模型整合了竞争-扩散权衡、中度干扰假说和空间异质性三种共存机制，发现机制间的相互作用对物种共存至关重要，并揭示了在干扰梯度上随栖息地破碎化增加会出现双峰等多样性模式。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 人类活动通过气候变化、栖息地破碎化和干扰频率增加威胁生物多样性，但我们对这些因素相对重要性及其相互作用的了解仍然有限。现有理论研究分散，需要整合多种共存机制来更好地理解物种共存。

Method: 采用理论建模方法，将竞争-扩散权衡、中度干扰假说和空间异质性三种共存机制整合到统一模型中。开发了将栖息地自相关整合到微分方程系统的新方法，创建了可用于研究不同干扰和栖息地结构情景下竞争层次中多个物种共存的简单灵活模型。

Result: 研究发现：1) 不同机制间的相互作用对解释物种共存至关重要；2) 随着栖息地破碎化增加，在干扰梯度上会出现与中度干扰假说预测的单峰曲线不同的多样性模式（如双峰模式）；3) 在高干扰情景下，栖息地丧失的影响超过栖息地自相关效应，但在低干扰下自相关可以塑造物种共存。

Conclusion: 研究强调了在生物多样性管理中整合空间和时间机制的必要性，表明考虑多种共存机制及其相互作用对于理解和预测人类活动影响下的生物多样性模式至关重要。

Abstract: Anthropogenic activity threatens biodiversity through climate change, habitat fragmentation, and increasing frequency and scale of disturbance. Various theoretical studies have sought to shed light on how these factors could promote or hinder the coexistence of species. However, our understanding of the relative importance of, and interactions between, these factors remains limited. In this study, we employ a theoretical approach integrating three commonly cited coexistence mechanisms -- the competition-colonisation trade-off, the intermediate disturbance hypothesis, and spatial heterogeneity -- into a unified model. We implement a novel method to integrate habitat autocorrelation into a system of differential equations, to create a simple and flexible model that can be used to investigate coexistence of multiple species arranged in a competitive hierarchy under different disturbance and habitat structure scenarios. Using this model, we find that considering interactions between different mechanisms is crucial for explaining the coexistence of species. Biodiversity patterns alternative to the uni-peak curve predicted by the intermediate disturbance hypothesis (e.g., bimodal) emerge along disturbance gradients as habitat fragmentation increases. Furthermore, habitat loss outweighs habitat autocorrelation effects in highly disturbed scenarios, yet autocorrelation can shape species coexistence under low disturbance. These findings underscore the need to integrate spatial and temporal mechanisms in biodiversity management.

### [2] [FcsIT: An Open-Source, Cross-Platform Tool for Correlation and Analysis of Fluorescence Correlation Spectroscopy Data](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29684)
*Tomasz Kalwarczyk*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: FcsIT是一个平台无关的开源工具，用于计算荧光相关光谱数据的相关性和拟合，提供TTTR数据读取、相关计算、TCSPC滤波等功能，具有直观的拟合界面和自定义模型功能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 开发一个平台无关的开源工具，为荧光相关光谱（FCS）用户提供高质量的数据相关性和拟合分析，替代商业软件，并支持自定义模型。

Method: 使用Python编写，基于Dear PyGUI引擎构建界面，采用循环块自举法计算相关数据及其方差，提供九个预定义数学模型和用户自定义模型功能。

Result: FcsIT的数据质量与商业软件相当，验证了其在模拟FCS数据和真实FCS实验中的可用性，具有广泛的用户吸引力。

Conclusion: FcsIT是一个功能强大、用户友好的开源FCS分析工具，能够满足各种FCS用户的需求，具有广泛的应用潜力。

Abstract: FcsIT is a platform-independent, open-source tool for calculating the correlation and fitting fluorescence correlation spectroscopy data. The software is written in Python and uses a powerful Dear PyGUI engine for its interface. It provides reading and correlating the TTTR data, as well as TCSPC filtering of the photon time-trace data. The circular-block bootstrap method applied to the calculation of correlation data and its variance results in data quality comparable to that obtained with commercially available software. An intuitive fitting interface provides efficient analysis of large datasets and includes nine predefined mathematical models for fitting correlation curves. Moreover, it allows users to add their own models in a user-friendly manner. Validation of the FcsIT tool against simulated FCS data and real FCS experiments confirms its usability and potential appeal to a wide variety of FCS users.

### [3] [ParetoEnsembles.jl: A Julia Package for Multiobjective Parameter Estimation Using Pareto Optimal Ensemble Techniques](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29986)
*Jeffrey D. Varner*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: ParetoEnsembles.jl是一个Julia包，使用帕累托最优集成技术生成参数集成，无需梯度信息，通过改进算法效率和并行执行来降低不确定性表征的门槛。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 自然和人工系统的数学模型通常有许多需要从多个可能冲突的数据集中估计的可调参数。与其报告单一的最佳拟合参数向量，不如生成一个参数集集成，共同映射出竞争目标之间的权衡关系。

Method: 使用基于模拟退火的帕累托最优集成技术(POETs)，无需梯度信息。实现中修正了原始支配关系（从弱帕累托支配改为严格帕累托支配），通过增量更新方案将每次迭代的排序成本从O(n²m)降低到O(nm)，并添加多链并行执行以提高前沿覆盖。

Result: 在无细胞基因表达模型和血液凝固级联模型上进行了演示。合成数据研究揭示了参数可识别性结构，单个速率常数偏差数倍但模型预测准确度达7%。五重复覆盖分析确认时序特征可靠覆盖而峰值振幅系统性地过于自信。验证显示集成预测保留条件误差在10%以内。

Conclusion: 通过使集成生成轻量化和易访问，ParetoEnsembles.jl旨在降低机制建模中常规不确定性表征的门槛。

Abstract: Mathematical models of natural and man-made systems often have many adjustable parameters that must be estimated from multiple, potentially conflicting datasets. Rather than reporting a single best-fit parameter vector, it is often more informative to generate an ensemble of parameter sets that collectively map out the trade-offs among competing objectives. This paper presents ParetoEnsembles.jl, an open-source Julia package that generates such ensembles using Pareto Optimal Ensemble Techniques (POETs), a simulated-annealing-based algorithm that requires no gradient information. The implementation corrects the original dominance relation from weak to strict Pareto dominance, reduces the per-iteration ranking cost from $O(n^2 m)$ to $O(nm)$ through an incremental update scheme, and adds multi-chain parallel execution for improved front coverage. We demonstrate the package on a cell-free gene expression model fitted to experimental data and a blood coagulation cascade model with ten estimated rate constants and three objectives. A controlled synthetic-data study reveals parameter identifiability structure, with individual rate constants off by several-fold yet model predictions accurate to 7%. A five-replicate coverage analysis confirms that timing features are reliably covered while peak amplitude is systematically overconfident. Validation against published experimental thrombin generation data demonstrates that the ensemble predicts held-out conditions to within 10% despite inherent model approximation error. By making ensemble generation lightweight and accessible, ParetoEnsembles.jl aims to lower the barrier to routine uncertainty characterization in mechanistic modeling.

</details>
