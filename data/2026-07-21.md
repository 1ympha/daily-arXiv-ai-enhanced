TL;DR: 本文系统评估了六种单细胞与空间基础模型（Nicheformer、CellPLM、scGPT-spatial、GenePT、scELMo、Novae），发现在不同模态、生物学任务和评估指标下，没有模型能全面领先，性能高度依赖于具体场景，为模型选择提供了实践指导。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 单细胞与空间基础模型声称具有可迁移的生物学表征能力，但其在不同模态、生物学领域和分析任务中的通用性尚未得到充分验证，缺乏系统性的公平比较。

Method: 使用统一的基准框架，在scRNA-seq、空间转录组学和Perturb-seq三种数据模态上，对六种代表性模型进行零样本和持续预训练下的聚类、监督注释、标记基因一致性和扰动预测等任务的评估。

Abstract: Single-cell and spatial foundation models promise transferable biological representations, yet their generality remains largely untested across modalities, biological domains and analytical tasks. We benchmarked six representative models, Nicheformer, CellPLM, scGPT-spatial, GenePT, scELMo and Novae, using a harmonised framework spanning scRNA-seq, spatial transcriptomics and Perturb-seq. We evaluated zero-shot and continually pretrained clustering, supervised annotation, marker-gene concordance and perturbation prediction.   <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 整合4个公开全血转录组队列（936名患者，248例死亡事件），统一至7660个基因的特征空间。采用留一队列交叉验证，比较基因级、通路级和混合分子表征，使用逻辑回归、随机森林、XGBoost和LightGBM四种模型。除AUROC和AUPRC外，还通过共形预测、校准分析、选择性预测和提出的通路稳定性指数进行评估。

Yet, the within-cohort evaluation strategies employed by many transcriptomic models inadequately reflect deployment across independent hospitals. Outside deployment scenarios introduce a cohort shift that can substantially degrade predictive performance and reliability of uncertainty estimates. We present a framework for evaluating transcriptomic sepsis mortality prediction under realistic cross-cohort deployment, systematically comparing gene-level, pathway-level and hybrid molecular representations. Four publicly available whole-blood transcriptomic cohorts consisting of 936 patients and 248 mortality events were harmonized into a shared 7,660-gene feature space and evaluated under leave-one-cohort-out validation using logistic regression, random forests, XGBoost and LightGBM. Beyond AUROC and AUPRC, model behavior was evaluated via conformal prediction, calibration analysis, selective prediction and the proposed Pathway Stability Index. Gene-level and hybrid representations were found to generally achieve the strongest discriminative performance, whereas pathway-level representations exhibited greater robustness across model families, more reliable uncertainty behavior under cross-cohort shift and stable molecular signatures enriched for immune and host-defense processes identified through Gene Ontology and KEGG enrichment analyses. These findings demonstrate that molecular representation influences not only predictive discrimination but also calibration, uncertainty reliability, biological coherence and transferability under external validation.

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有肾衰竭风险方程（如KFRE）仅依赖单次实验室测量值，未充分利用纵向实验室检测模式，导致预测精度受限，尤其是长期风险识别不足。

Method: 基于540万人的数据构建CLARK，纳入270,009名CKD患者，通过可解释的纵向扩展方法整合重复实验室检测序列，形成轨迹特征，并与静态最新值模型进行对比。

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 采用通用的转录后调控模型，应用泊松到达划分（PPA）框架，将其映射为类似于启动子调控转录的模型，从而借由现有启动子模型结果推导大偏差相关量（速率函数、驱动过程）。

However, the corresponding effects of post-transcriptional regulation on the occurrence of rare events corresponding to large deviations are far less explored and have only been considered for a special model. Here, we take a general model of post-transcriptional regulation and apply the partitioning of Poisson arrivals (PPA) framework to map it onto a model that resembles promoter-based regulation of transcription, leading to a general framework to obtain objects of interest in large deviations (i.e. large deviation rate function for quantifying the likelihood of observing rare protein production rates and the corresponding driven process that characterizes the system dynamics conditional on the rare event) for models of post-transcriptional regulation directly from prior results for promoter-based models. The results derived create new avenues to analyze rare events in general models of post-transcriptional regulation pertaining to various different biological settings.

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文研究了带有频率依赖回报的公共物品博弈，发现合作是否有利完全取决于全贡献状态下的投资回报率，且该规律在引入惩罚机制后依然成立——全合作状态回报率促进合作，全惩罚状态回报率促进惩罚。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统公共物品博弈中乘数因子通常设为常数，但现实中投资回报往往随群体策略组成动态变化，而此类频率依赖回报的演化动力学尚未被充分理解。

Method: 引入了通用的频率依赖乘数因子（取决于群体内策略组成），通过理论分析推导合作有利的数学条件，并进一步引入同伴惩罚机制，系统探索其对合作困境和二阶搭便车问题的影响。

Result: 合作是否优于背叛仅取决于全贡献状态下的投资回报率，该值越高合作者丰度越高。在引入惩罚后，合作者/惩罚者的丰度仅由全合作和全惩罚状态下的回报值决定。

Abstract: The public goods game serves as a significant paradigm for investigating the emergence and maintenance of cooperation in conflicting situations. In the traditional public goods game, the multiplication factor characterizing the synergy effect of common efforts is typically assumed to be constant. To date, the evolutionary dynamics of the public goods game with such frequency-dependent returns have remained not fully understood. In this work, we introduce a general frequency-dependent multiplication factor that depends on the strategy composition within the game group. Through theoretical analysis, we derive the mathematical conditions under which cooperation is favored. Our results show that whether cooperation has an evolutionary advantage over defection depends on the investment return rate in the full-contribution state of the game group, irrespective of the return rates in other states. Our results highlight that the abundance of cooperators or punishers is governed solely by the investment return values in the full-cooperation and full-punishment compositions of the group. A higher return rate in the full-cooperation state facilitates the promotion of cooperation, whereas a higher return rate in the full-punishment state favors the emergence of punishment. Our theoretical findings are verified by individual-based simulations.

### [6] [How genome redundancy can promote evolutionary innovation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.17687)
*Domenico Caudo,Mattia Miotto,Giancarlo Ruocco,Greta Grassmann*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本研究通过最小进化模型发现，多倍体在环境剧变时具有优势，且其适应性取决于遗传方式、基因型-表型映射以及开发-探索-收敛速度三者间的权衡。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 多倍体在生物界广泛存在，但其究竟是进化创新还是进化死胡同仍存争议，作者试图通过模型探究多倍体在不同选择压力下的适应优势。

Method: 构建了一个最小进化模型，系统考察了倍性水平与遗传方式（随机遗传 vs 结构化遗传）及基因型-表型映射（非线性最大型 vs 加性型）之间的相互作用。

Result: 1) 多倍体在环境剧变时具有优势；2) 随机遗传+最大型映射同时最大化表型开发和景观探索；3) 结构化遗传+加性映射在低到中等倍性水平有最优表现，与自然植物和细菌分布一致；4) 自由选择染色体数时，群体趋近于开发、探索和收敛速度的权衡结果。

Abstract: Polyploidy is defined as the existence of more than two complete sets of homologous chromosomes. Despite it being a widespread phenomenon across the tree of life, its role as either an evolutionary innovation or a dead end is still debated. Through a minimal evolutionary model we find that polyploidy is especially advantageous during abrupt environmental changes, confirming that polyploidization is often associated with ecological upheavals. We observe that stochastic inheritance combined with a nonlinear (maximum-based) genotype-phenotype mapping maximizes both phenotypic exploitation and landscape exploration across all environments. The relative weight of these three factors depends on the fitness landscape, providing a unifying framework for understanding when and why polyploidy is favored by natural selection.
