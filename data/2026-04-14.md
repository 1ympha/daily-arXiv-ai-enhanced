### [1] [Fragmentation is a diversity ratchet](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.09966)
*Russell K. Standish*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 景观破碎化通过降低物种间连接性来维持更高生物多样性，重新连接破碎景观会引发灭绝事件，而破碎-融合的循环过程会像棘轮效应一样推动生态系统达到更高多样性水平


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究景观破碎化与连接性对生物多样性的影响，探讨为什么破碎化景观反而能维持更高多样性，以及重新连接破碎景观的生态后果

Method: 通过理论模型分析景观破碎化与连接性对物种多样性的影响机制，研究破碎-融合循环过程的生态效应

Result: 破碎化景观通过降低物种间连接性维持更高多样性；重新连接破碎景观会引发灭绝事件，优先淘汰高度连接的物种；破碎-融合的循环过程会产生棘轮效应，推动生态系统达到比持续连接状态更高的多样性水平

Conclusion: 景观破碎化并非总是对生物多样性有害，适度的破碎化通过限制物种间连接性可以维持更高多样性；重新连接破碎景观需要谨慎，可能引发灭绝事件；破碎-融合的循环过程可以推动生态系统向更高多样性演化

Abstract: A fragmented landscape reduces the impact of interspecies
  connectivity, leading to higher diversity levels than otherwise
  possible in a connected landscape. Reconnecting a previously
  fragmented landscape initiates an extinction event, preferentially
  weeding out more highly connected species. A sequence of
  fragmentation-coalescence events will drive the ecosystem to higher
  levels of diversity in a ratchet-like effect, than if the landscape
  continuously remained connected.

### [2] [Universal statistical signatures of evolution in artificial intelligence architectures](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.10571)
*Theodor Spiro*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: AI架构演化的统计规律与生物演化相似，遵循重尾分布，有益突变比例更高（13% vs 1-6%），显示定向搜索优势，但分布形式保持一致。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究人工智能架构演化是否遵循与生物演化相同的统计规律，探索演化规律是否与选择机制无关，而是由适应度景观拓扑结构决定。

Method: 收集935个来自161篇文献的消融实验数据，分析架构修改的适应度效应分布（DFE），使用统计方法比较AI与生物系统的演化模式。

Result: AI架构修改的适应度效应分布遵循重尾Student's t分布（68%有害，19%中性，13%有益），与果蝇和酵母相似；有益突变比例高于生物系统；架构起源遵循逻辑斯蒂动力学，出现间断平衡和适应性辐射；14个架构特征独立发明3-5次，类似生物趋同演化。

Conclusion: 演化统计结构是基质无关的，由适应度景观拓扑结构而非选择机制决定，AI演化与生物演化共享相同的统计规律。

Abstract: We test whether artificial intelligence architectural evolution obeys the same statistical laws as biological evolution. Compiling 935 ablation experiments from 161 publications, we show that the distribution of fitness effects (DFE) of architectural modifications follows a heavy-tailed Student's t-distribution with proportions (68% deleterious, 19% neutral, 13% beneficial for major ablations, n=568) that place AI between compact viral genomes and simple eukaryotes. The DFE shape matches D. melanogaster (normalized KS=0.07) and S. cerevisiae (KS=0.09); the elevated beneficial fraction (13% vs. 1-6% in biology) quantifies the advantage of directed over blind search while preserving the distributional form. Architectural origination follows logistic dynamics (R^2=0.994) with punctuated equilibria and adaptive radiation into domain niches. Fourteen architectural traits were independently invented 3-5 times, paralleling biological convergences. These results demonstrate that the statistical structure of evolution is substrate-independent, determined by fitness landscape topology rather than the mechanism of selection.

### [3] [How complex behavioural contagion can prevent infectious diseases from becoming endemic](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.10995)
*Michael J. Plank,Matt Ryan,Lloyd Chapman,Roslyn I. Hickson,Thomas House,Emma McBryde,James M. McCaw*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 该研究探讨了传染病模型中的复杂行为传染机制，发现非线性行为采纳率可导致多个无病平衡点，在某些参数条件下，疫情爆发反而能触发自我维持的行为改变，最终导致疾病消除。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传染病传播与宿主行为变化之间存在复杂的双向互动关系。将适应性行为变化纳入流行病模型对于更好地理解传染病动态和开发政策相关的建模工具变得越来越重要。行为动态的一个重要方面是社会传染，即人们倾向于采纳周围其他人表现出的行为。

Method: 研究探索了一个具有复杂行为传染的流行病模型，其中行为采纳率是行为接触者数量的非线性函数。识别了模型的关键分岔参数，包括基本再生数R0、行为对疾病传播影响的强度，以及行为采纳相对于行为放弃的速度。

Result: 在某些参数空间区域，模型存在多个无病平衡点。在这种情况下，在初始行为实践水平较低的人群中发生疫情，可能触发自我维持的行为增加，从而导致疾病被消除。在某些情况下，中等R0值会导致疾病成为地方病，而较高的R0值可能导致行为驱动的疾病消除。这种由疫情触发行为采纳导致疾病消除的机制在存在和不存在暂时性感染后免疫的情况下都可能发生。

Conclusion: 复杂行为传染模型揭示了传染病动态中反直觉的现象：在某些条件下，更严重的疫情反而可能通过触发广泛的行为改变而导致疾病消除。这强调了在流行病模型中考虑非线性行为动态的重要性，并为公共卫生干预提供了新的见解。

Abstract: Infectious disease transmission in human populations has a complex two-way interaction with changes in host behaviour. It is increasingly recognised that incorporating adaptive behavioural change into epidemic models is important for improving understanding of infectious disease dynamics and developing policy-relevant modelling tools. An important aspect of behavioural dynamics is social contagion, where people tend to adopt behaviours exhibited by others around them. In a simple behavioural contagion model, the behaviour uptake rate increases linearly with the number of contacts who have adopted a given behaviour. Here, we explore an epidemic model with complex behavioural contagion, where the behaviour uptake rate is a nonlinear function of the number of behaving contacts. We identify key bifurcation parameters of the model, which include the basic reproduction number $R_0$, the strength of the behavioural effect on disease transmission, and the speed of behaviour uptake relative to behaviour abandonment. We show that, in some regions of parameter space, the model has multiple disease-free equilibria. In this situation, the occurrence of an epidemic in a population with an initially low level of behaviour practice can trigger a self-sustaining increase in behaviour, which then causes the disease to be eliminated. In some cases, while moderate values of $R_0$ lead to the disease becoming endemic, higher values of $R_0$ may lead to behaviour-driven disease elimination. We demonstrate that this mechanism of epidemic-triggered uptake of behaviour leading to disease elimination can occur in the presence and absence of temporary post-infection immunity.

### [4] [Neutralization titers reveal the structure of polyclonal antibody responses](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.11451)
*Henry Alston,Thierry Mora,Aleksandra M. Walczak*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 该研究提出通过中和滴度的统计特性来预测多克隆抗体反应的组成，避免了测序和单克隆抗体表达的技术挑战。研究发现流感抗体反应可分为集体现象（多个抗体贡献）或少数强结合体主导，后者导致滴度呈极值理论中的Gumbel分布。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 多克隆抗体反应的组成难以通过实验测量，但包含关于免疫稳健性的重要信息。传统方法如测序和单克隆抗体表达面临技术挑战，需要新的方法来量化分析抗体反应组成。

Method: 使用中和滴度的统计特性进行分析，应用极值理论中的Gumbel分布来描述滴度分布。提出平衡结合模型来定量捕捉滴度数据，并揭示多克隆反应的结构。

Result: 研究发现流感抗体反应可分为两种模式：集体现象（多个抗体贡献）或少数强结合体主导。Gumbel统计能准确描述免疫挑战前个体的滴度分布。平衡结合模型能定量捕捉滴度数据。

Conclusion: 仅通过中和滴度的统计特性就能预测多克隆抗体反应的组成，避免了传统方法的挑战。该方法可推广到其他病原体的免疫反应分析，为理解免疫反应结构提供了新途径。

Abstract: The composition of a polyclonal antibody response is hard to measure experimentally but contains vital information about the robustness of immunity. Here, we argue that the statistics of neutralization titers alone can be used to make quantitative predictions about the composition of the response, circumventing challenges arising through sequencing and monoclonal antibody expression. We show that the response against influenza within a cohort can be either driven by a collective phenomenon where many antibodies contribute to neutralization, or dominated by just a few strong binders, leading to a broad distribution of titers across individuals described by a Gumbel distribution from extreme value theory. Comparing titers across cohorts, we find that Gumbel statistics {accurately describe} individuals prior to an immune challenge. We propose an equilibrium binding model that quantitatively captures titer data and illustrates the structure of the polyclonal response. Our approach extends generically to immune responses to other pathogens.

### [6] [The origin of the genetic code is encrypted in the structure of present-day transfer RNAs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.11696)
*Jacques H. Daniel*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 通过比较枯草芽孢杆菌tRNA的一级结构构建系统发育树，发现tRNA之间存在亲缘关系并按特定时间顺序出现，这很可能反映了氨基酸进入通用密码子表的时间顺序。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 遗传密码的起源是理解生命如何从化学世界诞生的关键问题。自60年前遗传密码被破译以来，仍缺乏关于其出现的通用理论。作者希望通过分析tRNA这一翻译系统的关键组成部分，为遗传密码起源提供新的见解。

Method: 作者假设tRNA可能是遗传密码起源的关键元素，因此比较了枯草芽孢杆菌（Bacillus subtilis）中tRNA的一级结构，并基于这些比较构建了系统发育树。

Result: tRNA一级结构的比较产生了系统发育树，显示所有tRNA都是相关的，并按精确的时间顺序逐渐出现。分析该tRNA树的各种特征表明，它很可能反映了氨基酸进入通用密码子表的时间顺序。

Conclusion: 这些结果强烈表明tRNA实体确实是遗传密码形成的主要组成部分，并为生命初期各种氨基酸按时间顺序"殖民"通用密码子表提供了可能的场景。这些数据支持作者提出的遗传密码起源的通用理论——多tRNA理论。

Abstract: Background/ Objectives: Resolving the origin of the genetic code is fundamental to understanding how life began its journey out of the chemical world. Since its deciphering some 60 years ago, there is still no general theory of the emergence of the genetic code. My objectives are to bring some unique data that might provide some insight into this particular issue. Methods: Because tRNA (transfer RNA) constitutes a crucial piece of the present translational system, having unique structural characteristics, I hypothesized that they might constitute the key elements at the origin of the genetic code and thus decided to compare the primary structure of the tRNAs from a bacterium, Bacillus subtilis. Results: The comparison of the primary structure of the tRNAs from Bacillus subtilis generated a genealogical tree, meaning that the tRNAs were all related and appeared gradually in a precise time sequence. Remarkably, analysis of the various characteristics of this tRNAs tree showed that it very likely reflects the time of entry of amino acids into the Universal Codon Table. Conclusions: These results strongly suggest that the tRNA entity was indeed a major component in the formation of the genetic code and, further, provide a likely scenario for the time sequence of codon colonization of the Universal Codon Table by the various amino acids at the very beginning of life. Also, these data are interpreted in terms of a general theory of the origin of the genetic code I propose, the poly-tRNA theory.
