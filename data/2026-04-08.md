### [1] [Mathematical Models of Evolution and Replicator Systems Dynamics. Chapter 1: Introduction to Replicator Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.05720)
*A. S. Bratus,S. Drozhzhin,T. Yakushkina*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本章概述了复制子系统数学理论的基础结果，为广义达尔文主义框架下的进化过程提供统一数学形式化，适用于任何具有遗传、变异和选择的系统。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为广义达尔文主义提供一个统一的数学框架，使进化过程的数学形式化能够应用于任何具有遗传、变异和选择机制的系统，而不局限于特定的生物基质。

Method: 从相互作用的种群的Kolmogorov方程出发，推导出复制子方程，并研究三种典型机制：独立复制、自催化复制和超循环复制。然后考察准种框架（Eigen和Crow-Kimura模型），包括平衡点的全局稳定性、序列空间结构和误差阈值现象。

Result: 超循环被证明是永久的且能内在地携带进化变异性。准种框架展示了平衡点的全局稳定性、序列空间结构以及误差阈值现象等数学特性。

Conclusion: 重点在于揭示这些模型背后的数学结构而非生物学细节，目标是使该框架能够应用于超出原始分子生物学背景的抽象进化动力学。

Abstract: This chapter is an overview of foundational results in the mathematical theory of replicator systems. Its primary aim is to provide a unified framework for the mathematical formalisation of evolutionary processes in the spirit of generalised Darwinism -- that is, for any system in which heredity, variability, and selection can be meaningfully defined, regardless of the specific biological substrate. Starting from the Kolmogorov equations for interacting populations, we derive the replicator equation and examine three canonical regimes: independent, autocatalytic, and hypercyclic replication. The hypercycle is shown to be permanent and to carry evolutionary variability intrinsically. We then survey the quasispecies framework -- the Eigen and Crow--Kimura models -- covering global stability of equilibria, sequence space structure, and the error-threshold phenomenon. Throughout, the emphasis is on the mathematical structures that underlie these models rather than on biological detail, with the goal of making the framework applicable to abstract evolutionary dynamics beyond its original molecular biology context.

*Xinhong Xu,Yimeng Zhang,Qichen Qian,Yuanlong Zhang*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: CalM是一个基于钙信号轨迹的自监督神经基础模型，通过双轴自回归变换器建模神经和时间依赖关系，可适应多种下游任务


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的大规模多动物建模方法对功能钙信号轨迹分析仍局限于特定任务，缺乏跨常见神经科学目标的通用性

Method: 提出CalM自监督基础模型，包含高性能分词器将单神经元轨迹映射到共享离散词汇表，以及双轴自回归变换器同时建模神经轴和时间轴的依赖关系

Result: 在神经群体动态预测任务中超越专用基线；通过任务特定头部适应行为解码任务，优于监督解码模型；线性分析显示模型表示具有可解释功能结构

Conclusion: 提出了一种基于钙信号轨迹的新型有效自监督预训练范式，为功能神经分析的可扩展预训练和广泛应用铺平道路

Abstract: Recent work suggests that large-scale, multi-animal modeling can significantly improve neural recording analysis. However, for functional calcium traces, existing approaches remain task-specific, limiting transfer across common neuroscience objectives. To address this challenge, we propose \textbf{CalM}, a self-supervised neural foundation model trained solely on neuronal calcium traces and adaptable to multiple downstream tasks, including forecasting and decoding. Our key contribution is a pretraining framework, composed of a high-performance tokenizer mapping single-neuron traces into a shared discrete vocabulary, and a dual-axis autoregressive transformer modeling dependencies along both the neural and the temporal axis. We evaluate CalM on a large-scale, multi-animal, multi-session dataset. On the neural population dynamics forecasting task, CalM outperforms strong specialized baselines after pretraining. With a task-specific head, CalM further adapts to the behavior decoding task and achieves superior results compared with supervised decoding models. Moreover, linear analyses of CalM representations reveal interpretable functional structures beyond predictive accuracy. Taken together, we propose a novel and effective self-supervised pretraining paradigm for foundation models based on calcium traces, paving the way for scalable pretraining and broad applications in functional neural analysis. Code will be released soon.

### [3] [An Imbalanced Dataset with Multiple Feature Representations for Studying Quality Control of Next-Generation Sequencing](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.04981)
*Philipp Röchner,Clarissa Krämer,Johannes U Mayer,Franz Rothlauf,Steffen Albrecht,Maximilian Sprang*

Main category: q-bio.GN

TL;DR: 研究人员创建了一个包含37,491个NGS样本的数据集，提供两种质量相关特征表示，用于开发自动质量控制工具。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有NGS存储库提供的质量相关特征有限，难以开发自动质量控制工具，需要包含质量问题特征的数据集。

Method: 从37,491个NGS样本中提取两种特征表示：1) 34个质量控制工具特征(QC-34)；2) 从ENCODE黑名单问题区域读取计数中提取的8-1,183个可变特征(BL特征)。所有特征来自人类和小鼠的五个基因组检测样本，并包含基于自动质量控制和领域专家评估的二元质量标签。

Result: 3.2%的样本被标记为低质量。监督机器学习算法能够准确从特征中预测质量标签，证实了所提供特征表示的相关性。

Conclusion: 提出的特征表示使研究人员能够研究不同特征类型(QC-34 vs. BL特征)和粒度(可变数量的BL特征)对质量问题检测的影响，为开发NGS数据自动质量控制工具提供了有价值的资源。

Abstract: Next-generation sequencing (NGS) is a key technique for studying the DNA and RNA of organisms. However, identifying quality problems in NGS data across different experimental settings remains challenging. To develop automated quality-control tools, researchers require datasets with features that capture the characteristics of quality problems. Existing NGS repositories, however, offer only a limited number of quality-related features. To address this gap, we propose a dataset derived from 37.491 NGS samples with two types of quality-related feature representations. The first type consists of 34 features derived from quality control tools (QC-34 features). The second type has a variable number of features ranging from eight to 1.183. These features were derived from read counts in problematic genomic regions identified by the ENCODE blocklist (BL features). All features describe the same human and mouse samples from five genomic assays, allowing direct comparison of feature representations. The proposed dataset includes a binary quality label, derived from automated quality control and domain experts. Among all samples, $3.2\%$ are of low quality. Supervised machine learning algorithms accurately predicted quality labels from the features, confirming the relevance of the provided feature representations. The proposed feature representations enable researchers to study how different feature types (QC-34 vs. BL features) and granularities (varying number of BL features) affect the detection of quality problems.

TL;DR: 系统评估了9种转录组学免疫检查点抑制剂响应预测模型，发现它们在独立数据集上的预测性能普遍有限，跨队列泛化能力不足，生物学一致性较差。

Pathway-level analyses revealed sparse and inconsistent biomarker signals across models. Although scRNA-seq-based predictors converged on immune-related programs such as allograft rejection, bulk RNA-seq-based models exhibited little reproducible overlap. ### [5] [GenomeQA: Benchmarking General Large Language Models for Genome Sequence Understanding](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.05774)
*Weicai Long,Yusen Hou,Junning Feng,Houcheng Su,Shuo Yang,Donglin Xie,Yanlin Zhang*

Main category: q-bio.GN

TL;DR: GenomeQA是一个用于评估通用大语言模型在原始基因组序列推理任务上表现的基准测试，包含5,200个样本，涵盖6个任务家族，序列长度6-1000bp。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有基准测试要么专注于专门训练的DNA模型，要么仅通过文本问题评估生物学知识，缺乏对通用大语言模型直接处理原始基因组序列行为的系统评估。

Method: 开发了GenomeQA基准测试，包含5,200个来自多个生物数据库的样本，序列长度6-1000bp，涵盖增强子识别、启动子识别、剪接位点识别、分类学分类、组蛋白标记预测、转录因子结合位点预测和TF基序预测等6个任务家族。

Result: 在6个前沿大语言模型上的评估显示：模型始终优于随机基线，能够利用局部序列信号如GC含量和短基序，但在需要间接或多步序列模式推理的任务上性能下降。

Conclusion: GenomeQA为研究和改进通用大语言模型在原始基因组序列上的应用建立了诊断性基准，揭示了模型在序列推理任务上的优势和局限性。

Abstract: Large Language Models (LLMs) are increasingly adopted as conversational assistants in genomics, where they are mainly used to reason over biological knowledge, annotations, and analysis outputs through natural language interfaces. However, existing benchmarks either focus on specialized DNA models trained for sequence prediction or evaluate biological knowledge using text-only questions, leaving the behavior of general-purpose LLMs when directly exposed to raw genome sequences underexplored. We introduce GenomeQA, a benchmark designed to provide a controlled evaluation setting for general-purpose LLMs on sequence-based genome inference tasks. GenomeQA comprises 5,200 samples drawn from multiple biological databases, with sequence lengths ranging from 6 to 1,000 base pairs (bp), spanning six task families: Enhancer and Promoter Identification, Splice Site Identification, Taxonomic Classification, Histone Mark Prediction, Transcription Factor Binding Site Prediction, and TF Motif Prediction. Across six frontier LLMs, we find that models consistently outperform random baselines and can exploit local sequence signals such as GC content and short motifs, while performance degrades on tasks that require more indirect or multi-step inference over sequence patterns. GenomeQA establishes a diagnostic benchmark for studying and improving the use of general-purpose LLMs on raw genomic sequences.
