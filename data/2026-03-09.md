### [1] [In-batch Relational Features Enhance Precision in An Unsupervised Medical Anomaly Detection Task](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05534)
*P. Bilha Githinji,Xi Yuan,Ijaz Gul,Lian Zhang,Jinhao Xu,Zhenglin Chen,Peiwu Qin,Dongmei Yu*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 提出一种基于超图估计和图卷积的增强潜在表示方法，用于无监督医学图像异常检测，通过整合正常队列的上下文相似性来区分病理变化与正常解剖变异，降低假阳性率。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在无监督医学图像异常检测中，将正常解剖变异与病理变化混淆是一个主要挑战，导致大量假阳性。需要更好地整合健康变异信息来提高检测准确性。

Method: 通过批处理超图估计和共享权重的图卷积层，增强CNN自编码器的潜在表示，整合正常队列内的上下文相似性，生成群体感知的嵌入表示。

Result: 在异质性脑肿瘤2D MRI数据集上，该方法提高了健康与病理样本的可分离性：AUC-ROC达到0.90（95% CI 0.84-0.95，绝对增益5.7%），平均精度绝对提升16%（0.78 AP，95% CI 0.66-0.89），显著降低了假阳性率。

Conclusion: 该方法通过整合正常队列的上下文信息有效区分病理与正常变异，且异常检测性能随小批量上下文规模增加而提升，为整合健康变异提供了可调节的机制。

Abstract: Confounding pathology with normal anatomical variation remains a significant challenge in unsupervised medical-image anomaly detection, resulting in numerous false positives. To enhance integration of healthy variation, we augment the latent representation of a CNN autoencoder with contextual similarities within a normal cohort through batch-wise hypergraph estimation and a shared-weights graph convolution layer, producing a population-aware embedding. On a heterogeneous brain-tumor dataset of 2D MRI scans, the method improves separability between healthy and pathological samples, achieving an AUC-ROC of 0.90 (95% CI 0.84-0.95, 5.7% absolute gain), and a 16% absolute improvement in average precision (0.78 AP, 95% CI 0.66-0.89), thereby lowering false-positive rates. Moreover, both anomaly detection and downstream tumor versus no-tumor classification performance improve with the size of the mini-batch context captured in the augmented representation, suggesting a tunable lever for integrating healthy variation.

### [2] [Multicellular Tumour Spheroids Exposure to Pulsed Electric Field: A Combined Experimental and Mathematical Modelling Study Highlighting Temporal Dynamics of DAMP Release and Accelerated Regrowth at Intermediate Field Intensities](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06087)
*Emma Leschiera,Nicolas Mattei,Marie-Pierre Rols,Muriel Golzio,Jelena Kolosnjaj-Tabi,Clair Poignard*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 该研究结合体外实验和计算模型，探索电穿孔对肿瘤球体再生和免疫原性细胞死亡的影响，发现脉冲强度决定细胞死亡和DAMP释放程度，静止细胞在肿瘤再生中起双重作用。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 电穿孔作为肿瘤消融技术虽然有效，但肿瘤再生仍可能发生。同时，体外研究显示电穿孔可通过释放DAMP触发免疫原性细胞死亡，可能对抗肿瘤再生。需要解决两个关键问题：肿瘤再生的动态机制，以及ICD在三维细胞结构中的时空展开。

Method: 结合体外实验和混合个体/连续计算模型。实验量化肿瘤球体活力、生长速率以及ATP和HMGB1的释放。计算模型预测增殖细胞、静止细胞和坏死细胞的动态变化。

Result: 细胞死亡和DAMP释放与脉冲强度呈正相关。模型验证显示静止细胞具有双重作用：部分死亡释放空间和资源，部分存活并恢复增殖。肿瘤球体命运取决于脉冲强度。

Conclusion: 研究阐明了电穿孔后肿瘤再生的动态机制，强调了在设计电穿孔治疗时考虑静止细胞的重要性，为优化治疗策略提供了理论基础。

Abstract: Electroporation is increasingly used as a percutaneous ablation technique for tumours located near vital structures. Although effective, tumour regrowth may still occur. At the same time, in vitro studies on cell monolayers have shown that electroporation can trigger immunogenic cell death (ICD) through the release of damage-associated molecular patterns (DAMPs). These molecules can stimulate the immune system and could counteract tumour regrowth. To fully exploit electroporation, two key questions must be addressed: (1) what dynamics drive tumour regrowth, and (2) how ICD unfolds in space and time within three-dimensional cellular structures, which better mimic in vivo conditions than 2D cultures. Here, we combine in vitro experiments with a hybrid individual-based/continuous computational model to explore tumour spheroid regrowth and ICD potential under different pulse intensities. Experiments quantify spheroid viability, growth rate, and the release of ATP and HMGB1. In parallel, the hybrid model predicts the dynamics of proliferative, quiescent, and necrotic cells. Both approaches show that cell death and DAMP release scale with pulse intensity. The model, validated against experimental data, further highlights the dual role of quiescent cells: some die and free space and resources, while others survive and resume proliferation. Together, these findings demonstrate how spheroid fate depends on pulse strength and emphasize the importance of accounting for quiescent cells when designing electroporation-based therapies.

### [3] [The Impact of Neglecting Vaccine Unwillingness in Epidemiology Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05626)
*Glenn Ledder*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 研究探讨了在流行病学模型中如何更真实地纳入疫苗拒绝人群的影响，分析了忽略疫苗不情愿性对模型预测的误差程度，并评估了通过调整疫苗接种率常数是否能减少这种误差。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在许多社会中，有相当比例的人口拒绝接种疫苗，因此需要重新考虑如何在流行病学模型中更真实地纳入疫苗接种因素。传统模型通常将疫苗接种率应用于整个易感人群，而不是更现实地假设疫苗接种率函数应仅依赖于愿意且能够接种疫苗的易感人群。

Method: 使用一个简单的通用疾病模型来研究两个问题：1）忽略疫苗不情愿性对关键模型结果引入多少误差？2）通过将疫苗不情愿性纳入疫苗接种率常数而非速率图中，是否能减少这种误差？研究分析了两种时间尺度：地方性时间尺度（使用平衡点分析研究长期行为）和流行病时间尺度（使用数值模拟研究第一波大流行）。

Result: 误差程度在很大程度上取决于所关注的时间尺度。对于地方性时间尺度（研究长期平衡状态），忽略疫苗不情愿性会导致较大误差，且通过降低疫苗接种率常数无法改善这种误差。对于流行病时间尺度（研究第一波大流行），误差仍然显著，特别是对于传染性相对较低的疾病和接种速度相对较慢的疫苗接种计划。

Conclusion: 在流行病学模型中准确纳入疫苗不情愿性对于获得可靠的预测结果至关重要，特别是在研究长期平衡状态时。对于第一波大流行的预测，也需要考虑疫苗不情愿性，特别是对于传染性较低且接种速度较慢的情况。传统模型假设可能引入显著误差，需要更现实的建模方法。

Abstract: With significant population fractions in many societies who refuse vaccines, it is important to reconsider how vaccination is incorporated into compartmental epidemiology models. It is still most common to apply the vaccination rate to the entire class of susceptibles, rather than to use the more realistic assumption that the vaccination rate function should depend only on the population of susceptibles who are willing and able to receive a vaccination. This study uses a simple generic disease model to address two questions: (1) How much error is introduced in key model outcomes by neglecting vaccine unwillingness?, and (2) Can the error be reduced by incorporating vaccine unwillingness into the vaccination rate constant rather than the rate diagram? The answers depend greatly on the time scale of interest. For the endemic time scale, where longterm behavior is studied with equilibrium point analysis, the error in neglecting unwillingess is large and cannot be improved upon by decreasing the vaccination rate constant. For the epidemic time scale, where the first big epidemic wave is studied with numerical simulations, the error can still be significant, particularly for diseases that are relatively less infectious and vaccination programs that are relatively slow.

</details>
