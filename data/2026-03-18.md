### [1] [TPMM: Three-component Posterior Mixture Model Enables Robust Inverton Detection in Low-Depth Metagenomes and Suggests Potential Viral Invertons](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16194)
*Yi Lu,Jiaojiao Guan,Yang Shen,Jiayu Shang,Yanni Sun*

Main category: q-bio.GN

TL;DR: TPMM是一种用于宏基因组数据中inverton检测的三组分后验混合模型，通过概率方法解决低测序深度下的检测难题，相比现有方法在稀疏数据条件下表现更优。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 细菌相变通过DNA倒位（invertons）实现可逆表型切换，宏基因组测序为全面研究invertons提供了平台，但在低测序深度下，现有计算方法面临挑战：硬性读段支持阈值可能遗漏真实事件，而仅基于序列的预测器缺乏读段支持的可解释性和不确定性量化。

Method: TPMM是一个三组分后验混合模型，明确纳入测序深度，将inverton检测构建为概率混合问题。从被反向重复序列侧翼的候选区域开始，模型使用读段证据将候选分类为噪声、低概率或高概率倒位信号。最后，TPMM分配后验概率作为软标签，并应用累积贝叶斯错误发现率控制来稳健识别真实invertons。

Result: 在两个真实肠道宏基因组数据集上，TPMM在高测序深度下与PhaseFinder结果一致，但在系统性降采样下恢复了显著更多的invertons，在稀疏数据条件下表现出优越性能。研究还检查了病毒基因组中潜在的可逆倒位元件，并提供了支持性分析。

Conclusion: TPMM为宏基因组数据中的inverton检测提供了一种概率框架，特别适用于低测序深度条件，能够更全面地识别倒位介导的调控元件，扩展了倒位介导调控的研究范围。

Abstract: Bacterial phase variation enables reversible, locus-specific phenotypic switching, often driven by DNA inversion (invertons). To identify these events, researchers commonly rely on sequencing reads that provide orientation-specific support. Metagenomic sequencing, which captures total genetic material independent of cultivation, offers a powerful platform for the comprehensive study of invertons. However, computational inverton calling from metagenomic data is difficult at low sequencing depth: hard read-support cutoffs can miss true events, while sequence-only predictors lack read-backed interpretability and uncertainty quantification. To address this, we present TPMM, a three-component posterior mixture model for inverton calling in metagenomic data. TPMM explicitly incorporates sequencing depth to formulate inverton detection as a probabilistic mixture problem. Starting from candidates flanked by inverted repeats, the model classifies the candidates into noise, low-probability, or high-probability inversion signals using read evidence. Finally, TPMM assigns posterior probabilities as soft labels and applies cumulative Bayesian False Discovery Rate control to robustly identify true invertons. On two real gut metagenomic datasets, TPMM agrees well with PhaseFinder at high depth but recovers substantially more invertons under systematic downsampling, demonstrating superior performance in sparse-data regimes. We further examine potential reversible inversion elements in viral genomes and provide supporting analyses, suggesting a broader scope for inversion-mediated regulation.

### [2] [Whole slide and microscopy image analysis with QuPath and OMERO](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.15702)
*Léo Leplat,Alan O'Callaghan,Peter Bankhead*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: QuPath扩展支持从OMERO服务器远程访问图像像素和元数据，突破本地文件系统限制，为大型研究提供支持


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: QuPath作为桌面生物图像分析软件，虽然灵活易用，但仅依赖本地文件系统存储图像，限制了其在大型研究中的应用

Method: 开发了一个新的QuPath扩展，使其能够从OMERO服务器访问图像像素和元数据，实现远程图像处理

Result: 该扩展增强了QuPath的功能，使其能够高效处理远程存储的图像，同时为开发者连接QuPath到其他图像管理系统提供了模板

Conclusion: 通过OMERO服务器集成，QuPath突破了本地文件系统的限制，支持更大规模的研究，并为未来与其他图像管理系统的集成奠定了基础

Abstract: QuPath is open-source software for bioimage analysis. As a desktop application that is flexible and easy to install, QuPath is used by labs worldwide to visualise and analyse large and complex images. However, relying only on images stored only on a local file system limits QuPath's use for larger studies.
  This paper describes a new extension that enables QuPath to access pixels and metadata from an OMERO server. This enhances the software by allowing it to work efficiently with images stored remotely, while also serving as a template for developers who want to connect QuPath to other image management systems.

### [3] [Evaluating Targeted Mobility Restrictions on COVID-19 Transmission in Seoul: A Metapopulation Modeling Study Using Mobile Phone Data](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16064)
*Yuna Lim,Jonggul Lee,Eunok Jung*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 该研究开发了首尔25个区的年龄结构化SEIR元种群模型，结合手机移动数据，分析按年龄和目的分层的流动性限制策略，发现针对20-59岁成人工作/学校流动性的限制效果最佳。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 广泛流动性限制有助于控制传染病传播，但其社会经济成本高昂，且传播风险因移动目的、年龄组和空间连通性而异，需要更有针对性的方法。

Method: 开发了首尔25个区的年龄结构化SEIR元种群模型，整合手机来源-目的地数据，按年龄（0-19、20-59、60+）和目的（居住、学校/工作、其他非日常）分层分析流动性，使用2024年流动性数据作为基线，结合2020-2021年疫情期间的流动性偏差，研究不同目标场景下的反事实策略。

Result: 针对20-59岁成人工作/学校流动性的限制产生最高的人均感染减少效果；基于人口调整的工作流入量的空间聚类显示，高流入量的中央商务区对应模拟中发现的快速传播区；针对该聚类内和进入该聚类的工作流动限制在不同感染源位置下均能减少疫情规模；工作日包含的时间表优于仅周末限制。

Conclusion: 虽然全市范围的限制能实现更大的减少效果，但战略性地针对20-59岁成人在快速传播聚类内的日常学校/工作流动性，可以在减少更广泛社会经济干扰的同时提供显著的流行病学效益。

Abstract: Broad mobility restrictions can help control infectious disease spread, but their socioeconomic costs and the variation in transmission risks by mobility purpose, age group, and spatial connectivity highlight the need for targeted approaches. In this study, we developed an age-structured SEIR metapopulation model for COVID-19 across Seoul's 25 districts, integrating mobile phone-derived origin-destination data. We stratified mobility by age (0-19, 20-59, 60+) and purpose: residential (H), school/work (W), and other non-routine (O). Using 2024 mobility data as a baseline and incorporating pandemic-period (2020-2021) mobility deviations, we investigated counterfactual strategies under various targeting scenarios. Our results showed that W restrictions among adults aged 20-59 produced the highest per-capita reductions in infection. Spatial clustering based on population-adjusted W inflows showed that high-inflow central business districts corresponded to the fast-spreading districts identified in the simulations. Targeting W flows into and within this cluster consistently reduced epidemic size across uncertain seeding locations. Furthermore, weekday-inclusive schedules outperformed weekend-only restrictions. Overall, our findings suggest that although citywide restrictions achieve larger reductions, strategically targeting routine school/work mobility among adults aged 20-59 within fast-spreading clusters can provide substantial epidemiological benefits while reducing broader socioeconomic disruption.

### [4] [Age-dependent distribution of officially reported cases of vector-borne infections](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16773)
*Francisco Antonio Bezerra Coutinho,Marcos Amaku,Esper Georges Kallas,Eduardo Massad*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 该研究提出了一种分析媒介传播传染病年龄分布的新方法，发现伊蚊传播疾病（登革热、寨卡、基孔肯雅热）的病例年龄分布模式具有一致性，而其他媒介传播疾病则呈现不同模式。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究旨在开发一种分析媒介传播传染病年龄分布的新方法，以理解不同传染病在人群中的年龄分布模式及其与媒介生态学的关系。

Method: 使用官方报告的登革热、寨卡、基孔肯雅热、疟疾和利什曼病病例数据，将原始数据转换为年龄依赖的密度分布，并用特殊连续函数进行拟合分析。

Result: 发现伊蚊传播的三种传染病（登革热、寨卡、基孔肯雅热）的病例年龄分布模式基本一致，不受疫情规模、地理区域和时间影响；而其他媒介传播疾病的年龄分布则完全不同。

Conclusion: 伊蚊传播传染病的病例年龄分布模式具有普遍性，主要取决于媒介与宿主之间的生态相互作用，这一发现有助于理解传染病传播动力学和制定针对性防控策略。

Abstract: OBJECTIVE: To propose a new approach to analyze the age-distribution of reported cases for vector-transmitted infections. METHODS: Using officially reported number of cases of dengue, Zika, chikungunya, malaria and leishmaniasis for distinct geographical areas, in different periods. Data were treated in special but well-known procedure, transforming the raw data into a density age-dependent distribution and fitting a special continuous function to it. RESULTS: We found that the proportion of age-dependent cases with respect to the total number of cases in a given year (or any transmission season) is probably determined by the ecological interactions between vectors and hosts. The age-distribution of the proportion of cases for the three Aedes-related infections are essentially the same independently of the magnitude of the outbreak and the geographical region considered. On the other hand, for the infections transmitted by other vectors, the age-distributions of the proportion of cases are entirely different. CONCLUSIONS: During specific outbreaks, the ratio between the age distribution of the proportion of officially reported cases and the total number of cases for Aedes transmitted infections such as dengue, chikungunya and zika is independent of the size of the outbreak, the size of the studied population, the period when the outbreak occurs; and the geographical region considered. Our results also suggest that the age-distribution of cases is mainly due to the interaction between vectors and their hosts.

</details>
