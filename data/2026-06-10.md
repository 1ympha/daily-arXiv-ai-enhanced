### [1] [Adjusted trajectory of medication exposure taking into account the periodicity of dispensations and the number of dispensed packs and comparative analysis on EFEMERIS database](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.09952)
*Cécile Chouquet,Anna-Belle Beau,Christine Damase-Michel,David Jeauneau,Isabelle Lacroix,Sabine Mercier*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 提出了一种基于药包数量和配药类型（偶发性或规律性）的药物暴露轨迹调整计算方法，并通过EFEMERIS数据验证其对DDD数量、个体轨迹变化及聚类质量的影响。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的药物暴露轨迹计算方法未充分考虑药包数量和配药频率，可能导致暴露评估偏差，进而影响后续统计分析及临床结论的可靠性。

Method: 基于EFEMERIS数据，设计了三种不同场景的轨迹计算方案（是否考虑药包数量及配药周期），通过全局DDD指标、个体轨迹变化分析及四组聚类结果比较，评估调整方法的有效性。

Result: 65%的轨迹保持不变；其余轨迹在DDD数量和/或个体暴露特征上出现显著变化；4%的轨迹被归入不同聚类，且调整后的聚类质量更优；高剂量精神药物暴露组的新生儿病理发生率更高。

Conclusion: 调整方法能更准确地反映真实暴露情况，提升聚类质量，并在高剂量精神药物暴露与新生儿病理之间的关联分析中得出更可靠的结论，支持既往研究的发现。

Abstract: We presented an adjustment method for the calculation of medication exposure trajectories based on the number of dispensed packs and the type of dispensations (occasional or regular). A comparative study based on the EFEMERIS data was carried out using three different scenarios of trajectory calculation depending on whether or not the number of packs and the periodicity of medication dispensations were taken into account. The impact of the scenario was highlighted using global indicators on the number of Define-Daily Dose (DDD) on all women exposed; the study of changes in individual trajectories from one scenario to another was carried out; we also compared the results of a clustering into four groups. If 65% of the trajectories remained unchanged, we could observe on the rest significant changes in number of DDD and/or on individual exposure profile. We observed 4% of trajectories that were attributed to a different cluster, and the clustering was of better quality with the adjustment method. Depending on the study context, an impact on cluster distribution could be observed for some maternal characteristics and neonatal outcomes. This was the case for a higher occurrence of neonatal pathology for neonates from mothers belonging to the cluster with high doses of psychotropics, thus reinforcing the conclusions of previous studies of a link between high exposure to psychotropic medications and presence of pathology for the newborn.

### [2] [Spatial Model Selection and Uncertainty Quantification: Comparing Continuous and Discrete Wound Healing Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10873)
*John T. Nardini,Jana L. Gevertz*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种模型选择流程，用于在空间过程建模中比较偏微分方程（PDE）和基于个体的模型（ABM），发现PDE在计算速度上快1000倍以上，且在信息准则和数据预测中常被优先选择。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有数据驱动建模任务中，模型模态（如PDE与ABM）的选择缺乏明确指导原则，学者们常凭直觉选择模型类型。

Method: 构建了基于近似贝叶斯计算的模型选择流程，整合参数估计、不确定性量化和模型选择（信息准则+样本外预测），在人工数据和真实伤口愈合数据上测试。

Result: ABM与PDE参数估计性能相当，但ABM不确定性更高；PDE计算速度快1000倍以上；平均场PDE常被误选为真实生成模型；真实数据中带细胞牵引和时间延迟的PDE最合适但参数不确定性高。

Conclusion: 该流程为空间生物学数据选择建模模态提供了初步框架，揭示出计算效率与模型复杂性之间的权衡。

Abstract: All data-driven modeling tasks (e.g., parameter estimation, uncertainty quantification, and data forecasting) require the selection of a mathematical model. An overlooked aspect of model selection is modality; for example, there are no guidelines on when to use a partial differential equation (PDE) model or an agent-based model (ABM) for spatial processes. To address this, we created a model selection pipeline that uses approximate Bayesian computations to perform parameter estimation, uncertainty quantification, and model selection (using both information criteria and out-of-sample forecasting). Applying the pipeline to artificial datasets (generated from ABMs) reveals that while both modalities yield comparable parameter estimation performance, the ABM estimates exhibit higher uncertainty, and the PDE models compute more than 1,000$\times$ faster. Surprisingly, the mean-field PDE is often selected over the true generative ABM model using both information criteria and data forecasting. Applying the pipeline to public wound healing data indicates that a PDE model with cell pulling and a time delay is the most appropriate model for this data, however, this model has high levels of parametric uncertainty. This methodology establishes a preliminary framework for selecting the appropriate modeling modality for spatial biological data.

### [3] [Modeling pest dynamics in trap cropping to improve yield: the effects of attraction, retention, and land allocation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10605)
*Matthew H Holden*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文通过产量最大化模型证明，诱捕作物的有效性取决于害虫对诱捕植物的吸引强度及其离开频率。当诱捕植物的害虫扩散率降至经济作物的四分之一时，所需诱捕面积可从20-30%降至约5%，从而使诱捕作物从不可行变为可行。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 诱捕作物通过吸引害虫保护主作物，但害虫从诱捕作物反向扩散回主作物会削弱保护效果。本文旨在量化这一反向扩散的影响，并寻找使诱捕作物在商业规模下切实可行的条件。

Method: 建立了一个简单的产量最大化模型，综合考虑害虫对诱捕植物的吸引力、害虫从诱捕植物离开的扩散率、以及诱捕作物占用土地的比例三个关键因素。

Result: 当诱捕植物与主作物的害虫扩散率相同时，最优诱捕面积需达20-30%， growers难以接受；但当诱捕植物的害虫扩散率降至主作物的四分之一时，最优面积降至约5%。

Conclusion: 有效控制害虫从诱捕作物的反向扩散是诱捕策略成败的关键，这为从植物选择到针对性干预措施的诱捕作物设计提供了指导。

Abstract: Trap crops reduce damage to a cash (main) crop by attracting pests away from it. Yet this protection is weakened when pests disperse back into the cash crop. In this paper, we focus on the importance of preventing this backflow, showing that effective trap cropping depends jointly on how strongly pests are attracted to trap plants and how rarely they leave them. Together with the proportion of the field devoted to trap plants, these processes determine both the efficacy and feasibility of trap cropping at commercial scales. We formalise this relationship using a simple yield-maximisation framework, in which growers weigh pest suppression benefits against the land sacrificed to trap plants. The model shows that when dispersal from trap plants equals that from the cash crop, optimal trap coverage can exceed 20 to 30 percent of the landscape, levels rarely acceptable to growers. However, reducing pest dispersal off trap plants to just one-quarter of cash crop dispersal lowers the optimal required trap area to approximately 5 percent, transforming trap cropping from impractical to feasible. Understanding these relationships can guide trap-cropping design, from plant choice to targeted interventions that reduce pest movement, to minimise damage, maximise yield, and make trap cropping a reliable component of sustainable pest management.

### [4] [Chaos and stability in the marine trophic network: the importance of interactions over complexity](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10809)
*Ilaria Cunico,Guido Occhipinti,Gregor Fussmann,Paolo Lazzari*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本研究通过数值模拟探讨了包含微生物环的复杂海洋营养网络动力学，发现较长的营养链和更多的消费者增加系统混沌性，而杂食性相互作用促进稳定性。交互作用和反馈而非复杂性是稳定性的关键驱动因素，且许多配置表现出高比例的混沌行为。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现实世界复杂网络的动力学对于评估其可预测性、韧性以及改善生态系统管理至关重要，特别是在气候变化背景下。生态网络中稳定性与复杂性之间的关系在文献中仍存在争议，因此需要深入研究。

Method: 将微生物环纳入营养网络模型（包含1-3个初级生产者、1-2个消费者和多达3个营养级的捕食者），通过关闭物种间的捕食-猎物链接并改变高维参数空间，进行数值模拟以研究网络动力学行为。

Result: (i)更长的营养链和(ii)更多消费者增加系统混沌性，(iii)杂食性相互作用促进稳定性。许多配置表现出高比例的混沌行为。反馈回路分析表明正负相互作用的平衡对系统收敛到稳态起关键作用。

Conclusion: 交互作用和反馈而非复杂性是稳定性的关键驱动因素，不存在明确的稳定性-复杂性关系，而是存在稳定性-相互作用依赖性。混沌动力学可能具有重要影响，对可预测性和生态系统管理具有潜在意义。

Abstract: Understanding the dynamics of real world complex networks is crucial for assessing their predictability, resilience, and improving ecosystem management, especially in the context of climate change. The relationship between stability and complexity in ecological networks is still debated in the literature. In this modeling study, we investigate whether a complex marine trophic network, characterized by multiple trophic interactions and environmental constraints, exhibits predominantly stable, periodic or chaotic dynamics. We incorporate the microbial loop into a trophic network model, which includes one to three primary producers, one or two consumers, and up to three trophic levels of predators. The microbial loop is a key process in which bacteria recycle detritus from higher trophic levels into nutrients available for the growth of primary producers, ensuring mass conservation within the system. We perform numerical simulations to investigate the dynamic behavior of the network, exploring several configurations by turning off predator prey links between species and varying the high dimensional parameter space. Our results show that (i) longer trophic chains and (ii) a higher number of consumers increase system chaoticity, whereas (iii) omnivorous interactions promote stability. Notably, many of the configurations exhibit high percentages of chaotic behavior. Feedback loop analysis suggests that the balance between negative and positive interactions plays a key role in the convergence of the system toward a steady state. This study shows that interactions and feedback, rather than complexity, are key drivers of stability, pointing to the absence of a clear stability complexity relationship and instead highlighting a stability interaction dependence. Chaotic dynamics may also play an important role, with potential implications for predictability and ecosystem management.
