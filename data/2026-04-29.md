### [1] [Equation Learning for multiscale models of infectious diseases](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25038)
*James W. G. Doran,Cameron A. Smith,Christian A. Yates,Ruth Bowness*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文构建了一个性别分层的多尺度模型框架，通过常微分方程和随机耦合函数连接宿主内与宿主间尺度，以探究男性和女性在结核病负担差异中的机制，作为概念验证研究。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 结核病在男性中发病率显著高于女性（2023年全球成人病例中男性占62.5%），其原因可能涉及宿主内因素（如免疫反应差异）和群体尺度因素（如治疗完成率差异）。为系统研究不同尺度如何共同决定这一性别差异，需要建立一个能够同时表征宿主内和宿主间动态的多尺度框架。

Method: 开发了性别分层的多尺度框架：1）通过基于智能体的宿主内模型学习常微分方程，捕获宿主内平均动态；2）使用常微分方程描述宿主间人群动态；3）通过随机耦合函数连接两个尺度。通过反事实情景分析性别对结核病传播动力学的影响。

Result: 论文作为概念验证，展示了多尺度框架的开发与实现过程，并通过反事实情景初步揭示了性别因素对结核病动态的影响。

Conclusion: 本文成功构建了一个性别分层的多尺度模型框架，为未来更深入研究性别差异在结核病传播中的作用提供了方法论基础。

Abstract: Tuberculosis (TB) is an airborne disease caused by the pathogen Mycobacterium tuberculosis. In 2023, according to the World Health Organization, it ''probably'' replaced COVID-19 as the leading cause of death from an infectious agent globally; in the nineteenth century, one in seven of all humans deaths were as a result of tuberculosis. More than 10 million people are diagnosed with TB every year. The majority of cases in adults occur in males (62.5% of all global adult cases in 2023, compared to 37.5% in females). The main reasons for males suffering from a higher burden of global TB cases, compared to females, is likely to be a combination of within-host factors, such as differences in immune response, and population-scale factors, such as likelihood of completing treatment. To investigate the impact different scales have in determining this higher TB burden in males, we have developed a gender/sex-stratified multiscale framework. We have learnt ordinary differential equations (ODEs) to capture the average output of an agent-based within-host model, and used the resulting equations to describe the within-host scales of the multiscale framework. We evolve the population demographics at the between-host scale using ODEs, and link the scales with stochastic coupling functions. We have considered counterfactual scenarios to elucidate the impact of sex and gender on the infectious disease dynamics of TB. This paper is intended to provide a proof-of-concept for the development and implementation of the presented multiscale framework.

### [2] [From lab to outbreak: experimental mosquito extrinsic incubation period distributions shape dengue epidemic dynamics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25750)
*Léa Loisel,Sandie Arnoux,Gaël Beaunée,Pauline Ezanno*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 登革热传播模型使用实验推导的蚊虫外潜伏期（EIP）分布，相较于传统指数分布，会延迟并平缓流行高峰，降低峰值强度但延长危机持续时间，同时不影响暴发概率。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 当前登革热传播模型普遍假设蚊虫外潜伏期（EIP）服从指数分布，这种简化可能忽略了重要的生物学变异性，导致模型预测不准确。

Method: 构建了一个随机机制性登革热传播模型，对比了常用指数分布（EXP）与实验推导分布（ED）两种EIP设置下的流行病动力学差异。

Result: 实验推导的EIP分布使流行高峰延迟且更平缓，峰值强度降低但持续时间延长，危机时长略有增加；蚊虫死亡率和人类恢复率是主要调节因素，暴发概率基本不受影响。

Conclusion: 引入实验数据驱动的EIP分布能提升模型的生物学真实性，有助于改进登革热流行预测，从而优化病媒控制策略和公共卫生应对措施。

Abstract: Dengue virus transmission models commonly assume an exponential distribution for the mosquito extrinsic incubation period (EIP), potentially oversimplifying biological variability. We developed a stochastic mechanistic dengue transmission model comparing epidemic dynamics under commonly assumed exponential (EXP) versus experimentally derived (ED) EIP distributions. Our results show that using an experimentally derived EIP distribution delays and flattens epidemic peaks, resulting in lower but more prolonged peaks, slightly prolongs crisis durations, and reduces peak intensity compared to the exponential assumption, while outbreak probability remains largely unaffected. These differences are modulated by mosquito mortality and human recovery principally. Incorporating experimentally informed EIP distributions enhances the biological realism of models and may improve predictions of dengue epidemic dynamics, informing more effective vector control strategies and public health responses.

### [3] [PhyloSDF: Phylogenetically-Conditioned Neural Generation of 3D Skull Morphology via Residual Flow Matching](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25371)
*Kaikwan Lau,Gary P. T. Choi*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 提出PhyloSDF，一个结合DeepSDF与系统发育一致性损失+残差条件流匹配的生成模型，在仅24物种、每物种约4样本的极度稀缺数据下，生成生物合理的三维颅骨形态，并实现系统发育外推与祖先重建。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 计算进化生物学中生成新颖、生物合理的3D形态结构面临两大挑战：极度数据稀缺（每物种仅少量样本）以及生成形态需尊重物种间的系统发育关系（进化树结构）。现有生成方法在如此小样本和系统发育约束下表现不佳。

Method: (1) DeepSDF自解码器 + 系统发育一致性损失（Phylogenetic Consistency Loss）：让隐空间结构编码进化距离（Pearson r=0.993）。(2) 残差条件流匹配（Residual CFM）：将生成分解为物种质心的解析查找 + 残差学习的预测，使模型能从每物种约4个样本生成。

Result: 在24物种100个达尔文雀及近缘种颅骨微CT扫描上，生成网格达到真实种内变异88-129%，所有180个生成网格均验证为非记忆。Residual CFM在保真度（Chamfer距离0.00181）和形态Fréchet距离（10,641）上优于去噪扩散（完全失败）、标准流匹配（模式坍缩至3-6%变异）和高斯混合基线。18物种的留一法实验证明系统发育外推能力，隐空间平滑插值生成生物合理的祖先颅骨重建。

Conclusion: PhyloSDF成功解决了极端数据稀缺下的3D形态生成问题，首次实现了系统发育约束下的神经生成建模，为进化形态学提供了计算工具，尤其适用于博物馆标本等珍贵小样本数据。

Abstract: Generating novel, biologically plausible three-dimensional morphological structures is a fundamental challenge in computational evolutionary biology, hampered by extreme data scarcity and the requirement that generated shapes respect phylogenetic relationships among species. In this work, we present PhyloSDF, a phylogenetically-conditioned neural generative model for 3D biological morphology that integrates two innovations: (1) a DeepSDF auto-decoder regularized by a novel Phylogenetic Consistency Loss that structures the latent space to correlate with evolutionary distances (Pearson $r=0.993$); (2) a Residual Conditional Flow Matching (Residual CFM) architecture that factorizes generation into analytic species-centroid lookup and learned residual prediction, enabling generation from as few as ~4 specimens per species. We evaluate PhyloSDF on 100 micro-CT-scanned skulls of Darwin's Finches and their relatives across 24 species. The model generates novel meshes achieving 88-129% of real intra-species variation at the code level, with all 180 generated meshes verified as non-memorized. Residual CFM surpasses denoising diffusion (which fails entirely at this scale), standard flow matching (which mode-collapses to 3-6% variation), and a Gaussian mixture baseline in both fidelity (Chamfer Distance 0.00181 vs. 0.00190) and morphometric Fréchet distance (10,641 vs. 13,322). Leave-one-species-out experiments across 18 species demonstrate phylogenetic extrapolation capability, and smooth latent interpolations produce biologically plausible ancestral skull reconstructions.

</details>
