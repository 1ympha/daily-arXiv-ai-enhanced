### [1] [Noninvasive H3 K27M screening in pediatric diffuse midline glioma using radiomics on heterogeneous T2-weighted MRI](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.01497)
*Arthur Zagitov,Alexander Beznosikov,Vladimir Bozhenko,Ninel Kamyshnikova,Tatiana Kulinich,Sofia Polozova,Yaroslav Kholodov*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 基于T2加权MRI的影像组学可作为H3K27M突变型弥漫中线胶质瘤的中等筛查辅助手段，但不能替代组织学诊断。最佳流程（预处理+特征选择+体积+CatBoost）准确率0.730，F1-score 0.826。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: H3K27M突变状态的确认通常需要对中线结构进行有创组织取样，而T2加权MRI是临床上最常可获取的序列。本研究旨在评估基于该序列的影像组学能否在异质性转诊队列中提供辅助筛查信号，减少对侵入性活检的依赖。

Method: 分析98例经组织学确认的患儿（73例突变阳性，25例野生型）。采用PyRadiomics提取特征，经过各向同性重采样、双重颅骨剥离、多尺度滤波等预处理，系统消融预处理、特征选择、肿瘤体积及TabDDPM合成少数类增强等环节，使用CatBoost和TabPFN模型，在100次分层训练/测试分割中评估性能。

Result: 纯影像组学准确率0.664，F1-score 0.784。最佳流程（预处理+特征选择+体积+CatBoost）准确率0.730±0.068，F1-score 0.826±0.044。TabDDPM增强使TabPFN在200个增强行时F1-score达到0.81±0.05。

Conclusion: T2加权影像组学可作为中等程度的筛查和分诊辅助工具，但不能替代组织学诊断。

Abstract: Histone H3K27M mutation status defines a clinically aggressive subgroup of pediatric diffuse midline glioma and informs prognosis and trial eligibility, but confirmation usually requires tissue sampling from eloquent midline structures. We evaluated whether radiomics from routinely available T2-weighted MRI can provide an adjunctive screening signal in a heterogeneous referral-style cohort, where scans are often acquired externally and T2-weighted imaging is the only consistently available sequence. Ninety-eight pediatric patients with tissue-confirmed status were analyzed (73 mutation-positive, 25 wild-type). Expert tumor segmentations defined the regions of interest for PyRadiomics feature extraction after isotropic resampling, dual skull stripping, and multi-scale filtering. We systematically ablated preprocessing, correlation pruning with repeated recursive feature elimination, tumor volume, and TabDDPM synthetic minority augmentation across 100 stratified train/test splits with real-only test sets. Pure radiomics achieved accuracy 0.664 and F1-score 0.784. The best pipeline used preprocessing, feature selection, and volume with CatBoost, achieving accuracy 0.730$\pm$0.068 and F1-score 0.826$\pm$0.044. TabDDPM improved TabPFN to F1-score 0.81$\pm$0.05 at 200 augmented rows. These results support T2-weighted radiomics as a moderate screening and triage aid, not a replacement for tissue-based diagnosis.

### [2] [Identifiability Limits of Physics-Informed Inference for Spatial Stochastic Dynamics from Static Snapshots](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.01749)
*Rujie Gu,Ray Zirui Zhang,Christopher E. Miles*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 本文研究如何利用物理信息机器学习从静态生物测量数据（如单张基因表达快照）中推断空间异质性动力学参数（扩散、生成、降解等），发现点源可恢复参数可辨识性，而分布式源不可辨识，且边界条件、空间正则性和随机微积分约定等建模选择会影响结果。多种物理信息推断方案在单快照上表现良好，但需结合可辨识性分析以确保结果有意义的解读。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 许多生物学测量是破坏性的，只能获取空间信息而丢失了动态信息。为了从静态快照中推断出背后的动力学过程，本文以亚细胞基因表达成像为动机，探究如何利用物理信息机器学习从静态空间分子分布中识别空间异质性参数。

Method: 首先进行结构性可辨识性分析，比较分布式源与点源（如转录位点）的参数可辨识性；然后分析边界条件、空间正则性和随机微积分约定等建模选择的影响；最后适配多种物理信息推断方案（不同方式表示解和强制约束方程），并在单张快照数据上进行推断实验。

Result: 分布式源不可辨识，而点源可恢复可辨识性；边界条件、空间正则性和随机微积分约定等建模选择显著影响可辨识性；多种物理信息方案能从单张快照中有效推断出空间异质性参数。

Conclusion: 物理信息方法能从静态数据中恢复生物动力学的空间异质性，但其应用必须伴随仔细的可辨识性分析，才能获得有意义的解释。

Abstract: Despite increasing scale and resolution, many biological measurements remain destructive, revealing only spatial information rather than the dynamics it encodes. By combining flexible representations with mechanistic constraints, physics-informed machine learning offers a promising route to inferring these dynamics from static snapshots. Motivated by subcellular imaging of gene expression, we ask when a static spatial pattern of molecules can identify spatially varying diffusivity, creation, destruction, and boundary exchange, and how different inference schemes perform on the task. A structural identifiability analysis shows that distributed sources are non-identifiable, whereas a point source such as a transcription site can restore identifiability. These limits are further shaped by seemingly innocuous modeling choices: the boundary conditions, the spatial regularity of the underlying dynamics, and even the stochastic calculus convention. We then adapt several physics-informed schemes, differing in how they represent the solution and enforce the governing equations, and demonstrate effective inference from a single snapshot. Physics-informed approaches can thus recover spatial heterogeneities of biological dynamics from static data, but their use should be accompanied and guided by careful identifiability analysis for meaningful interpretation of the results.
