### [1] [Cross-Species Antimicrobial Resistance Prediction from Genomic Foundation Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11141)
*Huilin Tai*

Main category: q-bio.GN

TL;DR: 该研究针对跨物种抗生素耐药性预测的OOD泛化问题，提出基于基因组基础模型嵌入和局部激活模式保留的方法，显著提升了跨物种预测性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 跨物种抗生素耐药性预测本质上是分布外泛化问题，现有模型在训练物种上表现良好但在不同物种间泛化能力差，因为耐药性机制在不同物种间存在异质性混合（局部基因盒转移和物种特异性基因组背景）。

Method: 1) 建立可解释的k-mer基线(Kover)；2) 使用Evo-1-8k-base基因组基础模型嵌入；3) 基于激活尺度、各向同性、有效秩和跨种子稳定性进行层选择诊断；4) 将每窗口嵌入视为有序多元信号，应用MiniRocket总结多尺度局部激活模式而非全局池化。

Result: 1) 严格的物种留出协议显示强物种内性能在真实跨物种评估中崩溃；2) 在深层发现稳定性边界，靠近该边界提取的嵌入提供更鲁棒表示；3) 保留局部激活模式显著改善当耐药机制局部化时的泛化能力；4) 聚合策略在跨物种AMR预测中起核心作用。

Conclusion: 跨物种AMR预测需要保留可转移的生物信号而非放大系统发育捷径，局部激活模式的保留和适当的聚合策略对改善跨物种泛化至关重要，特别是在耐药机制局部化的情况下。

Abstract: Cross-species antimicrobial resistance (AMR) prediction is fundamentally an out-of-distribution (OOD) generalization problem: models trained on one set of bacterial taxa must transfer to phylogenetically distinct genomes that may rely on different resistance mechanisms. Across species, resistance arises from a heterogeneous mixture of localized, horizontally transferred gene cassettes and diffuse species-specific genomic backgrounds, making successful transfer inherently mechanism-dependent. Using a strict species holdout protocol, we first establish an interpretable k-mer baseline with Kover and show that strong within-species performance collapses under true cross-species evaluation. This motivates representation-level approaches that preserve transferable biological signals rather than amplify phylogenetic shortcuts.  We investigate genomic foundation model embeddings derived from Evo-1-8k-base and introduce diagnostics for layer selection based on activation scale, isotropy, effective rank, and cross-seed stability under native bfloat16 inference. These analyses identify a stability boundary in deeper layers and reveal that embeddings extracted near this boundary provide more robust representations for downstream prediction. To preserve localized resistance signals, we treat per-window embeddings as an ordered multivariate signal and apply MiniRocket to summarize multi-scale local activation patterns instead of relying on global pooling. Our results show that aggregation strategy plays a central role in cross-species AMR prediction and that preserving local activation patterns substantially improves generalization when resistance mechanisms are localized.

### [2] [A Standardized Framework For Evaluating Gene Expression Generative Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11244)
*Andrea Rubbi,Andrea Giuseppe Di Francesco,Mohammad Lotfollahi,Pietro Liò*

Main category: q-bio.GN

TL;DR: GGE是一个开源Python框架，用于标准化单细胞基因表达生成模型的评估，解决当前评估实践中指标不一致、超参数不可比和缺乏生物学基础指标的问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 单细胞基因表达生成模型的快速发展迫切需要标准化评估框架。当前评估实践存在指标实现不一致、超参数选择不可比、缺乏生物学基础指标等问题，导致方法间无法公平比较。

Method: GGE提供全面的分布度量套件，具有明确的计算空间选项；通过差异表达基因（DEG）分析和扰动效应相关性进行生物学动机评估；支持标准化报告和可复现的基准测试。

Result: 通过对单细胞生成建模文献的广泛分析，发现不存在标准化评估协议，不同方法报告的指标在不同空间和超参数下计算，指标值因实现选择而有显著差异。

Conclusion: GGE能够实现生成方法间的公平比较，加速扰动响应预测、细胞身份建模和反事实推理的进展，为单细胞生成模型评估提供标准化解决方案。

Abstract: The rapid development of generative models for single-cell gene expression data has created an urgent need for standardised evaluation frameworks. Current evaluation practices suffer from inconsistent metric implementations, incomparable hyperparameter choices, and a lack of biologically-grounded metrics. We present Generated Genetic Expression Evaluator (GGE), an open-source Python framework that addresses these challenges by providing a comprehensive suite of distributional metrics with explicit computation space options and biologically-motivated evaluation through differentially expressed gene (DEG)-focused analysis and perturbation-effect correlation, enabling standardized reporting and reproducible benchmarking. Through extensive analysis of the single-cell generative modeling literature, we identify that no standardized evaluation protocol exists. Methods report incomparable metrics computed in different spaces with different hyperparameters. We demonstrate that metric values vary substantially depending on implementation choices, highlighting the critical need for standardization. GGE enables fair comparison across generative approaches and accelerates progress in perturbation response prediction, cellular identity modeling, and counterfactual inference.

### [3] [ELISA: An Interpretable Hybrid Generative AI Agent for Expression-Grounded Discovery in Single-Cell Genomics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11872)
*Omar Coser*

Main category: q-bio.GN

TL;DR: ELISA是一个可解释的单细胞RNA测序分析框架，通过整合scGPT表达嵌入、BioBERT语义检索和LLM解释，实现交互式单细胞发现，显著优于现有方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 当前单细胞RNA测序数据分析存在瓶颈：智能AI系统无法直接访问转录组表示，而表达基础模型对自然语言不透明，需要桥梁连接转录组数据探索与生物学发现。

Method: 开发ELISA框架，统一scGPT表达嵌入与BioBERT语义检索，结合LLM解释。包含自动查询分类器，根据查询类型（基因标记、自然语言概念或混合）路由到相应分析流程。集成通路活性评分、配体-受体相互作用预测、条件感知比较分析和细胞类型比例估计等模块。

Result: 在六个不同的scRNA-seq数据集上评估，ELISA在细胞类型检索方面显著优于CellWhisperer（组合置换检验p<0.001），基因标记查询改进尤其显著（Cohen's d=5.98）。能够复现已发表的生物学发现（平均综合得分0.90），通路对齐和主题覆盖接近完美（各0.98）。

Conclusion: ELISA通过基于LLM的推理生成候选假设，成功弥合了转录组数据探索与生物学发现之间的鸿沟，为单细胞基因组学提供了表达基础的可解释分析框架。

Abstract: Translating single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq) data into mechanistic biological hypotheses remains a critical bottleneck, as agentic AI systems lack direct access to transcriptomic representations while expression foundation models remain opaque to natural language. Here we introduce ELISA (Embedding-Linked Interactive Single-cell Agent), an interpretable framework that unifies scGPT expression embeddings with BioBERT-based semantic retrieval and LLM-mediated interpretation for interactive single-cell discovery. An automatic query classifier routes inputs to gene marker scoring, semantic matching, or reciprocal rank fusion pipelines depending on whether the query is a gene signature, natural language concept, or mixture of both. Integrated analytical modules perform pathway activity scoringacross 60+ gene sets, ligand--receptor interaction prediction using 280+ curated pairs, condition-aware comparative analysis, and cell-type proportion estimation all operating directly on embedded data without access to the original count matrix. Benchmarked across six diverse scRNA-seq datasets spanning inflammatory lung disease, pediatric and adult cancers, organoid models, healthy tissue, and neurodevelopment, ELISA significantly outperforms CellWhisperer in cell type retrieval (combined permutation test, $p < 0.001$), with particularly large gains on gene-signature queries (Cohen's $d = 5.98$ for MRR). ELISA replicates published biological findings (mean composite score 0.90) with near-perfect pathway alignment and theme coverage (0.98 each), and generates candidate hypotheses through grounded LLM reasoning, bridging the gap between transcriptomic data exploration and biological discovery. Code available at: https://github.com/omaruno/ELISA-An-AI-Agent-for-Expression-Grounded-Discovery-in-Single-Cell-Genomics.git (If you use ELISA in your research, please cite this work).

### [4] [Ill-Conditioning in Dictionary-Based Dynamic-Equation Learning: A Systems Biology Case Study](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11330)
*Yuxiang Feng,Niall M Mangan,Manu Jayadharan*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 该研究系统分析了数据驱动方程发现中数值病态问题对生物系统识别的影响，发现即使只有2-3项组合也会出现强多重共线性，正交多项式基并不总能解决病态问题，且当数据分布与正交基权重函数对齐时才能改善模型恢复精度。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 数据驱动方法从时间序列数据中发现控制方程为理解复杂生物系统提供了强大框架，但库基方法面临候选函数强相关时的数值病态挑战。采样限制和特定候选库选择会导致强多重共线性和数值不稳定，测量噪声可能导致恢复模型差异巨大，阻碍准确系统识别。

Method: 使用系统生物学基准模型，系统分析病态条件对稀疏识别生物动力学的影响。研究不同候选库（包括单项式和正交多项式基）在数据分布与正交基权重函数对齐或偏离时的表现，评估条件数和模型恢复精度。

Result: 研究发现：1) 即使只有2-3项组合也会表现出强多重共线性和极大条件数；2) 正交多项式基不能一致解决病态问题，当数据分布偏离正交基相关权重函数时，其表现可能比单项式库更差；3) 当数据采样分布与正交基适当权重函数对齐时，数值条件改善，正交多项式基能在两个基准模型中提高模型恢复精度。

Conclusion: 数值病态是数据驱动方程发现中的重要挑战，需要仔细考虑候选库选择和采样策略。正交多项式基并非万能解决方案，其有效性取决于数据分布与基函数权重函数的对齐程度。在适当条件下，正交基可以改善数值条件并提高模型恢复精度。

Abstract: Data-driven discovery of governing equations from time-series data provides a powerful framework for understanding complex biological systems. Library-based approaches that use sparse regression over candidate functions have shown considerable promise, but they face a critical challenge when candidate functions become strongly correlated: numerical ill-conditioning. Poor or restricted sampling, together with particular choices of candidate libraries, can produce strong multicollinearity and numerical instability. In such cases, measurement noise may lead to widely different recovered models, obscuring the true underlying dynamics and hindering accurate system identification. Although sparse regularization promotes parsimonious solutions and can partially mitigate conditioning issues, strong correlations may persist, regularization may bias the recovered models, and the regression problem may remain highly sensitive to small perturbations in the data. We present a systematic analysis of how ill-conditioning affects sparse identification of biological dynamics using benchmark models from systems biology. We show that combinations involving as few as two or three terms can already exhibit strong multicollinearity and extremely large condition numbers. We further show that orthogonal polynomial bases do not consistently resolve ill-conditioning and can perform worse than monomial libraries when the data distribution deviates from the weight function associated with the orthogonal basis. Finally, we demonstrate that when data are sampled from distributions aligned with the appropriate weight functions corresponding to the orthogonal basis, numerical conditioning improves, and orthogonal polynomial bases can yield improved model recovery accuracy across two baseline models.

### [5] [Binding Free Energies without Alchemy](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12253)
*Michael Brocidiacono,Brandon Novy,Rishabh Dey,Konstantin I. Popov,Alexander Tropsha*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 提出了一种无需炼金中间态的隐式溶剂绝对结合自由能计算方法DBFE，相比传统方法大幅减少模拟需求


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统绝对结合自由能方法需要大量模拟炼金中间态，计算成本高，限制了其实际应用

Method: 开发了DBFE方法，这是一种隐式溶剂中的末端态方法，无需炼金中间态，仅需受体、配体和复合物的模拟

Result: 在宿主-客体基准测试中优于OBC2双解耦方法，在蛋白质-配体基准测试中与OBC2 MM/GBSA表现相当

Conclusion: DBFE方法计算效率高，受体和配体模拟可预先计算并跨化合物摊销，每个配体仅需一次复合物模拟，适合虚拟筛选工作流

Abstract: Absolute Binding Free Energy (ABFE) methods are among the most accurate computational techniques for predicting protein-ligand binding affinities, but their utility is limited by the need for many simulations of alchemically modified intermediate states. We propose Direct Binding Free Energy (DBFE), an end-state ABFE method in implicit solvent that requires no alchemical intermediates. DBFE outperforms OBC2 double decoupling on a host-guest benchmark and performs comparably to OBC2 MM/GBSA on a protein-ligand benchmark. Since receptor and ligand simulations can be precomputed and amortized across compounds, DBFE requires only one complex simulation per ligand compared to the many lambda windows needed for double decoupling, making it a promising candidate for virtual screening workflows. We publicly release the code for this method at https://github.com/molecularmodelinglab/dbfe.
