### [1] [Dose-Dependent Cardiac Complexity Changes in Children Following Prenatal Glucocorticoid Exposure: Complementary Evidence from Multiscale Entropy Analysis and ECG Foundation Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.04074)
*Nicolas B. Garnier,Michelle Dreiling,Valeska Kozik,Matthias Schwab,Florian Rakers,Martin G Frasch*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 产前糖皮质激素暴露对儿童心脏有剂量依赖性影响：高剂量暴露儿童在认知压力下表现出更快的熵率衰减，而心电图基础模型检测到剂量独立的形态学"暴露指纹"。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究产前糖皮质激素暴露是否对儿童心脏功能产生剂量依赖性影响，以及如何通过多尺度复杂性特征和心电图基础模型来检测这些影响。

Method: 研究49名儿童（8-15岁），根据母亲孕期接受倍他米松治疗多发性硬化症的剂量分为三组：低剂量（<5g累积）、高剂量（≥5g）和对照组。通过5分钟心电图记录，计算12个多尺度复杂性特征，使用11个心电图基础模型维度，采用线性混合模型、Kruskal-Wallis检验、Spearman相关性和Jonckheere-Terpstra趋势检验进行分析。

Result: 高剂量暴露儿童表现出显著更快的熵率衰减率（p=0.031），效应主要出现在心算压力段（p=0.005）。熵率衰减率对输入信号选择和标准化具有鲁棒性（r>0.98），而样本熵和近似熵则不然。心电图基础模型维度显示弱剂量响应证据，仅1/22的协变量调整对比通过FDR校正，且出现矛盾的低剂量效应更强现象。

Conclusion: 研究提出产前糖皮质激素心脏编程的两组分模型：剂量独立的形态学"暴露指纹"和剂量依赖的动力学效应。熵率衰减率作为鲁棒的剂量依赖性指标，在认知压力下检测心脏自主神经动力学变化。由于样本量小，这些结果应视为假设生成，需要在更大队列中验证。

Abstract: \noindent\textbf{Background} Prenatal glucocorticoid exposure alters cardiac development, but whether persistent cardiac effects in childhood follow a dose-response relationship remains unknown. We recently showed that ECG foundation models detect robust cardiac differences between steroid-exposed and control children, while traditional heart rate variability metrics lose significance after covariate adjustment. Here, we investigate the dose-response dimension using complementary analytical approaches. \noindent\textbf{Methods} We studied 49 children (ages 8--15) whose mothers received betamethasone during pregnancy for multiple sclerosis: 12 low-dose ({$<$}5\,g cumulative), 13 high-dose ({$\geq$}5\,g), and 24 controls. Five-minute ECG recordings during the Trier Social Stress Test yielded 251 observations. We computed 12 multiscale complexity features and tested 11 ECG foundation model (FM) dimensions using linear mixed models, Kruskal--Wallis tests with Dunn's post-hoc comparisons, Spearman correlations, and Jonckheere--Terpstra trend tests. \noindent\textbf{Findings} The binary exposed-versus-controls comparison showed no significant complexity effects ($p>0.39$). However, dose-based analysis revealed that high-dose children exhibited significantly faster entropy rate ($h$) decay rates than low-dose children ($p=0.031$); neither sample entropy nor approximate entropy decay rates reached significance ($p=0.18$ and $p=0.12$, respectively). Effects localized to the mental arithmetic stress segment (Kruskal--Wallis $p=0.005$; Dunn's $p=0.004$). A cross-condition robustness analysis confirmed that $h$ decay rate is invariant to input signal choice and normalization ($r>0.98$), while sample and approximate entropy are not. In contrast, the 11 FM dimensions showed weak dose-response evidence: only 1 of 22 covariate-adjusted contrasts survived FDR correction, with paradoxically stronger low-dose effects. \noindent\textbf{Interpretation} The entropy rate decay rate -- uniquely robust across input conditions -- reveals a dose-dependent effect on cardiac autonomic dynamics under cognitive stress, while FM dimensions detect a dose-independent morphological ``exposure fingerprint.'' These exploratory findings suggest a two-component model of prenatal glucocorticoid cardiac programming -- ~morphological (dose-independent) and dynamical (dose-dependent)~ -- providing more complete characterization than either approach alone. Given the small sample size, these results should be considered hypothesis-generating and require replication in larger cohorts.

### [2] [On estimating the effective sample size of phylogenetic trees in an autocorrelated chain](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03521)
*Jonathan Klawitter,Lars Berling,Jordan Douglas,Dong Xie,Alexei J. Drummond*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文比较了贝叶斯系统发育推断中树有效样本量(ESS)的估计方法，评估了基于条件分支分布(CCD)的新方法和基于独立链间分支频率差异的概率估计器，为复杂系统发育分析提供可靠的树ESS估计指导。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 贝叶斯系统发育推断中，准确估计MCMC样本的有效样本量(ESS)对于考虑自相关性至关重要。虽然连续参数的ESS估计方法已很成熟，但树空间的离散性和高维特性带来了巨大挑战。

Method: 比较现有树ESS估计器与基于可处理树分布（特别是条件分支分布CCD）的新方法，以及基于独立链间分支频率差异的新概率估计器。使用已知ESS边界的模拟链评估估计器准确性，并在模拟和真实数据集上评估其稳定性和鲁棒性。

Result: 基于CCD的估计器与现有方法表现相当，其中两种方法通过多个估计的平均降低了方差。相比之下，概率估计器和两种先前推荐的方法在长链上计算成本过高。研究发现后验分布的多模态性和混合不良会显著影响ESS估计。

Conclusion: 研究结果为复杂系统发育分析中可靠且高效的树ESS估计提供了指导，强调了仔细解释ESS估计的重要性，特别是在存在多模态和混合不良的情况下。

Abstract: Estimating the effective sample size (ESS) is fundamental in Bayesian phylogenetic inference to properly account for autocorrelation in MCMC samples. While methods for continuous parameters are well established, the discrete and high-dimensional nature of treespace poses substantial challenges. Here, we compare existing tree ESS estimators with novel approaches that leverage tractable tree distributions, specifically Conditional Clade Distributions (CCDs), as well as a new probabilistic estimator based on clade frequency differences between independent chains. Using simulated chains with known ESS bounds, we assess estimator accuracy and evaluate their stability and robustness on simulated and real datasets. We further examine how multimodality in posterior distributions and poor mixing can substantially affect ESS estimates, highlighting the need for careful interpretation. Our CCD-based estimators perform comparably to existing approaches, with two methods showing lower variance by averaging across multiple estimates. In contrast, the probabilistic estimator and two previously recommended methods incur prohibitive computational costs for long chains. Together, these results provide guidance for reliable and efficient tree ESS estimation in complex phylogenetic analyses.

### [3] [Mutation Rate Variation Across Genomic Regions in \textit{Arabidopsis thaliana}](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03591)
*Elisa Heinrich-Mora,Marcus W. Feldman*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 研究使用拟南芥基因水平的新生突变数据，发现染色质特征不仅能预测平均突变率，还能预测基因间突变率的变异性，特别是与活跃转录相关的组蛋白标记与较低的突变率和变异相关。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统群体遗传学将突变率视为基因组均匀参数，但经验证据显示突变率在基因组不同区域存在系统性变异，与染色质组织和表观遗传特征相关。研究旨在探索染色质特征是否不仅能预测平均突变率，还能预测基因间突变率的变异性。

Method: 使用拟南芥基因水平的新生突变数据，为减少选择异质性，分析仅限于受强纯化选择的基本和致死基因座。采用多种互补的多变量模型：异方差稳健线性回归、长度加权回归和带暴露偏移的泊松广义线性模型。

Result: 与活跃转录相关的组蛋白标记（H3K4me1、H3K4me3、H3K36ac）始终与较低的突变率和显著降低的基因间变异相关。GC含量在控制染色质预测因子后与平均突变率关联不大，但与突变率变异性正相关。偏度和峰度估计显示没有显著的高阶结构可归因于表观基因组预测因子。

Conclusion: 突变率在功能受限基因内受染色质状态系统性地结构化，进化过程不仅作用于预期突变率，还可能作用于基因座间的突变率变异性。

Abstract: In population genetics, mutation rate is often treated as a homogeneous parameter across the genome. Empirical evidence, however, shows systematic variation across genomic contexts associated with chromatin organization and epigenomic features. Using gene-level de novo mutation data from Arabidopsis thaliana, we test whether chromatin features predict not only the mean per-base mutation rate but also its variability across genes. To reduce heterogeneity in selective regime, we restrict analysis to essential and lethal loci subject to strong purifying selection. Across complementary multivariable models including heteroskedasticity-robust linear regression, length-weighted regression, and Poisson generalized linear models with exposure offsets, histone marks associated with active transcription (H3K4me1, H3K4me3, H3K36ac) are consistently associated with lower mean mutation rates and substantially reduced between-gene variance. GC content shows little association with the mean once chromatin predictors are controlled but is positively associated with mutation-rate variability. Estimates of skewness and kurtosis reveal no significant higher-order structure attributable to epigenomic predictors. A standardized Tajima's $D$ statistic yields directionally consistent but statistically underpowered associations with both the mean and variance of gene-level mutation rates. These results indicate that mutation rate is systematically structured by chromatin state within functionally constrained genes and suggest that evolutionary processes may act not only on expected mutation rate but also on its variability across loci.

</details>
