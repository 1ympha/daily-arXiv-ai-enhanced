### [2] [AntigenLM: Structure-Aware DNA Language Modeling for Influenza](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.09067)
*Yue Pei,Xuebin Chi,Yu Kang*

Main category: q-bio.GN

TL;DR: AntigenLM是一个基于流感基因组预训练的DNA语言模型，通过保持功能单元的完整性来捕捉进化约束，在抗原变异预测和亚型分类任务上表现出色。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 当前DNA基础模型在序列分析中往往落后于任务特定方法，原因不明。研究者希望通过保持基因组功能单元的完整性来构建更具生物学基础的DNA语言模型。

Method: 开发AntigenLM生成式DNA语言模型，在保持完整对齐功能单元的流感基因组上进行预训练，然后对血凝素(HA)和神经氨酸酶(NA)序列的时间序列数据进行微调。

Result: AntigenLM能够准确预测跨区域和亚型的未来抗原变异（包括训练中未见过的），优于系统发育和进化模型，同时实现近乎完美的亚型分类。消融研究表明破坏基因组结构会严重降低性能。

Conclusion: AntigenLM不仅为抗原进化预测提供了强大框架，也为构建具有生物学基础的DNA基础模型提供了一般性原则，强调了保持功能单元完整性的重要性。

Abstract: Language models have advanced sequence analysis, yet DNA foundation models often lag behind task-specific methods for unclear reasons. We present AntigenLM, a generative DNA language model pretrained on influenza genomes with intact, aligned functional units. This structure-aware pretraining enables AntigenLM to capture evolutionary constraints and generalize across tasks. Fine-tuned on time-series hemagglutinin (HA) and neuraminidase (NA) sequences, AntigenLM accurately forecasts future antigenic variants across regions and subtypes, including those unseen during training, outperforming phylogenetic and evolution-based models. It also achieves near-perfect subtype classification. Ablation studies show that disrupting genomic structure through fragmentation or shuffling severely degrades performance, revealing the importance of preserving functional-unit integrity in DNA language modeling. AntigenLM thus provides both a powerful framework for antigen evolution prediction and a general principle for building biologically grounded DNA foundation models.

### [4] [Reply To: Global Gridded Population Datasets Systematically Underrepresent Rural Population by Josias Láng-Ritter et al](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.09248)
*Till Koebe,Emmanuel Letouzé,Tuba Bircan,Édith Darin,Douglas R. Leasure,Valentina Rotondi*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 该论文批评了全球网格化人口数据集系统性低估农村人口的说法，认为这种偏差更多源于方法学决策和历史分配问题，而非实际人口漏计。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 作者旨在对Láng-Ritter等人的研究提出批评，认为该研究关于全球网格化人口数据集系统性低估农村人口的关键主张过于大胆，缺乏充分证据支持，且缺乏谨慎和细致的讨论。

Method: 通过分析Láng-Ritter等人研究的方法论，指出其报告的偏差数据更多源于有争议的方法学决策和历史网格化人口估计在地方层面的错误分配，而非实际农村人口漏计。

Result: 认为原研究的偏差数据不能证明全球网格化人口数据集系统性低估农村人口，而是反映了方法学问题和历史数据分配错误。

Conclusion: 建议在未来的讨论和人口估计方法改进中考虑这些批评点，强调需要更谨慎、更细致的方法来评估人口数据集的准确性。

Abstract: The paper titled ''Global gridded population datasets systematically underrepresent rural population'' by Josias Láng-Ritter et al. provides a valuable contribution to the discourse on the accuracy of global population datasets, particularly in rural areas. We recognize the efforts put into this research and appreciate its contribution to the field. However, we feel that key claims in the study are overly bold, not properly backed by evidence and lack a cautious and nuanced discussion. We hope these points will be taken into account in future discussions and refinements of population estimation methodologies. We argue that the reported bias figures are less caused by actual undercounting of rural populations, but more so by contestable methodological decisions and the historic misallocation of (gridded) population estimates on the local level.
