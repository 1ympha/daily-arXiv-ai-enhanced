### [1] [A loser in both environments can survive by switching between them](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.04204)
*Riz Fernando Noronha,Kunihiko Kaneko*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 研究表明，在被两种静态环境中均会导致灭绝的捕食者-被捕食者模型中，当环境以足够快的速度交替变化时，物种可以无限期存活。该机制可推广到任意两物种系统，并发现药物轮换策略可能因非耐药性原因而失败。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探究一个物种如何在总是被竞争排除的环境中持续生存，即环境变化节奏本身能否成为物种存续的关键因素。

Method: 使用最小捕食者-被捕食者模型，设置环境依赖参数；推导临界切换速率；比较周期性切换与随机（泊松）切换的效果；将机制推广到任意两物种系统，提出"切换拯救函数"。

Result: 环境交替速度足够快时，原本在两种静态环境下都会灭绝的捕食者可以持续生存；随机切换需要比周期性切换更快的速率；推广至病毒动力学模型时，发现两种单独有效的药物在交替使用时可能失效。

Conclusion: 环境变化的节奏本身（而非环境本身）可以决定物种的存亡，这为药物轮换策略的失败提供了非耐药性解释。

Abstract: How can a species persist in an environment where it is always outcompeted? Using a minimal predator-prey model with environment-dependent parameters, we show that a predator driven to extinction in each of two static environments can survive indefinitely once the environment alternates between them fast enough. We derive the critical switching rate above which persistence occurs, and show that random (Poisson) switching needs to be faster than periodic switching in order to offset prolonged spells in the unfavorable environment. We then generalize the mechanism to any two-species system, and can predict persistence solely based on the sign of a single ``switching rescue function" assembled from the two boundary vector fields. This general result has broad reaching consequences: for instance, when applied to a standard model of viral dynamics, it predicts that two drugs which each clear a pathogen on their own can fail when alternated, giving a non-resistance-based explanation for the failure of drug-cycling strategies. Our results demonstrate that the tempo of environment change, as opposed to the environments themselves, can lead to species survival.

### [2] [Diffusion bridge with randomized initial and terminal times and its application to fish migration](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.04253)
*Hidekazu Yoshioka,Mohammed Louriki*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文构建了带随机起止时间的Cox-Ingersoll-Ross桥扩散模型，用于描述环境因素影响下洄游鱼类在固定观测点的数量动态，并基于水温数据对香鱼上溯洄游进行了参数估计与环境DNA数据分析。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有鱼类洄游模型难以有效整合环境因素（如水温度）对洄游起始和结束时间波动的随机影响，亟需一种兼顾数学严谨性与环境可解释性的新方法。

Method: 提出具有非Lipschitz扩散系数的Cox-Ingersoll-Ross桥过程，通过时间变化引入随机初始和终止时间，构建随机微分方程；先证明模型的适定性，再利用多年日观测数据对香鱼(Ayu)洄游参数进行估计。

Result: 证明了所建非Lipschitz扩散桥模型的适定性；基于水温假设成功估计了香鱼洄游参数；模型还可拓展应用于环境DNA数据分析。

Conclusion: 该研究为考虑环境因素的鱼类洄游理论提供了简洁而有效的新框架，在应用数学和生态学领域均有重要意义。

Abstract: We mathematically model the dynamics of the number of migratory fish observed at a fixed location along a river in a random environment. Particularly, as a new approach, we construct a stochastic differential equation that incorporates the influence of environmental factors on the fluctuations in the start and end of migration. The model is a diffusion bridge with a non-Lipschitz diffusion coefficient, called the Cox-Ingersoll-Ross bridge, and has random initial and terminal times arising from time-change, so that the influences of environmental factors can be efficiently incorporated. The well-posedness of the model is first established, which is considered novel and significant in applied mathematics. Second, we estimate the parameters of the model based on the latest multiyear daily data set for the upstream migration of Plecoglossus altivelis altivelis (Ayu) by relying on the hypothesis that water temperature affects the migration of the fish, which has been suggested in existing studies. We also explore the application of the proposed model to the challenging task of analyzing environmental DNA data. This study advances the development of a theory of fish migration that is simple yet can take environmental factors into account.

### [3] [Mathematical Model of Evolution of Non-Degenerate Replicator Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.04456)
*Alexander S. Bratus,Sergey Drozhzhin,Tatiana Yakushkina*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文提出并分析了一个非退化复制子系统的进化适应数学模型，在快慢时间尺度分离下，将适应度景观矩阵的优化简化为线性规划问题。应用于四个经典系统后，发现进化遵循三阶段普适模式（初始适应度增长、优势物种出现、稳定化），最终系统获得抗寄生物种能力。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有复制子系统进化模型缺乏对非退化（永久性）系统的数学分析，且未充分揭示适应度景观演化与物种动态之间的多时间尺度耦合机制。

Method: 采用快慢时间尺度分离方法（基于Tikhonov定理），将适应度矩阵优化问题简化为凸集上的线性规划问题，推导出适应度变分公式及最大化的充要条件。

Result: 在超循环、双超循环、蚁丘系统和RNA分子网络四个系统中，进化均呈现三阶段普适模式；所有进化后的系统均获得抗寄生物种能力；无退化约束下会导致物种连续灭绝。

Conclusion: 该模型揭示了非退化复制子系统进化的数学结构，证明了适应度最大化与抗寄生能力获得的必然联系，为理解进化动力学提供了新视角。

Abstract: We propose and analyse a mathematical model of evolutionary adaptation for non-degenerate (permanent) replicator systems, in which the fitness landscape matrix evolves on a slow timescale -- the evolutionary time -- while the species dynamics unfold on a fast timescale. Under a two-timescale separation justified by Tikhonov's theorem, the adaptation problem reduces to maximising the mean fitness at steady state over a convex admissible set of fitness landscape matrices. We derive a fitness variation formula and establish necessary and sufficient conditions for a fitness maximum, showing that the optimisation reduces at each step to a linear programming problem. The algorithm is applied to four canonical replicator systems: the hypercycle, the bi-hypercycle, the anthill system, and the RNA molecule network. In all cases the evolutionary process follows a universal three-phase pattern: an initial phase of fitness growth without equilibrium shift, during which purely altruistic replication gives way to mixed altruistic-selfish behaviour; a second phase of dominant species emergence; and a stabilisation phase analogous to the error catastrophe threshold in quasispecies models. A key consequence is that all evolved systems acquire resistance to parasitic species. We further prove that without non-degeneracy constraints the process leads to sequential species annihilation, with a provable spectral lower bound on fitness increase by dimension reduction.

### [4] [Diffusion learning reveals viable parameter manifolds and compensation geometry in biological dynamical systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.03671)
*Ruilin Zhang,Louis Tao,Zhuo-Cheng Xiao*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种将复杂系统参数空间中的可行集形式化为“可行参数流形”的框架，并利用条件扩散模型对参数-特征对进行学习，以高效采样、可视化和分析这些流形。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 复杂系统通常参数量远多于可观测实验量，导致不同参数组合可能产生相似动力学行为，但现有方法难以系统刻画这些参数间的补偿与退化结构。

Method: 将可行参数集定义为参数到特征映射下目标动力学行为的逆像；利用条件分数扩散模型在模拟的参数-特征对上训练，作为先验加权的可行集摊销采样器。

Result: 在Lorenz系统中，单标量轨迹统计生成薄可行片，双特征条件定位过渡邻接通道；Izhikevich模型中四个放电描述符近乎二维特征族，揭示规律与不规则补偿几何；在脉冲网络降维模型中，框架揭示兴奋-抑制补偿、时间尺度-耦合权衡等结构。

Conclusion: 鲁棒性、补偿和隐藏参数依赖可组织为逆几何问题，扩散模型为采样、可视化和探究该几何提供了实用工具。

Abstract: Models of complex systems often have many parameters, yet are constrained by far fewer experimentally accessible observables: similar activity can emerge from coordinated parameter changes. We formalize these compatible parameter sets as \emph{viable parameter manifolds}: the inverse images of a system's target dynamical behaviors under a parameter-to-feature map. The relevant codimension is not the number of reported features, but the effective rank of that map at the target scale. Co-varying features lower the codimension, while poor conditioning, high curvature, or regime mixing degrade learnability. We train conditional score-based diffusion models on simulated parameter--feature pairs and use them as amortized samplers of prior-weighted viable sets. In the Lorenz system, scalar trajectory statistics generate thin viable sheets, and two-feature conditioning localizes a transition-adjacent corridor. In the Izhikevich neuron model, four firing descriptors lie close to a nearly two-dimensional family of features, and the learned inverse images reveal distinct regular and irregular compensation geometries. In a recent ODE reduction of finite spiking networks, the same framework reveals excitatory--inhibitory compensation, timescale--coupling tradeoffs, and input-dependent viable manifolds across 4--12 parameter dimensions. In this view, robustness, compensation, and hidden parameter dependencies are organized as inverse geometry, with diffusion models providing practical tools for sampling, visualizing, and interrogating that geometry.
