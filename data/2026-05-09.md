### [1] [Higher-order interactions in ecology can be hidden in plain sight](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06301)
*Violeta Calleja-Solanas,Santiago Lamata-Otín,Carlos Gómez-Ambrosi,Jesús Gómez-Gardeñes,Sandro Meloni*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文表明高阶Lotka-Volterra动力学在某些情况下可被有效成对模型准确再现，因此高阶相互作用无法仅从时间序列数据中推断，存在机制可识别性问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 高阶相互作用被认为是生态动力学的重要组成部分，但作者希望检验是否能仅从时间序列数据中可靠地推断出高阶相互作用的存在。

Method: 通过比较高阶Lotka-Volterra动力学与有效成对模型在相同丰度时间序列上的拟合效果，分析两者是否可区分。

Result: 高阶相互作用在某些场景下可被成对模型准确再现，导致不同的相互作用机制产生几乎无法区分的动力学。

Conclusion: 仅靠时间序列数据无法可靠推断高阶相互作用结构，需要补充额外的生态学信息。

Abstract: Higher-order interactions are increasingly recognized as a key component of ecological dynamics. However, we show that higher-order Lotka-Volterra dynamics can, in some scenarios, be accurately reproduced by effective pairwise models fitted to the same abundance time series. Consequently, higher-order interactions cannot, in general, be inferred from time-series data alone. We further identify a fundamental problem of mechanistic identifiability, whereby distinct interaction mechanisms generate nearly indistinguishable dynamics, potentially leading to accurate yet misleading ecological interpretations. Our results highlight the need to complement time-series data with additional ecological information to infer interaction structure reliably.

### [2] [Mathematical Modeling of Early Embryonic Cell Cycles of Drosophila melanogaster](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06598)
*Meskerem Abebaw Mebratie,Benedikt Drebes,Katja Kapp,Arno Müller,Werner M. Seiler*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 该论文构建了果蝇胚胎细胞周期的生化数学模型，通过模型简化后分析了振荡行为，并重点研究了CycB合成速率和APC激活系数两个参数的作用。数值模拟表明，CycB合成的时间依赖性可以解释前14个细胞周期的周期延长现象，与实验数据定性吻合。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 果蝇胚胎早期细胞周期具有快速且高度协调的特点，但控制周期逐步延长的分子机制尚不清晰，作者希望通过数学模型揭示关键参数的调控作用。

Method: 建立基于CDK/CycB结合、CDC25/Wee1磷酸化调控的生化数学模型，经过模型简化后进行分析；通过参数空间探索确定振荡区域，并引入时间依赖的CycB合成进行数值模拟。

Result: 模型在特定参数区域内呈现振荡行为；数值模拟显示，当CycB合成随时间动态变化时，细胞周期时长逐渐增加，与实验观测的前14个周期延长现象定性一致。

Conclusion: CycB合成速率是控制果蝇胚胎细胞周期逐步延长的关键因素，APC激活系数也参与调控；该模型为理解胚胎细胞周期时序调控提供了理论框架。

Abstract: In the early stages of development, Drosophila melanogaster embryos possess very fast and well-coordinated cell cycles. In the cell cycle, CDK activity is essentially regulated by binding CDK and CycB to form an active complex and by phosphorylating CDK via CDC25 and dephosphorylating it via Wee1. We develop a mathematical model for the embryonic cell cycle which is biochemically sound and which can be rigorously analysed after a model reduction. We show that there exists a region in the parameter space where the model describes oscillations. We then focus on the role of two parameters: the CycB synthesis and the activation coefficient of APC. Our main biological hypothesis is that the first one is responsible for the period lengthening over the first 14 cycles which can be experimentally observed and this hypothesis is supported by numerical simulations of our model: if the CycB synthesis is made time-dependent with a prescribed dynamics, then our simulations show qualitatively a very similar behavior to experimental data reported in the literature.

</details>
