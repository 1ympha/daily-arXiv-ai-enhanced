### [1] [Regressor-guided Diffusion Model for De Novo Peptide Sequencing with Explicit Mass Control](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.20209)
*Shaorong Chen,Jingbo Zhou,Jun Xia*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: DiffuNovo：一种基于回归器引导扩散模型的新方法，通过显式肽段质量控制改进从头肽段测序，解决了现有方法中质量一致性约束不足的问题。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有深度学习模型在从头肽段测序中未能充分强制执行基本质量一致性约束，即预测肽段的质量必须与实验测量的前体质量匹配。现有方法通常将此关键信息作为简单输入特征或在后处理中使用，导致许多不符合这一基本物理属性的不可信预测。

Method: 提出DiffuNovo，一种新颖的回归器引导扩散模型，提供显式肽段级质量控制。该方法在两个关键阶段整合质量约束：训练期间使用新颖的肽段级质量损失指导模型优化；推理期间使用基于回归器的引导，通过潜在空间的梯度更新来引导生成，强制预测肽段遵守质量约束。

Result: 在已建立的基准测试上的综合评估表明，DiffuNovo在DNPS准确性方面超越了最先进的方法。作为首个采用扩散模型作为核心骨干的DNPS模型，DiffuNovo利用扩散架构的强大可控性，显著降低了质量误差，从而产生更符合物理实际的肽段。

Conclusion: 这些创新代表了向稳健且广泛适用的从头肽段测序的重大进展。DiffuNovo通过显式质量控制解决了现有方法的局限性，为蛋白质发现提供了更可靠的肽段识别工具。

Abstract: The discovery of novel proteins relies on sensitive protein identification, for which de novo peptide sequencing (DNPS) from mass spectra is a crucial approach. While deep learning has advanced DNPS, existing models inadequately enforce the fundamental mass consistency constraint, that a predicted peptide's mass must match the experimental measured precursor mass. Previous DNPS methods often treat this critical information as a simple input feature or use it in post-processing, leading to numerous implausible predictions that do not adhere to this fundamental physical property. To address this limitation, we introduce DiffuNovo, a novel regressor-guided diffusion model for de novo peptide sequencing that provides explicit peptide-level mass control. Our approach integrates the mass constraint at two critical stages: during training, a novel peptide-level mass loss guides model optimization, while at inference, regressor-based guidance from gradient-based updates in the latent space steers the generation to compel the predicted peptide adheres to the mass constraint. Comprehensive evaluations on established benchmarks demonstrate that DiffuNovo surpasses state-of-the-art methods in DNPS accuracy. Additionally, as the first DNPS model to employ a diffusion model as its core backbone, DiffuNovo leverages the powerful controllability of diffusion architecture and achieves a significant reduction in mass error, thereby producing much more physically plausible peptides. These innovations represent a substantial advancement toward robust and broadly applicable DNPS. The source code is available in the supplementary material.

### [2] [Unveiling Scaling Laws of Parameter Identifiability and Uncertainty Quantification in Data-Driven Biological Modeling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.20495)
*Shun Wang,Wenrui Hao*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 该研究提出了一个计算框架，通过渐近分析揭示控制实际可识别性的基本尺度定律，用于高维生物数据驱动的机制建模，确保可解释性和泛化性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将高维生物数据整合到数据驱动的机制建模中需要严格的实际可识别性，以确保可解释性和泛化性。然而，坐标可识别性分析经常在奇异局部极小值附近遇到数值不稳定性问题。

Method: 提出了一个计算框架，通过渐近分析揭示控制实际可识别性的基本尺度定律。该方法综合了Fisher信息与扰动Hessian矩阵，建立了一个分层方法来量化坐标可识别性，并在不同阶数上为非可识别子空间提供不确定性量化。

Result: 该框架在合成数据和真实数据上得到验证，并应用于HIV-宿主动力学和时空淀粉样蛋白β传播。这些应用展示了该框架在阐明HIV诊断和阿尔茨海默病进展关键机制方面的效率。

Conclusion: 在大规模机制数字孪生时代，该框架为数据驱动建模提供了参数可识别性和不确定性方面的尺度定律，确保数据驱动的推断基于可验证的生物现实。

Abstract: Integrating high-dimensional biological data into data-driven mechanistic modeling requires rigorous practical identifiability to ensure interpretability and generalizability. However, coordinate identifiability analysis often suffers from numerical instabilities near singular local minimizers. We present a computational framework that uncovers fundamental scaling laws governing practical identifiability through asymptotic analysis. By synthesizing Fisher information with perturbed Hessian matrices, we establish a hierarchical approach to quantify coordinate identifiability and inform uncertainty quantification within non-identifiable subspaces across different orders. Supported by rigorous mathematical analysis and validated on synthetic and real-world data, our framework was applied to HIV-host dynamics and spatiotemporal amyloid-beta propagation. These applications demonstrate the framework's efficiency in elucidating critical mechanisms underlying HIV diagnostics and Alzheimer's disease progression. In the era of large-scale mechanistic digital twins, our framework provides the scaling laws for data-driven modeling in terms of both parameter identifiability and uncertainty, ensuring that data-driven inferences are grounded in verifiable biological reality.

### [3] [Tipping points in complex ecological systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.20702)
*Alan Hastings,Sergei Petrovskii,Valerio Lucarini,Andrew Morozov*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 该论文对复杂系统中"临界点"概念进行了批判性综述，重点回顾了过去15年在该领域的研究进展，特别以生态系统作为复杂非线性开放系统的范例。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 临界点研究是现代复杂系统物理学的热点话题，但对其定义和机制的理解仍存在多样性。论文旨在系统梳理临界点科学的发展，澄清不同数学结构下的临界点行为，特别关注空间扩展系统和多尺度系统中的临界点级联现象。

Method: 采用批判性综述方法，以生态系统为范例，系统分析过去15年临界点研究的主要成果。通过比较不同数学结构（如分岔诱导临界点等）和不同环境强迫条件下的临界机制，揭示临界点科学的理论框架。

Result: 识别了临界点研究中的主要发现，包括：1）临界点定义的多样性；2）不同数学结构展现临界行为的差异；3）空间扩展系统中临界点级联现象；4）不同环境强迫对临界机制的影响。同时指出了当前知识体系中的空白领域。

Conclusion: 论文为临界点科学提供了系统性综述，强调了临界点概念的复杂性及其在不同系统环境中的多样性表现。通过识别知识空白并制定研究路线图，为未来临界点研究提供了理论指导和发展方向。

Abstract: Tipping points are one of the hot topics in modern physics of complex systems. But what is a tipping point? A generic definition declares it as ``a state of the system where a small change in its parameters can lead to a significant change in its properties''. Additional ingredients that often enter the definition of tipping process are the abruptness of the resulting change and its irreversibility, i.e. it is impossible to recover the initial state if one reverses the protocol of change of the parameters. However, there exists a number of different mathematical structures that can show this behavior, the one that was originally suggested as a tipping point (nowadays usually referred to as bifurcation induced tipping) is just one of many. Different preconditions and/or different level of details included into the model, reflecting also different environmental forcing, can lead to a variety of tipping mechanisms. Furthermore, in a spatially extended system and/or a system with multiple scales, different parts can react to a change in environmental conditions differently or at a different time, interacting with each other to create a tipping cascade. In this paper, using ecosystems as a paradigm of complex nonlinear open systems, we provide a critical overview of the progress made in tipping point science over the last 15 years. We highlight the main findings, identify gaps in our knowledge, and outline a roadmap for further progress.

### [4] [Age Structured Epidemic Model under Vaccination with Vector Transmission](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.21150)
*Sourav Banerjee,Thomas Götz,Satyananda Panda*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文建立了一个考虑年龄结构和疫苗接种时间的登革热传播模型，通过固定点理论证明了在弱传播条件下地方性平衡点的存在唯一性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 登革热因其高死亡率和经济负担仍是全球重大公共卫生问题。数学建模对于理解其传播机制和评估干预策略至关重要。现有模型需要更好地整合年龄结构和疫苗接种时间对传播动态的影响。

Method: 构建了一个向量宿主模型，其中人群按年龄分层，接种者进一步按接种时间描述。蚊群动态与宿主动态耦合，在准稳态假设下简化。通过积分疫苗接种年龄，得到非线性稳态公式，将地方性平衡表示为感染蚊群数量的固定点问题。利用Lipschitz估计和收缩论证，在弱传播条件下建立平衡点的存在唯一性。

Result: 成功证明了在弱传播条件下，考虑年龄依赖疫苗接种的登革热模型存在唯一的地方性平衡点。分析揭示了年龄结构疫苗接种对长期登革热动态的影响。

Conclusion: 该研究为理解年龄结构疫苗接种对登革热传播的长期影响提供了理论框架，证明了在合理条件下模型存在唯一的稳态解，有助于评估疫苗接种策略的长期效果。

Abstract: Dengue remains a major global public health concern due to its high mortality and economic burden. Mathematical modeling is essential to understand its transmission mechanisms and for evaluating intervention strategies. In this paper, we formulate a vector host model in which the human population is structured by age, and vaccinated individuals are further described by time since vaccination. The mosquito population is coupled to the host dynamics and reduced under a quasi steady state assumption. By integrating over vaccination age, we obtain a nonlinear steady state formulation and express the endemic equilibrium as a fixed point problem for the infected mosquito population. Using Lipschitz estimates and a contraction argument, we establish existence and uniqueness of the equilibrium under a weak transmission condition. The analysis highlights the influence of age dependent vaccination on long term dengue dynamics.
