  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 对异二聚体模型的朊病毒样传播动力学进行数学分析：推导混合相的精确内解并与健康相的外解匹配；建立毒性稳态附近的准稳态简化；将动力学简化为逻辑增长方程。

Result: 获得了混合相和毒性相的完整解析描述：解释了健康物种的凹形行为；建立了毒性物种指数增长的显式条件和机制可解释的增长速率；证明了动力学可简化为逻辑增长方程，连接了指数增长和饱和过程。

Furthermore, we formalise a quasi steady-state reduction near the toxic steady state and show that the dynamics reduce to a logistic growth equation, linking exponential growth to saturation.
  Together, these results provide a unified and mechanistic description of prion-like dynamics across all phases of disease progression and establish a foundation for predictive modelling of biomarker trajectories.

When new traits appear with accumulating mutations, we find that variations in fitness in range expansion may be described by the Tracy--Widom distribution.
