### [1] [When evolution realizes large deviations of fitness: from speciation to dynamical phase transitions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.04325)
*Sara Dal Cengio,Quentin Laurenceau,Vivien Lecomte,Charline Smadi,Julien Tailleur*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 该论文探索了进化与大型偏差理论之间的联系，通过研究Moran模型变体中的进化动力学，发现在大种群极限下，繁殖和选择的影响相当于实现了非相互作用随机游走的大型偏差动力学。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索进化生物学与大型偏差理论之间的理论联系，理解进化过程中繁殖和选择的数学本质。

Method: 使用Moran模型的变体研究进化动力学，包括突变、繁殖和选择过程。在大种群极限下，将繁殖和选择的影响映射到非相互作用随机游走的大型偏差动力学。

Result: 发现繁殖和选择的影响相当于实现了非相互作用随机游走的大型偏差动力学，这种映射在所有时间尺度上都成立。可以将种群基因组分布的转变重新解释为动态相变，并使用大型偏差理论的工具箱进行研究。

Conclusion: 建立了进化动力学与大型偏差理论之间的理论联系，这种映射不仅适用于Moran模型，还可以扩展到更广泛的模型类别，为研究进化过程提供了新的数学框架。

Abstract: We explore the connection between evolution and large-deviation theory. To do so, we study evolutionary dynamics in which individuals experience mutations, reproduction, and selection using variants of the Moran model. We show that, in the large population size limit, the impact of reproduction and selection amounts to realizing a large-deviation dynamics for the non-interacting random walk in which individuals simply explore the genome landscape due to mutations. This mapping, which holds at all times, allows us to recast transitions in the population genome distribution as dynamical phase transitions, which can then be studied using the toolbox of large-deviation theory. Finally, we show that the mapping extends beyond the class of Moran models.

### [2] [Cell size control in bacteria is modulated through extrinsic noise, single-cell- and population-growth](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.05193)
*Arthur Genthon,Philipp Thomas*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 该研究通过两个随机映射统一了细胞大小控制的现象学模型，揭示了群体动力学如何改变母机实验中测量的大小控制，并发现细菌主要通过外在噪声机制维持大小稳态，优先保持狭窄的分裂大小分布而非最大化生长速率。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 细胞通过主动补偿大小波动来维持大小稳态，但现有模型未能统一整合单细胞生长率波动和大小依赖性噪声机制，且母机实验与群体动态之间的关系尚不明确。

Method: 提出了两个随机映射：一个适用于母机谱系，另一个适用于指数生长细胞群体的谱系树。通过分析细菌数据，识别了外在噪声作为大小变异性的主导机制，并推导了群体生长率增益与分裂大小噪声之间的权衡关系。

Result: 群体动力学显著改变母机实验中测量的大小控制特性；细菌数据表明外在噪声是大小变异性的主要机制，表现为分裂大小的条件方差-均值呈二次关系；细菌优先保持狭窄的分裂大小分布而非最大化生长速率。

Conclusion: 该研究统一了细胞大小控制的现象学模型，揭示了群体动态对大小控制测量的影响，识别了外在噪声的主导作用，并表明细菌在进化权衡中优先维持大小稳态而非最大化生长速率。

Abstract: Living cells maintain size homeostasis by actively compensating for size fluctuations. Here, we present two stochastic maps that unify phenomenological models by integrating fluctuating single-cell growth rates and size-dependent noise mechanisms with cell size control. One map is applicable to mother machine lineages and the other to lineage trees of exponentially-growing cell populations, which reveals that population dynamics alter size control measured in mother machine experiments. For example, an adder can become more sizer-like or more timer-like at the population level depending on the noise statistics. Our analysis of bacterial data identifies extrinsic noise as the dominant mechanism of size variability, characterized by a quadratic conditional variance-mean relationship for division size across growth conditions. This finding contradicts the reported independence of added size relative to birth size but is consistent with the adder property in terms of the independence of the mean added size. Finally, we derive a trade-off between population-growth-rate gain and division-size noise. Correlations between size control quantifiers and single-cell growth rates inferred from data indicate that bacteria prioritize a narrow division-size distribution over growth rate maximisation.

### [3] [Biomechanically Informed Image Registration for Patient-Specific Aortic Valve Strain Analysis](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.04375)
*Mohsen Nakhaei,Alison Pouch,Silvani Amin,Matthew Daemer,Christian Herz,Natalie Yushkevich,Lourdes Al Ghofaily,Nimesh Desai,Joseph Bavaria,Matthew Jolley,Wensi Wu*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 该研究开发了一种结合图像配准与有限元方法的新框架，用于提高主动脉瓣膜追踪和生物力学评估的准确性，在20名患者中比直接配准方法提高了40%的准确性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 主动脉瓣生物力学对维持正常心脏功能至关重要，特别是二叶式主动脉瓣的病理变化会改变瓣叶负荷、增加应变并加速疾病进展。当前成像和计算方法往往无法捕捉快速瓣膜运动和复杂的患者特异性特征，需要更准确的瓣膜几何和变形表征来预测疾病进展并指导修复。

Method: 结合图像配准与有限元方法，使用4D经食道超声心动图和CT获取的患者特异性瓣膜几何进行有限元建模，模拟主动脉瓣关闭并生成中间变形状态。有限元生成的状态促进瓣叶追踪，而配准算法校正模拟与图像之间的不匹配。

Result: 在20名患者中，有限元增强配准比直接配准提高了40%的准确性（TEE提高33%，CT提高46%）。三叶式成人瓣膜显示均匀变形，二叶式瓣膜呈现不对称应变，儿科瓣膜具有低平均面积应变但高变异性。三叶式成人和儿科瓣膜在平均有效应变上的趋同表明体积变形驱动了年龄和大小相关的差异。

Conclusion: 有限元增强配准框架提高了几何追踪能力，为患者特异性主动脉瓣变形提供了临床相关见解，支持个体化干预规划。该方法减少了边界条件和材料假设的不确定性，实现了直接从成像进行更可靠的应变估计。

Pathological variations, particularly in bicuspid aortic valves (BAVs), alter leaflet loading, increase strain, and accelerate disease progression. Accurate, patient-specific characterization of valve geometry and deformation is essential for predicting disease progression and guiding durable repair. Current imaging and computational methods often fail to capture rapid valve motion and complex patient-specific features. To address these challenges, we combined image registration with finite element method (FEM) to enhance AV tracking and biomechanical assessment. The FEM-generated states facilitated leaflet tracking, while the registration algorithm corrected mismatches between simulation and image. Across 20 patients, FEM-augmented registration improved accuracy by 40% compared with direct registration (33% for TEE, 46% for CT). This improvement enabled more reliable strain estimation directly from imaging and reducing uncertainties from boundary conditions and material assumptions. Areal and Green-Lagrange strains, as well as effective strain, were quantified in adult trileaflet/bicuspid, and pediatric patients. Trileaflet adults showed uniform deformation, BAVs exhibited asymmetric strain, and pediatric valves had low mean areal strain with high variability. Convergence between trileaflet adult and pediatric valves in mean effective strain suggests volumetric deformation drives age- and size-related differences. The FEM-augmented registration framework enhances geometric tracking and provides clinically relevant insights into patient-specific AV deformation, supporting individualized intervention planning.

### [4] [Identifying expanding TCR clonotypes with a longitudinal Bayesian mixture model and their associations with cancer patient prognosis, metastasis-directed therapy, and VJ gene enrichment](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.04536)

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种贝叶斯纵向混合模型，用于分析T细胞受体克隆动态变化，避免了传统Fisher精确检验的成对比较限制，并应用于前列腺癌转移导向治疗的研究中。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 当前分析T细胞受体克隆动态变化主要使用Fisher精确检验，但这种方法需要成对的、患者内比较，存在局限性。需要一种更灵活的方法来分析克隆在治疗干预下的扩张或收缩动态。

Method: 开发了一种灵活的贝叶斯纵向混合模型，能够处理不同长度的患者随访数据，处理缺失值，避免因结构性问题而遗漏数据。模型将克隆分为动态（扩张或收缩）和静态类别，并开发了高维惩罚对数线性模型分析受体基序和VJ基因富集组合。

Abstract: Examination of T-cell receptor (TCR) clonality has become a way of understanding immunologic response to cancer and its interventions in recent years. An aspect of these analyses is determining which receptors expand or contract statistically significantly as a function of an exogenous perturbation such as therapeutic intervention. Once clones are partitioned by the model into dynamic (expanding or contracting) and static categories, one can associate their counts or other characteristics with disease state, interventions, baseline biomarkers, and patient prognosis. We apply these developments to a cohort of prostate cancer patients who underwent randomized metastasis-directed therapy or not. Our analyses reveal a significant increase in clonal expansions among MDT patients and their association with later progressions both independent and within strata of MDT. Analysis of receptor motifs and VJ gene enrichment combinations using a high-dimensional penalized log-linear model we develop also suggests distinct biological characteristics of expanding clones, with and without inducement by MDT.
