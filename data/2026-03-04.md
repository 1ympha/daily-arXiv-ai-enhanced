### [1] [Fluctuating environments are sufficient to drive substantial variability in species abundance across locations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02403)
*James F. D. Henderson,Andreas Tiffeau-Mayer*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 研究物种在时空变化环境中丰度分布的非平衡效应，通过最小化模型分析环境波动如何影响物种在不同地点的丰度差异。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 物种在时空变化的环境中生长时，即使没有持久的位置偏好，其丰度在不同地点也会存在差异。本研究旨在量化这种非平衡效应，理解环境波动如何影响物种的空间分布模式。

Method: 采用最小化模型研究空间分隔群落，假设时间平均中性竞争但存在位置依赖的环境波动。分析推导两点不等式的分布（定义为物种在两个地点丰度的对数比），研究迁移松弛与波动强度之间的平衡如何影响这些分布的统计特性。

Result: 在双斑块和无限斑块情况下，表征了迁移松弛与波动强度平衡如何决定分布的整体和极值统计特性。发现了噪声诱导的双峰不等式转变，该转变取决于环境波动的相关时间尺度。

Conclusion: 在具有时间相关性的环境中，有限迁移率具有进化优势。研究揭示了环境波动如何塑造物种的空间分布模式，为理解非平衡生态过程提供了理论框架。

Abstract: Species growing in environments that change in time and space will vary in their abundance across locations, even in the absence of persistent location preferences. Here we quantify this non-equilibrium effect by studying a minimal model of a spatially compartmentalised community with time-averaged-neutral competition but location-dependent environmental fluctuations. We analytically derive distributions of two-point inequality, defined as the log-ratio of a species' abundance across a pair of locations. We characterise how the balance of relaxation via migration and fluctuation strength determine the bulk and extreme value statistics of these distributions in the two-patch and infinite-patch cases. We demonstrate the existence of a noise-induced transition to bimodal inequality, which depends on the correlation timescale of the environmental fluctuations. Finally, we discuss the evolutionary benefit of finite migration rates in environments with temporal correlations.

### [2] [Recombination Rate Modifiers under Stochastic Transmission](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02496)
*Elisa Heinrich-Mora,Marcus W. Feldman*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 研究发现在随机重组率变化下，经典的"减少原则"可能被逆转，即使没有选择波动，时间变异本身就能改变重组率演化的方向。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 经典的减少原则假设重组率在世代间恒定，但实际生物系统中重组率可能存在随机波动。本研究旨在探索当重组率随机变化时，中性修饰基因的演化动态是否会改变。

Method: 使用二倍体模型，两个位点受对称乘法适合度选择，第三个中性位点调节重组率。所有参数固定，但修饰基因杂合子的重组率按随机过程在世代间变化。通过随机矩阵乘积的顶部Lyapunov指数分析入侵条件。

Result: 当重组率恒定时，减少原则成立：降低重组的修饰基因被选择。但当重组率随机变化时，仅凭平均重组率不足以决定入侵结果，需要完整分布和有序积累。时间变异本身就能逆转确定性模型的预测方向。

Conclusion: 重组率的时间变异是重组率演化中一个独立且性质不同的力量，能够改变经典减少原则的预测，表明传输参数的随机波动对演化动态有重要影响。

Abstract: The Reduction Principle states that, near a stable equilibrium under fixed viability selection, a selectively neutral modifier allele that reduces recombination rate among selected loci is favored, whereas one that increases recombination rate is eliminated. This result assumes constant transmission parameters across generations, so that invasion is determined by the dominant eigenvalue of a single transmission-selection matrix. Here we analyze a minimal departure from this framework. In a diploid model, two loci experience symmetric multiplicative viability selection and a third, neutral locus modifies their recombination rate. All parameters are fixed except that recombination in modifier heterozygotes varies randomly across generations according to a stochastic process. When the recombination rate in modifier heterozygotes is constant, the Reduction Principle holds exactly: invasion occurs if the rare modifier allele reduces recombination relative to the resident rate. When recombination varies randomly across generations, invasion is governed by the top Lyapunov exponent of a product of random matrices. We show that temporal variation in recombination rate alone, in the absence of fluctuating viability selection, can reverse the direction of selection on the modifier locus predicted by the deterministic model. The mean recombination rate is insufficient to determine invasion of $M_2$; instead, outcomes depend on the full distribution of recombination rates and their ordered accumulation across generations. Parameters that affect only the magnitude of selection under constant transmission - including resident recombination, selection strength, and background linkage - can alter its sign under stochastic transmission. These results demonstrate that temporal variability in transmission constitutes an independent and qualitatively distinct force in the evolution of recombination rates.

  <summary>Details</summary>

Conclusion: 该研究不仅为理解肿瘤-巨噬细胞相互作用的动态机制提供了理论框架，还提出了两个潜在的临床预后标志物：M1型巨噬细胞浸润水平和M3型巨噬细胞激活峰值时间。这些发现有助于开发新的癌症治疗策略和预后评估工具。

Abstract: The interplay between tumor cells and macrophages plays a central regulatory role in cancer progression. In this study, we developed a mathematical model that incorporates tumor cells, M1 type macrophages, M2 type macrophages and an M3 type macrophage population characterized by dual phenotypic features. Notably, using approximate Bayesian computation for parameter inference and dynamic simulations, the model successfully recapitulated the evolutionary trajectories of eight tumor samples. The results demonstrate that lower tumor burden is significantly associated with higher M1 type macrophage infiltration and delayed peak time of M3 type macrophage activation. Moreover, survival analysis indicated that both enhanced M1 type macrophage infiltration and delayed peak time of M3 type macrophage activation are correlated with longer survival time. In summary, this study not only provides a theoretical framework for understanding the dynamic mechanisms underlying tumor macrophage interactions but also proposes two potential clinical prognostic markers: the level of M1 type macrophage infiltration and the peak time of M3 type macrophage activation.

### [4] [GPU-accelerated single-cell analysis at scale with rapids-singlecell](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02402)
*Severin Dicks,Lukas Heumos,Lilly May,Sara Jimenez,Philipp Angerer,Ilan Gold,Isaac Virshup,Felix Fischer,Michelle Gill,Melanie Boerries,Corey J Nolet,Tiffany J. Chen,Fabian J. Theis*

Main category: q-bio.GN

TL;DR: rapids-singlecell是一个GPU加速的单细胞分析框架，通过集成到scverse生态系统并使用AnnData数据结构，实现了数百倍的速度提升，将分析时间从小时级缩短到分钟级。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 随着单细胞数据集规模达到数千万细胞级别，基于CPU的分析流程面临严重的可扩展性瓶颈，处理时间长达数小时甚至数天，限制了交互式数据探索和迭代假设测试。

Method: 基于CuPy数组和NVIDIA CUDA-X Data Science (RAPIDS)生态系统构建，提供与scanpy核心分析步骤几乎直接替换的GPU实现，完全集成到scverse生态系统并直接操作AnnData数据结构。

Result: 在预处理、降维、邻域图构建、聚类和批次校正等标准单细胞分析流程中，相比优化的CPU基线实现了高达数百倍的速度提升，分析时间从小时缩短到分钟，同时保持一致的生物学解释。

Conclusion: rapids-singlecell的性能改进使得大规模单细胞数据集能够近乎实时分析，无需数据分割，结合实时参数调整和迭代工作流程，实现了交互式大规模单细胞分析的可能性。

Abstract: Single-cell sequencing technologies reveal cellular heterogeneity at high resolution, advancing our understanding of biological complexity. These scalability limitations fundamentally restrict common interactive data exploration and iterative hypothesis testing. Here we introduce rapids-singlecell, a GPU-accelerated framework that integrates natively with the scverse ecosystem and operates directly on the AnnData data structure, which delivers orders-of-magnitude speedups for single-cell workflows. Built on CuPy arrays and the NVIDIA CUDA-X Data Science (RAPIDS) ecosystem, rapids-singlecell provides near drop-in GPU replacements for core scanpy-based analysis steps. Across standard single-cell workflows such as preprocessing, dimensionality reduction, neighborhood graph construction, clustering, and batch correction, rapids-singlecell achieves speedups of up to several hundred-fold compared to optimized CPU baselines. This reduces analysis time from hours to minutes on standard hardware, while maintaining consistent biological interpretations. These performance improvements make it possible to analyze large data sets in close to real time, without the need for data splitting. Together with real-time parameter tuning and iterative workflows, rapids-singlecell makes interactive large-scale single-cell analysis possible.

### [5] [Contextual Invertible World Models: A Neuro-Symbolic Agentic Framework for Colorectal Cancer Drug Response](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02274)
*Christopher Baker,Karen Rafferty,Hui Wang*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种神经符号智能体框架，通过整合定量机器学习世界模型和基于LLM的智能体推理层，解决精准肿瘤学中的小样本高维度数据问题，实现可解释的AI药物敏感性预测。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 精准肿瘤学面临小样本高维度数据的矛盾，深度学习模型虽然预测准确但缺乏可解释性，无法提供临床决策所需的因果机制。

Method: 开发神经符号智能体框架，整合定量机器学习世界模型和LLM智能体推理层，使用Sanger GDSC数据集（N=83），通过显式建模临床背景（如MSI状态），并引入逆向推理概念进行CRISPR扰动模拟。

Result: 系统实现了稳健的预测相关性（r=0.504），通过临床背景建模获得显著性能提升，能够区分治疗机会和背景耐药性，并在人类临床数据中得到验证（p=0.023）。

We present a Neuro-Symbolic Agentic Framework that bridges this gap by integrating a quantitative machine learning World Model with an LLM-based agentic reasoning layer. Our system utilises a forensic data pipeline built on the Sanger GDSC dataset (N=83), achieving a robust predictive correlation (r=0.504) and a significant performance gain through the explicit modelling of clinical context, specifically Microsatellite Instability (MSI) status. We introduce the concept of Inverse Reasoning, where the agentic layer performs in silico CRISPR perturbations to predict how specific genomic edits, such as APC or TP53 repair, alter drug sensitivity. By distinguishing between therapeutic opportunity and contextual resistance, and validating these findings against human clinical data (p=0.023), our framework provides a transparent, biologically grounded path towards explainable AI in cancer research.

  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 基于大量实验室跟踪实验数据，应用随机建模方法分析长腿蚂蚁运动，结合主动布朗运动和跑停模型来模拟轨迹统计特征。
