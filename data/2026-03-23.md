### [1] [Assessing 3D tree model quality and species classification using imbalance indices](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.19341)
*Sophie J. Kersting,Mareike Fischer*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 该研究将系统发育树平衡指数应用于3D树木模型分析，结合传统3D指标，提高了森林监测中异常检测和物种分类的准确性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究动机是探索如何利用系统发育树（非3D）的平衡指数来补充3D树木模型分析，为森林监测提供更全面的视角，并提高模型质量控制和物种分类的准确性。

Method: 方法包括：1）使用英国Wytham Woods的"虚拟森林"数据集；2）评估3D模型质量和物种分类性能；3）将系统发育树平衡指数与3D不平衡指数结合应用；4）开发计算高效、可重复的工作流程；5）更新R包treeDbalance以包含相关功能。

Result: 结果显示：1）系统发育树平衡指数可成功应用于有机树木的3D模型；2）结合3D和非3D指数能提供互补视角，改善异常检测；3）物种分类模型准确率最高达81.8%；4）添加过滤后的3D和非3D统计量能持续改善结果，特别是对少数物种类别；5）提供了计算高效的质量控制步骤。

Conclusion: 结论是系统发育树平衡指数与3D不平衡指数的结合应用为3D树木模型分析提供了有价值的补充工具，提高了森林监测的准确性和效率，相关功能已集成到R包treeDbalance中供研究社区使用。

Abstract: We investigate the use of additional 3D and phylogenetic non-3D tree balance indices for analyzing and monitoring forests using an exemplary "virtual forest" dataset from the Wytham Woods, Oxford, UK. This study assesses 3D model quality, species classification performance, and the relevance of these indices. Our study shows that indices stemming from the study of ancestry trees of species can be successfully applied to 3D models of organic trees and, accompanied with recently introduced 3D imbalance indices, offer a complementary perspective on 3D tree models and improve the detection of deviations. Their computational efficiency combined with the simple and reproducible workflow presented in this manuscript form a computationally feasible quality control step in the 3D model construction.
  Species classification models reached an estimated accuracy of up to 81.8% and allowed to make confident species predictions for a large portion of the unlabeled trees in the dataset. While conventional tree metrics can already provide strong predictive performance, the addition of filtered 3D and non-3D statistics improved results consistently, particularly for minority species classes.
  Alongside this manuscript, we provide updated functionality in the R package treeDbalance to include the necessary functionalities and release the derived index datasets and species predictions.
