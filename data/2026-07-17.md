### [1] [A vision foundation model for single-cell biology via spatial gene cartography](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.14163)
*Ridvan Yesiloglu,Sakib Mostafa,James Zou,Ash Alizadeh,Jiajun Wu,Lei Xing,Ehsan Adeli,Md Tauhidul Islam*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: scVision是一个将单细胞转录组数据转换为连续图像的视觉基础模型，通过在固定布局上排列基因（使共表达基因成为空间邻居），利用视觉Transformer进行掩码图像预训练，在零样本细胞类型注释、基因程序恢复和多研究整合中优于现有方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有单细胞基础模型将细胞表示为基因令牌序列，忽略了基因间的关系和表达量大小，作者希望保留这些信息，将单细胞表征学习重新定义为视觉问题。

Method: 使用最优传输将基因放置在共享的泛组织布局上，使共表达基因成为空间邻居，将转录组转为连续图像；在7200万个人类细胞上通过掩码图像建模预训练视觉Transformer，冻结编码器不做微调。

Result: 在六个独立保留研究的零样本评估中，scVision是最准确的细胞类型注释器，能在无监督下恢复基因程序；在多研究整合中匹配最强的令牌模型同时保留最多生物学结构。打乱基因布局比移除视觉Transformer本身更严重地降低准确性。

Conclusion: 通过保留表达量和基因关系，scVision将单细胞表征学习重新框架为视觉问题，连接了计算机视觉的成熟方法，证明了生物学上有意义的位置信息（而非网络架构）承载了主要信号。

Abstract: Most single-cell foundation models are adapted from language models, representing each cell as a sequence of gene tokens. This discards the relationships among genes and often the magnitude of their expression. We present scVision, a vision foundation model that instead renders each cell as a continuous image. Using optimal transport, it places genes at fixed positions on a single shared, pan-tissue layout so that co-expressed genes become spatial neighbours, turning a transcriptome into an image in which gene programs appear as local texture. We pretrain a vision transformer by masked image modelling on 72 million human cells and use the frozen encoder with no fine-tuning. In zero-shot evaluations on six independent, held-out studies, scVision is the most accurate cell-type annotator and recovers gene programs without supervision, ahead of existing foundation models and classical baselines; on multi-study integration it matches the strongest token-based model while conserving the most biological structure, without ever seeing a batch label. Permuting the gene layout with the network fixed sharply lowers accuracy, more than removing the vision transformer itself, showing that biologically meaningful position, not the network, carries the signal. By preserving expression magnitude and gene relationships, scVision reframes single-cell representation learning as a vision problem, connecting it to the mature methods of computer vision.

### [2] [Analyzing Post-transcriptional Regulation in Stochastic Gene Expression Models Using Partitioned Poisson Arrivals](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.14404)
*Kenny Wong,Argenis Arriojas,Sho Inaba,Hodjat Pendar,Abhyudai Singh,Rahul Kulkarni*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种将转录后调控模型映射为启动子调控模型的解析框架，从而利用已知的mRNA分布精确结果计算蛋白质分布的矩（在某些情况下可得到完整分布），并扩展至转录爆发模式。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 尽管已有针对启动子调控的mRNA矩和分布的精确解析框架，但蛋白质波动的类似框架尚缺失，因此需要建立统一的理论工具来量化转录后调控对基因表达噪声的影响。

Method: 利用泊松到达的分配性质，将一类通用的转录后调控随机模型映射为类似启动子调控的模型，从而借用已知的mRNA分布精确结果；进一步扩展引入转录爆发机制。

Result: 获得了蛋白质分布矩的精确解析结果，并在某些情况下得到了完整的蛋白质分布；该方法统一了转录后调控与启动子调控的分析框架。

Conclusion: 该框架为分析转录后调控在随机基因表达中的作用提供了通用且精确的数学工具，有助于深入理解基因表达噪声的调控机制。

Abstract: Gene expression is a stochastic process that allows for fluctuations in protein levels that can give rise to phenotypic heterogeneity within a population of genetically identical cells. Thus, there is great interest in quantifying how natural variation (noise) in gene expression is impacted by cellular control mechanisms, such as the various mechanisms pertaining to post-transcriptional regulation. Although previous research has developed a general analytical framework to compute the exact moments of mRNA distributions for any promoter-based regulatory motif, and the exact mRNA distribution itself in some cases, a similar framework for protein fluctuations is currently lacking. Here, we invoke the partitioning property of Poisson arrivals to map a general class of stochastic models of post-transcriptional regulation onto models that resemble promoter-based regulation. This approach leads to exact analytical results for the moments of protein distributions, and in certain cases the full distribution itself, using known exact results for mRNA distributions undergoing arbitrary promoter-based regulation. We further extend the framework to incorporate transcriptional bursting, leading to a versatile, unifying analytical framework for analyzing post-transcriptional regulation in stochastic gene expression.

### [3] [Infectious Disease Induces Emergent Oscillations, Extinction and Changes in Community Persistence in a Food Chain](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.14360)
*Hooman Saveh,Fakhteh Ghanbarnejad*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本论文研究了将传染病（SIR模型）引入由广义Lotka-Volterra方程描述的食物链（猎物、捕食者、顶级捕食者）中的影响，发现捕食者感染时不会灭绝但会出现种群振荡，顶级捕食者感染时可能灭绝且群落持续性变化不同。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有食物网模型大多未同时考虑传染病的影响，因此作者希望将SIR模型引入食物链，研究传染病对食物链动态的影响。

Method: 基于广义Lotka-Volterra方程构建包含猎物、捕食者和顶级捕食者的食物链模型，对其中某一物种引入SIR模型，并设置参数w以增加感染者的被捕食率、降低其捕食率。

Result: 捕食者感染时不会灭绝，但特定条件下出现种群振荡；顶级捕食者感染时不出现振荡，但特定参数范围内可能灭绝；捕食者的群落持续性增加，顶级捕食者的群落持续性降低。

Conclusion: 传染病在食物链不同层级的影响存在显著差异：捕食者感染导致振荡而非灭绝，顶级捕食者感染则可能导致灭绝，且群落持续性变化相反。

Abstract: Food webs have been extensively studied from both ecological and mathematical aspects. However, most of the models studied in this area do not capture the effects of infectious diseases simultaneously. Recently, the idea of including an infectious disease in a food web model has been investigated. We study and simulate a small food chain consisting of only prey, predators, and apex predators governed by the generalized Lotka-Volterra equations, and we implement the Susceptible-Infected-Recovered (SIR) model on only one of the species at a time in the food chain. To study the effects of an infectious disease on the food chain, we introduce a new parameter that increases the predation rate by a factor of $w$ and decreases the hunting rate by a factor of $1/w$ for infected species. When the infectious disease is present in predators, we observe that predators do not become extinct under any set of parameters; however, an oscillation in their population size occurs under some circumstances, which we do not observe in ordinary SIR or the generalized Lotka-Volterra equations alone. When an infectious disease is present in apex predators, oscillations in the population size do not happen; but if the set of parameters is in a specific range the apex predators may become extinct. Furthermore, the chance of survival of the community, known as community persistence, increases for the predators and decreases for the apex predators.

### [4] [Revisiting a random model of lateral gene transfer in phylogenetics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.14653)
*Laura Kubatko,Simone Linz,Kristina Wicke*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文重新审视了水平基因转移（LGT）的泊松过程模型，推导了2-3物种情况下的基因树和位点模式概率，探讨了模型可识别性及LGT与不完全谱系分选（ILS）的可区分性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 尽管多物种溯祖模型对ILS有丰富的理论理解，但LGT的概率模型受到的关注相对较少，因此需要重新审视基本的LGT模型并探讨其可识别性问题。

Method: 基于LGT事件服从恒定速率的泊松过程这一基本模型，聚焦于两物种和三物种的最简情况，推导基因树概率和位点模式概率，并进行理论分析。

Result: 得到了2-3物种下基因树和位点模式概率的具体表达式，明确了模型的可识别性条件，并讨论了LGT与ILS在概率模型下能否区分的问题。

Conclusion: 该工作为LGT的概率建模提供了理论基础，指出了实证应用方向及未来工作的拓展路径。

Abstract: Processes such as incomplete lineage sorting (ILS) and reticulate evolution (arising, for example, from hybridization and lateral gene transfer (LGT)) are known to cause discordance between gene trees and species trees, complicating phylogenetic inference. While the multispecies coalescent model has led to a rich theoretical understanding of ILS, probabilistic models for LGT have received comparatively less attention. Here, we revisit a basic LGT model in which random LGT events occur according to a Poisson process with a constant transfer rate. Focusing on the simplest cases of two and three species, we derive gene tree and site pattern probabilities and discuss their implications for model identifiability. We also address the question of whether LGT and ILS can be distinguished from one another under these probabilistic models. We discuss empirical applications and outline directions for future work.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 确定性进化博弈论无法区分单态种群与多态种群，而颤抖手假说认为人口噪声选择单态性，但仿真研究结果矛盾，需要理论解释。

Abstract: Deterministic evolutionary game theory makes no distinction between a monomorphic population of individuals all of whom share a mixed evolutionarily stable strategy and a polymorphic population of players of pure strategies present in a ratio that reproduces the mixed strategy on average. ### [7] [Topology-Informed Survival Analysis of Breast Cancer Patients Using the Mapper Algorithm](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15022)
*Emmanuel Kibisi,Olakunle Abawonse,Donald Woukeng*

Main category: q-bio.GN

TL;DR: 本研究利用拓扑数据分析中的Mapper算法，对超过1000名TCGA-BRCA患者的基因表达数据进行分析，发现了与生存相关的隐藏分子模式，并识别出传统分类方法无法捕捉的预后信息。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有乳腺癌分类方法无法完全揭示基因表达数据中隐藏的分子模式与生存之间的复杂关系，需要更强大的数学工具来挖掘传统方法遗漏的临床预后信息。

Method: 采用拓扑数据分析中的Mapper算法，对1000多名TCGA-BRCA患者的基因表达数据构建拓扑网络，识别高风险区域和患者亚群，并通过训练集和测试集验证风险分组的预后价值。

Result: 高风险区域患者生存显著更差；高增殖基因表达模式与更差预后相关但治疗缩小了差距；发现了一组预后与预期不符的Basal-like亚型患者，其良好预后与独特的治疗响应基因特征相关；拓扑衍生风险分组在调整年龄、分期和治疗后仍显著预测生存。

Conclusion: 基因表达数据的几何结构包含超越传统乳腺癌分类方法的临床预后信息，拓扑数据分析能发现传统方法遗漏的分子程序和有临床意义的患者亚群。

Abstract: This study applied a mathematical tool from Topological Data Analysis (TDA), called the Mapper algorithm, to gene expression data from more than 1,000 TCGA-BRCA patients to identify hidden molecular patterns associated with survival. Patients located near high-risk regions of the network showed significantly poorer survival, and highly proliferative gene expression patterns were associated with worse outcomes overall, although treatment narrowed this survival gap across proliferation groups. The analysis further uncovered patients whose survival outcomes were inconsistent with their expected clinical behavior, including a subgroup of Basal-like patients with unexpectedly favorable outcomes linked to a distinct, more treatment-responsive gene signature, revealing molecular programs missed by traditional classification methods. Validation through training and testing on unseen patients confirmed that topology-derived risk groups remained significantly associated with survival after adjusting for age, tumor stage, and treatment, demonstrating that the geometric structure of gene expression data contains clinically meaningful prognostic information beyond traditional breast cancer classification methods.

</details>
