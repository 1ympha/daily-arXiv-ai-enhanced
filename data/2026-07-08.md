### [1] [The collective statistical mechanical personality of a group](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05597)
*Sarah Marzen*

Main category: q-bio.OT

TL;DR: 本文提出基于最大熵模型的群体人格数学框架，通过降维得到“集体人格”，并揭示集体智力的存在，可用于分析组织心理学中的性格特征。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有组织心理学缺乏对群体内在性格特征的数学分析工具，而非仅关注观点动力学或决策过程。

Method: 采用最大熵模型对群体人格进行建模，通过降维方法得到单一“集体人格”，并引入智力和情商分数以揭示集体智力，利用矩阵分析进行推导。

Result: 弱人际耦合可显著影响群体性格；可塑性人格主导集体人格；可数学求解最优自上而下指令以实现特定群体人格；群体可能产生人格障碍即使个体均为神经典型。

Conclusion: 该框架为组织心理学中内在性格的数学分析提供了起点，可推广至更复杂的最大熵模型。

Abstract: We propose a mathematical framework for organizational psychology based on a Maximum Entropy model of a group's personalities. The Maximum Entropy model is then decimated to a single ``collective personality''. If the original personality scores are augmented by intelligence and emotional quotients, then a collective intelligence is also mathematically revealed. With simple matrix analyses of the collective personality, one can understand: that weak interpersonal coupling can strongly affect group character; that malleable rather than stubborn personalities control the group's collective personality; that one can mathematically solve for optimal top-down directives to achieve certain group personalities; and that groups can have a personality disorder even if the individuals composing the groups are neurotypical. We hope that this framework provides a useful starting point for future mathematical analyses in organizational psychology related to innate character rather than opinion dynamics or decision making, and note that the analysis can be applied to much more complex Maximum Entropy models than the one proposed here if empirical evidence suggests that the Gaussian model proposed here is overly simplistic.

### [2] [Network amplification of dengue declines as endemicity rises: climate-adjusted directional spread across Costa Rican cantons, 1993-2012](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05740)
*Fabio Sanchez*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本研究利用哥斯达黎加81个县24年的登革热病例数据，通过去除季节和气候信号，重建了县际传播网络图。结果发现，调整气候因素后，登革热传播具有强烈的方向性，主要集中在低地加勒比和太平洋沿岸县份，局部疫情在传播网络中会被放大3-4倍，这种放大效应在1990年代疫情爆发初期最强，随后随年病例增加而显著下降，但传播方向保持稳定。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 气候模型能描述哪些地区有利于登革热传播，但无法揭示登革热如何在不同地点之间定向传播，以及这种传播方向如何影响疫情的下一个爆发地点。本研究旨在弥补这一空白。

Method: 利用1993-2012年哥斯达黎加81个县的每周病例数据（共246,524例），以约三周的登革热代际间隔重建县际传播地图；去除共有的季节和气候信号，仅保留方向特异性传播；通过接收县和来源县、放大因子和方向性指数进行总结，并在五年时间窗口内追踪。

Result: 气候调整后的传播具有强方向性，集中在低地加勒比和太平洋沿岸县（Limón, Matina, Guácimo, Garabito, Orotina）。局部疫情在网络中被放大3-4倍，尽管整体传播并未增长。放大效应在1990年代疫情爆发初期最大，随年病例增加而显著下降，但传播方向保持固定。

Conclusion: 常规监测数据即可绘制登革热的传播路径，为优先部署监测和病媒控制提供依据，尤其在新血清型或疾病首次建立时。作为多年窗口平均行为的历史描述，它属于规划性输入，其前瞻性价值尚待验证。

Abstract: \textbf{Background:} In Costa Rica, dengue is reported and controlled at the canton level, and outbreaks in one canton are often followed by outbreaks in others. Climate models describe where conditions favor transmission but not how dengue moves \emph{between} places, the directional, between-place spread that shapes where an outbreak travels next.
  \noindent \textbf{Methods:} From weekly case counts for all 81 cantons (1993--2012; \num{246524} cases) we reconstructed a canton-to-canton spread map using the roughly three-week dengue generation interval, removed the shared seasonal and climatic signal so that only direction-specific spread remained, and summarized it by the receiving and source cantons, an amplification factor, and a directionality index, tracked over five-year windows.
  \noindent \textbf{Results:} Climate-adjusted spread is strongly directional and concentrates in the lowland Caribbean and Pacific cantons (Limón, Matina, Guácimo, Garabito, Orotina). A local outbreak is amplified about three- to fourfold across the network even though overall transmission is not growing. This amplification was greatest during the emergence phase of the 1990s and declined markedly as annual reported cases increased, while the \emph{direction} of spread remained fixed; the decline persists after controlling for the broadening of surveillance coverage.
  \noindent \textbf{Conclusions:} Routine surveillance alone can map which cantons tend to experience dengue and the pathways through which it appears to spread, providing a potential input for prioritizing surveillance and vector control, particularly when a serotype or the disease itself is newly establishing. As a historical description of average behavior over multi-year windows, it is a planning input whose prospective value remains to be tested.

### [3] [Modeling the Impact of Immune Boosting on Population-Level Vaccine Effectiveness](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06235)
*Nir Gavish,Guy Katriel,Zohar Rom-Ramon*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 该研究将标准SIR模型扩展，纳入自然免疫增强机制，推导出封闭形式的最终规模关系，揭示了疫苗覆盖率与相对疫苗有效性之间的反直觉关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统SIR模型未考虑自然免疫增强在短期疫情爆发中的作用，导致对疫苗有效性的理解可能存在偏差。

Method: 扩展标准SIR模型，纳入自然免疫增强机制，推导封闭形式的最终规模关系，进行数学分析和渐近分析。

Result: 存在一个关键增强阈值：超过该阈值时，更高的疫苗覆盖率会矛盾地降低相对疫苗有效性；但对于高传播性变种，相对疫苗有效性收敛于一个与覆盖率完全无关的正极限。

Conclusion: 虽然高覆盖率可能降低相对有效性，但总体人群层面的影响始终是有益的，能持续减少绝对疾病负担。

Abstract: We extend the standard susceptible-infected-recovered framework to incorporate natural immune boosting during a short-scale outbreak. By deriving closed-form final size relations, we analytically link total attack rates to boosting dynamics and vaccine coverage. This framework identifies a critical boosting threshold: above it, higher vaccine coverage paradoxically decreases relative vaccine effectiveness. This occurs because successful epidemic suppression deprives vaccinated individuals of the silent pathogen exposures required to maintain their relative immunological advantage. Crucially, the overall population-level impact remains beneficial, consistently reducing absolute disease burden. For highly transmissible variants, asymptotic analysis reveals that relative vaccine effectiveness converges to a positive limit entirely independent of coverage.

### [4] [ssys: Exact algebraic recasting of ODE models into S-system or GMA form](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05607)
*William S. Hlavacek*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: ssys是一个Python包，能够将支持的ODE模型精确代数重构为S-系统或广义质量作用形式，支持Antimony和SBML格式输入，并通过符号提升、多种验证及工作流集成实现可复现的系统生物学建模。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 使经典的幂律重构方法在系统生物学建模中变得实用且可复现，解决ODE模型向S-系统或广义质量作用形式转换的自动化需求。

Method: 开发了ssys Python包，通过符号提升引入辅助变量，支持Antimony和SBML模型读取，并利用符号、数值和轨迹检查对转换后的系统进行验证。

Result: 提供了命令行工作流、笔记本生成功能，并在精选模型和BioModels示例上进行了基准测试验证。

Conclusion: ssys包使得经典的幂律重构方法在可复现的系统生物学建模中变得实用。

Abstract: ssys is a Python package for exact algebraic recasting of supported ODE models into S-system or Generalized Mass Action form. It reads Antimony and SBML models, introduces auxiliary variables through symbolic lifting, and validates transformed systems using symbolic, numerical, and trajectory-based checks. The package provides command-line workflows, notebook generation, and benchmark evidence across curated models and BioModels examples, making classical power-law recasting practical for reproducible systems biology modeling.

### [5] [Characterization of DLBCL cell of origin-phenotypes based on tumor microenvironment features](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06129)
*Stefano Ugliano,Martim Dias Gomes,Noémie Moreau,Marta Pistone,Marcel Kirchner,Alexandra Just,Adrian Georg Simon,Christian Kukat,Reinhardt Buettner,Katarzyna Bozek*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于深度学习的无偏管道，用于从弥漫性大B细胞淋巴瘤（DLBCL）的肿瘤微环境（TME）中提取特征，通过多路成像自动分析细胞组成、形态和空间组织，发现ABC亚型中免疫细胞比例增加且与M2型巨噬细胞存在优先相互作用，从而改进风险分层。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统的Hans分类器仅基于肿瘤相关标志物进行DLBCL亚型分类（GCB vs non-GCB/ABC），忽略了肿瘤微环境（TME）中免疫细胞、基质细胞、细胞因子等关键信息，导致风险分层不够精确。因此，需要一种整合TME信息的方法来改进患者分组和预后判断。

Method: 采用无偏的深度学习方法，构建自动化处理和分析多路成像图像的管道，从DLBCL患者队列的TME中提取特征，量化细胞组成、形态特征和空间组织信息。

Result: 发现ABC与GCB亚型之间存在多种细胞群比例的差异，包括ABC亚型中免疫细胞比例增加，以及ABC亚型与M2型巨噬细胞之间存在优先相互作用。

Conclusion: 该分析提供了对DLBCL亚型的深入表征，证明了该管道可用于对肿瘤及其亚型进行详细的定量分析，有望改进风险分层。

Abstract: Diffuse large B-cell lymphoma (DLBCL) is an aggressive form of non-Hodgkin lymphoma with a high recurrence rate. The molecular profiling of DLBCL tumors culminated in several immunohistochemistry algorithms for prognostic stratification. Among those, the Hans classifier is widely used for classifying DLBCL into germinal center B-cell-like (GCB) and non-germinal center/activated B-cell-like (non-GCB/ABC) subtypes. The Hans classifier primarily evaluates protein expression of tumor-associated markers, however the tumor microenvironment (TME) of DLBCL includes a myriad of immune and stromal cells, cytokines, and extracellular matrix components that contribute to tumor growth, immune evasion, and recurrence rate. Although the Hans classifier provides a practical method for subtype identification, incorporation of TME information may improve risk stratification and further refine patient groups. Here, we present an unbiased deep learning-based approach to extract meaningful features from TME of DLBCL tumors for the automated processing and analysis of multiplexed images of a DLBCL patient cohort. Our pipeline quantifies a range of features that describe tumor sample cell composition, morphology, and its spatial organization. We point to alterations in the proportions of several cell populations between GCB and ABC tumors including increased immune cell proportions of the ABC and its preferential interaction with the M2-macrophages. Our analysis offers an in-depth characterization of the DLBCL subtypes and is exemplary of how our pipeline can be used for detailed quantitative analysis of a tumor and its subtypes.

</details>
