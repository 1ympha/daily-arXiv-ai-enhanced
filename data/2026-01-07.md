### [1] [Instant cost and delayed reward. Demographic eco-evolutionary game dynamics under the impact of the delay resulting from the offspring maturation time](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.02552)
*Krzysztof Argasiński,Ryszard Rudnicki,Robert Szczelina*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 该研究将延迟微分方程引入人口生态演化博弈模型，分析幼体成熟时间延迟对种群动力学的影响，发现延迟会破坏平衡稳定性并导致复杂动力学行为


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统生态演化博弈模型基于抽象的"适应度"概念，缺乏对实际生物过程中时间延迟（如幼体成熟时间）的考虑，需要建立更贴近现实的模型来研究延迟对种群动力学的影响

Method: 扩展人口生态演化博弈方法，引入幼体成熟时间延迟，采用延迟微分方程建模，分析三种不同密度依赖抑制模式：成体死亡率、幼体成熟后存活率（无延迟）、幼体出生时存活率（有延迟）

Result: 延迟显著影响系统动力学，超过临界延迟会导致平衡稳定性丧失；第三种模式（幼体出生时存活率）产生极端模式如分岔、复杂周期和混沌动力学，但意外地延长了"幽灵吸引子"的暂态持续时间

Conclusion: 时间延迟是生态演化动力学中的重要因素，密度依赖机制的选择对系统行为有决定性影响，幼体出生时存活率模式虽然导致复杂动力学，但能延长暂态稳定性，对系统抗干扰能力有重要意义

Abstract: In this paper, we extend the demographic eco-evolutionary game approach, based on explicit birth and death dynamics instead of abstract "fitness" interpreted as an abstract "Malthusian parameter", by the introduction of the delay resulting from the juvenile maturation time. This leads to the application of the Delay Differential Equations (DDE). We show that delay seriously affects the resulting dynamics and may lead to the loss of stability of equilibria when critical delay is exceeded. We provide theoretical tools for the assessment of the critical delays and the parameter values when this may happen. Our results emphasize the importance of the mechanisms of density dependence. We analyze the impact of three different suppression modes based on: adult mortality, juvenile recruitment survival after the maturation period (without delay), and juvenile recruitment at birth (with the delay). The last mode leads to extreme patterns such as bifurcations, complex cycles, and chaotic dynamics. However, surprisingly, this mode leads to extension of the duration of the temporary transient metastable states known as "ghost attractors". In addition, we also focus on the problem of resilience of the analyzed systems against external periodic perturbations and feedback-driven factors such as additional predator pressure.

### [2] [Apparent Selection Pressure for Channel Capacity in Bacterial Chemotactic Sensors](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.02446)
*Ziyi Cui,Sarah Marzen*

Main category: q-bio.QM

TL;DR: 该研究通过异质MWC模型分析大肠杆菌混合Tar/Tsr趋化受体簇的静态传感极限，发现通道容量在参数空间中呈现显著局部最大值，而有效希尔系数和动态范围未显示类似优化，表明个体细胞水平上通道容量可能是自然选择的目标。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究大肠杆菌趋化传感系统如何将噪声化学信号转换为奔跑和翻滚行为，特别关注混合Tar/Tsr受体簇在个体细胞水平上的静态传感性能极限。

Method: 采用异质Monod-Wyman-Changeux（MWC）模型，在七维参数空间中进行扫描，计算三个传感性能指标：通道容量、有效希尔系数和动态范围。

Result: 在大肠杆菌样参数范围内，通道容量始终呈现显著的局部最大值，而有效希尔系数和动态范围未显示类似优化。实现最大通道容量的输入分布是双峰的，表明个体细胞通过采样低浓度和高浓度区域来最大化信息获取。

Conclusion: 这些结果表明，在个体细胞水平上，大肠杆菌受体簇可能通过自然选择优化了通道容量，而非其他传感性能指标，这有助于理解细菌趋化传感系统的进化适应机制。

Abstract: Bacterial chemotactic sensing converts noisy chemical signals into running and tumbling. We analyze the static sensing limits of mixed Tar/Tsr chemoreceptor clusters in individual Escherichia coli cells using a heterogeneous Monod-Wyman-Changeux (MWC) model. By sweeping a seven-dimensional parameter space, we compute three sensing performance metrics-channel capacity, effective Hill coefficient, and dynamic range. Across E. coli-like parameter regimes, we consistently observe pronounced local maxima of channel capacity, whereas neither the effective Hill coefficient nor the dynamic range exhibit comparable optimization. The capacity-achieving input distribution is bimodal, which implies that individual cells maximize information by sampling both low- and high concentration regimes. Together, these results suggest that, at the individual-cell level, channel capacity may be selected for in E. coli receptor clusters.
