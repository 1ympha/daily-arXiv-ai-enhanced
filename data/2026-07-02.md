### [1] [Demographic senescence as multi-level selection in miniature](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.00262)
*Ananda Shikhara Bhat,Hanna Kokko*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文通过数学框架证明，生物衰老产生的死亡模式可被理解为多层级选择（Multi-level Selection）的产物，揭示了衰老与多层级选择之间的形式等价性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 多层级选择与衰老表面上看毫无关联，作者试图揭示二者之间的深层数学联系，为理解衰老机制提供新视角。

Method: 构建了两层Moran过程模型，利用其缩放极限（scaling limits）分析群体结构种群中的多层级选择与损伤累积模式及死亡率曲线的关系。

Result: 衰老个体中损伤累积与死亡模式，等价于代内多层级选择过程：个体内子系统相当于粒子，有机体相当于集体，选择性消失相当于群体选择。

Conclusion: 衰老的涌现性死亡模式可视为多层级选择的结果，这一形式等价性为衰老研究提供了统一的理论框架。

Abstract: Multi-level selection and senescence do not at first sight have much in common. Here, we demonstrate that the emergent mortality patterns generated by demographic senescence can be understood as the product of multi-level selection. We formulate a two-level Moran type process and use its scaling limits to illustrate that a simple mathematical framework that models multi-level selection in group-structured populations also models damage accumulation patterns and resultant mortality curves in ageing organisms. To verbally make the connection, observe that defectors spread within a group consisting of cooperators and defectors; when groups compete against each other, defector-rich groups suffer, and between-group selection causes such groups to be systematically under-represented. Exactly analogously, senescing individuals accumulate damage to physiological sub-systems, and `damage begets damage'; individuals who are more damaged are more likely to die, hence damage-rich individuals are systematically under-represented in later age classes. Thus, emergent senescence patterns in complex, integrated organisms are formally equivalent to the patterns generated by a within-generation multi-level selection process in which intra-organismal sub-systems play the role of particles, organisms play the role of collectives, and selective disappearance plays the role of group selection.

### [2] [How Environment and Urbanization Shape Bird Diversity in Sri Lanka](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.00582)
*Dilusha Chandrasiri,Maneesha Herath,Yasith Hewarathna,Muditha Herath,Gishan Bandara,Madara Mendis,Nathali Athukorala,Nisansa de Silva,Sandareka Wickramanayake*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本研究整合空间、时间和环境数据，系统分析了斯里兰卡鸟类多样性，发现土地覆盖类型是鸟类多样性的最强预测因子，城市化对鸟类多样性具有尺度依赖的复杂影响。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有研究缺乏对斯里兰卡鸟类多样性与多尺度环境因子之间关系的系统理解，亟需通过整合时空与环境数据来揭示鸟类多样性的驱动机制，为生物多样性保护提供科学依据。

Method: 整合鸟类观测记录与环境变量（天气、空气污染、NDVI、土地覆盖、海拔、夜间人工光照），在2km、5km、10km多网格尺度进行空间分析，采用空间稀疏化减少采样偏差；使用稀化丰富度和占据率等校正指标评估时间趋势；通过泊松广义线性模型和相关分析识别环境驱动因子；分析群落结构、优势度和β多样性。

Result: 土地覆盖类型相比NDVI或温度等连续变量是更强的鸟类多样性预测因子；夜间人工光照（ALAN）表征的城市化具有尺度依赖效应，支持少数广适种高丰度但降低整体丰富度。

Conclusion: 研究为斯里兰卡鸟类多样性的格局与驱动因素提供了可操作的见解，建立了一个可扩展、可复现的生物多样性研究与保护规划框架。

Abstract: This study presents a comprehensive analysis of bird diversity across Sri Lanka by integrating spatial, temporal, and environmental data. Bird observation records were combined with environmental variables, including weather conditions, air pollution, the Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI), land cover, elevation, and Artificial Light At Night (ALAN), and rigorously preprocessed to ensure data quality. Spatial analyses were conducted on multiple grid scales (2 km, 5 km, 10 km) to evaluate patterns in species richness while minimizing sampling bias through spatial thinning. Temporal trends were assessed using effort-corrected metrics including rarefied richness and occupancy rates to account for variations in observation effort over time. Environmental drivers of bird diversity were examined using multivariate statistical models, including Poisson Generalized Linear Models (GLMs) and correlation analyses, to identify key associations between ecological factors and species richness. Additionally, community structure, dominance patterns, and beta diversity were analyzed to understand variations in species composition across regions and time. The study found that land-cover type is a stronger predictor of bird diversity than individual continuous variables such as NDVI or temperature alone. Urbanization, measured by ALAN, exhibits nuanced scale-dependent effects, supporting high abundances of a few generalist species while reducing overall richness. The findings provide actionable insights into the patterns and drivers of avian diversity in Sri Lanka, offering a scalable and reproducible framework for biodiversity research and conservation planning.

Method: 基于异质性SI模型[1]的宏观动力学约束，构建以最终感染规模最小化为目标的最优控制问题；引入药物供应约束（积分等式约束）；应用庞特里亚金最小值原理推导最优控制的刻画条件。

Conclusion: 该研究为考虑种群异质性的流行病最优控制提供了理论框架，从微观异质性视角出发的策略刻画有助于更精准地制定有限资源下的疫情防控方案。

Abstract: This work addresses an optimal control problem for a SI epidemic model incorporating heterogeneities in resistance and viral load at the population level. Building upon the heterogeneous SI framework developed in [1], a minimization problem constrained to the macroscopic counterpart of the SI dynamics derived therein is proposed. Unlike traditional optimal control problems in homogeneous epidemic models, the present approach focuses on an optimal control problem that accounts for population heterogeneity, offering insights from a microscale perspective. The contribution aims to minimize the final size of the infection within a finite time horizon by developing a pharmaceutical strategy, under a supply constraint that translates into an integral equality constraint in the control function. By applying the Pontryagin Minimum Principle, a characterization of an optimal control is provided.

### [4] [Effective population sizes for asymmetrically regulated birth-death processes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.00628)
*Yunbei Pan,Tom Chou*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文研究了多物种生灭过程中，种群调节机制（抑制复制、提高死亡率或两者兼有）如何影响宏观随机动力学。通过引入可调参数α_i，发现调节机制在确定性动力学中不可见，但在扩散演化中起关键作用。对于两物种情况，推导了固定时间和概率，表明不同调节机制会在随机意义上偏好出生调节更强的物种。还定义了与α相关的有效种群大小N_e(α)，并利用微扰理论计算了近中性或异质种群的光谱隙，将其解释为多样性丧失时间尺度。结果特别适用于T细胞克隆的相互作用亚群。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 多物种生灭过程中，种群调节机制（抑制复制与提高死亡率）对宏观随机动力学的影响尚未被详细分析，作者希望揭示这些机制在随机演化中的关键作用。

Method: 引入可调调节分配参数α_i，将d物种生灭过程投影到(d-1)维Moran过程上，推导出依赖于调节机制的扩散张量；对两物种情况精确求解固定时间和概率；利用微扰理论计算光谱隙。

Result: 调节机制在确定性动力学中不可见但在扩散动力学中显著；不同调节机制导致固定时间和概率差异，即使在完全中性条件下也会随机偏好出生调节更强的物种；定义了与α相关的有效种群大小N_e(α)；光谱隙可解释为多样性丧失时间尺度。

Conclusion: 揭示了种群调节机制对随机动力学的关键影响，为理解T细胞克隆多样性丧失等生物过程提供了理论框架。

Abstract: In multispecies birth-death processes, how population regulation -- through suppressed replication, elevated mortality, or both -- affects macroscopic stochastic dynamics has escaped detailed analysis. Here, we show that the distribution of regulation mechanisms can be invisible in deterministic or mean-field dynamics but play a significant role in the diffusive evolution of population frequencies. By introducing a tunable regulation partitioning parameter $α_i$ and projecting a $d$-species birth-death process onto a $(d{-}1)$-dimensional Moran process, we find a regulation-mechanism-dependent diffusion tensor. For the simple two-species case, we derive exact fixation times and probabilities to show how different regulation mechanisms stochastically favors a more birth-regulated species, even under complete deterministic neutrality. Our model also allows us to define an $α$-dependent effective population size $N_{\rm e}(α)$ among neutral species, generalizing its classical interpretation. For near-neutral populations or populations that are heterogeneous in their regulation mechanism, we used perturbation theory to calculate the spectral gap, identifying it with a diversity loss timescale which can also be interpreted as setting an effective population size. Our results are particularly applicable to interacting subpopulations of T cells ("clones") which are near-neutral, are regulated through proliferation and apoptosis, and lose diversity with time.

### [5] [Immune history shapes recurrent epidemics of antigenically related variants](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.00905)
*Ryuichi Kumata,Yuma Fujimoto,Hisashi Ohtsuki,Akira Sasaki*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文建立了一个针对抗原变异病原体的递归SIR模型，发现稳定、规模相等的周期性疫情广泛存在，但在强传播和有限抗原逃逸下会失稳，且疫情规模在中等基本再生数时最大。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 过去疫情带来的群体免疫力会影响新变异株的传播，但抗原相似性如何具体影响疫情动态尚不清晰，需要理论模型来阐明这一机制。

Method: 构建了一个递归SIR模型，假设群体依次面对抗原相关变异株，基于状态依赖性群体免疫力推导出群体易感性的递归映射关系。

Result: 模型揭示了稳定等规模周期性疫情的存在及其失稳条件（强传播+有限抗原逃逸可导致周期-2或更复杂动力学），并发现疫情规模在中等基本再生数时达到最大值。

Abstract: Population immunity carried over from past epidemics of an antigenically variable pathogen influences the epidemic of new variants based on their antigenic similarity to the previous ones. We develop a recurrent SIR model where a population faces sequential, antigenically related variants. The model yields a recurrence map for the population susceptibility to successive variants under the assumption of status-based population immunity. The model reveals that stable, equal-sized recurrent epidemics occur across broad parameter ranges, but can be destabilized when transmission is strong and antigenic escape is limited, leading to period-2 or more, or even more complex epidemic dynamics. Epidemic size is maximized at an intermediate basic reproduction number: higher transmissibility boosts immediate infection but also enhances cross-immunity, reducing future susceptibility of the population. Our results clarify how immune history shapes recurrent epidemics and why success in one wave does not ensure larger future epidemics.

### [6] [Approximating Peak Prevalence in Multistage SIR Epidemics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.01014)
*Denis Tverskoi,Andrew Gothard,Grzegorz A. Rempala*

Main category: q-bio.PE

TL;DR: 本文研究多阶段SIR流行病模型中加权感染阶段峰值与真实患病率峰值之间的关系，发现两者在特定缩放条件下渐近等价，并解释了“二倍近似”的适用条件与失效原因，提出了精度改进方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在多阶段SIR模型中，患病率峰值难以直接求解，但常用加权感染阶段聚合作为替代指标。作者旨在厘清加权峰值与真实患病率峰值之间的关系，并评估“二倍近似”的可靠性。

Method: 采用确定性SI(k)R模型，分析不同阶段进展速率缩放方式（朴素缩放与Erlang缩放）下加权峰值与患病率峰值的关系；通过极限表示推导理论误差界，并开发基于曲率和参数的修正方法。

Result: 朴素缩放下加权峰值与患病率峰值渐近等价但二倍近似失效；Erlang缩放下模型收敛到延迟形式，为二倍近似提供理论基础，并揭示了其在高尖峰流行病波中精度下降的原因。所提出的修正方法显著提高了估计精度。

Conclusion: 研究明确了加权阶段峰值作为患病率峰值代理变量的适用条件，并提供了在标准近似失效时的精化估计方法，对医疗资源需求预测具有重要实践意义。

Abstract: Estimating peak prevalence is a central problem in epidemic modeling because it determines the period of greatest infectious burden and is closely linked to health-care demand. In multistage SIR models, however, peak prevalence is generally less tractable than in the classical model with exponentially distributed infectious periods. Motivated by the use of weighted infectious-stage aggregates as surrogates for prevalence, we investigate the relationship between the prevalence peak and the maximum of a weighted stage functional in deterministic SI$(k)$R epidemic models. We show that this relationship depends critically on how the stage-progression rate is scaled as the number of infectious stages increases. Under naive scaling, in which the progression rate remains fixed, the weighted peak is asymptotically equivalent to the prevalence peak and the commonly used factor-two approximation fails. Under Erlang scaling, which preserves the mean infectious period, the multistage model converges to a delay formulation in which prevalence and the weighted stage functional become unweighted and triangularly weighted moving averages of incidence.
  This limiting representation provides a theoretical basis for the factor-two approximation and identifies the regimes in which it is accurate. It also explains why this approximation deteriorates as epidemic waves become more sharply peaked. We derive analytical error bounds and develop curvature-based and parameter-based corrections that substantially improve accuracy. Numerical studies confirm these improvements across a broad range of epidemiological parameters. Overall, the results show when weighted-stage peaks can be used reliably as proxies for peak prevalence and how the resulting estimates can be refined when the standard approximation loses accuracy.
